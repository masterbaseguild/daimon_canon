# Cosmic Sphere

A **Cosmic Sphere** is a concept used to separate regions of the universe that do not have any kind of contact, interaction or communication with each other.

---

# Definition and Properties

The fundamental principle that regulates the existence of the Cosmic Spheres lies in the absolute mutual isolation:

* **Total Isolation:** As long as two macro-regions of the universe have no way to exchange information, send signals or travel towards each other, they belong to two distinct Cosmic Spheres.
* **Phasing and Merging:** The Cosmic Spheres are not physical barriers or immutable boundaries. At the exact moment in which any type of contact is established - be it a radio pulse, the opening of a portal or the chance meeting between two entities belonging to different Spheres - the conceptual separation instantly decays and the Spheres merge into a single interconnected reality.
