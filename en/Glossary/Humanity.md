# MasterVerse Sociology and Ethics

The MasterVerse universe is characterized by a profound redefinition of the concepts of humanity, maturity and morality. Following the **Extension Charter**, law and philosophy have ceased to be anchored to a purely aural perspective, adopting a universal and inclusive vision that has undermined the certainties of the past.

---

# I. Identity Redefinition

The **Extension Charter**, ratified in the aftermath of the first contact with alien races, sanctioned a universal paradigm shift:

* **The Concept of "Human":** It is no longer a biological category linked to the species of Aura, but an ontological definition based on **consciousness**. Including Artificial General Intelligences and scanned minds under the label “human” has made race biologically irrelevant.
* **The Concept of "World":** The extension of the term from "home planet" to "the entire known universe" has formalized the overcoming of planetary provincialism, laying the foundations for a cosmic administration of resources.

---

# II. The New Morality

Morality in the MasterVerse is a fluid landscape, constantly challenged by technological progress and the study of [[en/Magic/Aether|Ether]].

* **Desensitization:** Violence is an integrated component of daily life, perceived in a less traumatic way than in the real world. The concept of "loss" has lost its absolute value, becoming a statistical or transitory element.
* **Cruel and Ruthless:** This is a moral system based on strength and adaptability. The characters find themselves having to choose whether to embrace this pragmatic nihilism or oppose it in the name of pre-extension ideals.
* **Maturity and Competence:** Thanks to advanced AI-driven assessment techniques, maturity has been separated from biological age. An individual is considered an adult only when he passes a very high precision **Self-Sufficiency Test**, a requirement that has fueled the growth of [[en/Glossary/Gilde|guilds]].

---

# III. Politics and Power Structures

The universe is a patchwork of factions in constant tension, governed by webs of shifting alliances and age-old enmities. The management of power is expressed through different organizational forms:

| Type of Organization | Main Focus |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Alliances** | Mutual defense and trans-systemic diplomacy. |
| **Nations** | Traditional territorial administration. |
| **Corporations** | Exploitation of resources and markets (e.g. Trade Houses). |
| **Religions** | Cults based on the veneration of [[en/Magic/Aether|Arcana]] or higher entities. |
| **Guilds** | Paramilitary models that are supplanting state structures. |

---

# IV. Dangers of the Cosmos

Survival in such a vast universe is constantly undermined by threats of a dual nature:

* **Planetary Threats:** Catastrophic natural disasters, extreme climates, and hostile local wildlife that evolve in response to magical or chemical pollution.
* **Space Threats:** Predatory space wildlife, [[en/Glossary/Aether-Portal|Aether Portal]] instability, and gravitational cataclysms that can wipe out entire trade routes.

# V. Biological Diversity
The MasterVerse family tree is vast. In addition to advanced alien species, the universe teems with sentient floral lifeforms and hybrid faunas resulting from millennia of natural evolution and arcane manipulation. The coexistence between these races is the very fabric on which the chronicles of the system are woven.
