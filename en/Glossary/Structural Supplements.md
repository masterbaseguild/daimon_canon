# Structural Supplements

**Structural Supplements** constitute a specialized class of pharmacological agents and bio-enhancers designed to raise the human body's physical tolerance threshold. Their main purpose is to prevent collapse or permanent damage to tissues, bones and vital organs in situations characterized by **high accelerations** (extreme G-forces) or high intensity impacts.

---

# I. Function and Mechanism of Action

Unlike cybernetic enhancements like the [[en/Technology/MasterGear|MasterGear]], structural supplements work at the molecular and cellular level:

* **Reinforcement of the Bone Matrix:** They increase the mineral density and elastic resilience of the skeleton, drastically reducing the risk of load fractures during sudden maneuvers or rapid acceleration.
* **Soft Tissue Stabilization:** They act on the bonds of the connective tissue to avoid muscle tears, tendon detachments or damage to internal organs caused by the compression of body fluids under strong acceleration.
* **Circulation Optimization:** They include agents that regulate blood viscosity, preventing blood outflow from the brain (G-LOC, *G-force induced Loss Of Consciousness*) and maintaining tissue oxygenation even under extreme load.

---

# II. Tactical Applications

The use of these medicines is a consolidated practice among operators who move in the Void:

* **Interceptor and Shuttle Pilots:** To support the thrusts of the warp engines during tactical maneuvering, where the forces exerted greatly exceed standard biological capabilities.
* **Questers in Combat Operations:** Used in combination with the [[en/Magic/School-of-Kinetic-Magic|Kinetic magic]] to avoid suffering injury from the backlash of one's magical actions (for example, when a wizard "shifts" his weight with superhuman strength).
* **Members of the Aegift Division:** Deployed to enable sustained mobility in variable or perturbed gravity environments on Iphiridia.

---

# III. Risks and Contraindications

Despite their effectiveness, structural supplements are not without side effects:

* **Metabolic Overload:** The forced alteration of physiology requires a higher energy intake. Without adequate nutrition or monitoring via MasterGear, the user risks premature depletion of organic reserves.
* **Protocol Dependence:** Prolonged use can induce a weakening of the natural regenerative capacity, creating a chronic chemical dependence to maintain the achieved performance.
* **Accumulation Toxicity:** The metabolization of chemical enhancers can, in the long term, strain filtering organs such as the liver and kidneys, making detox cycles necessary between one mission and another.

# IV. Status and Integration
In the current panorama, structural supplements are often administered in the form of pre-use cocktails via the injection ports of [[en/Glossary/Argus|Argus]] bracelets or belts (where still in use) or via controlled release protocols via MasterGear. Scientific research in the MasterVerse continues to search for cleaner compounds, seeking to emulate the biological resilience of the hardier alien races discovered in the Void.
