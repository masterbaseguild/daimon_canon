# Kinetic Magic

**Kinetic Magic** is one of the core classes of spells in the MasterVerse. Its key principle is the conversion of magical energy, drawn from the [[en/Magic/Aether|Ether]], into pure kinetic energy in the real world, allowing the user to move, accelerate or stop bodies and masses of any nature.

---

# I. Domination Subclasses

The operational difficulty of this magic is directly proportional to the molecular resistance of the state of matter on which it is acted:

* **Gas Domination (Low Difficulty):** It is the most accessible and widespread form. Since the gaseous state is characterized by weak intermolecular forces and high freedom of movement, it requires the least expenditure of mana to create currents, air voids or atmospheric compressions.
* **Liquid Mastery (Intermediate Difficulty):** Requires greater mastery to overcome the cohesion and surface tension typical of liquid fluids. It is a fundamental technique for handling water resources, solvents or industrial fluids.
* **Domination of Solids (High Difficulty):** The most difficult discipline. It requires a huge energy effort to overcome the crystalline structure or the compactness of solids. A master of this subclass can lift rubble, manipulate architectural structures, or deflect solid projectiles.

---

# II. The Academic Debate on Thermal Magic

There is a controversial line of thought in the theoretical field which proposes to downgrade **Thermal Magic** to a simple subclass of Kinetics.

* **The Thesis:** Since heat is, at the atomic level, the agitation of particles, thermal magic would be nothing more than kinetic magic applied on a molecular scale (micro-kinetics).
* **Academic Status:** Despite the logical coherence of the theory, it remains a niche position. Most academics prefer to keep the two classes separate, citing the complexity of the variables involved in thermal manipulation compared to the macroscopic vector handling of classical kinetics.

---

# III. Tactical and Strategic Applications

Kinetic magic isn't just for moving objects; is the mainstay of tactical combat in the MasterVerse:

1. **Vectorization:** The caster can generate vectors of force to block incoming attacks, create kinetic barriers, or project blunt objects with pinpoint accuracy.
2. **Synergy with other Classes:** Kinetic magic is essential for [[en/Magic/School-of-Fire|Firebending]] (to place fuel) and for [[en/Magic/Cinetoplasmic-Magic|Cinetoplasmic Magic]] (to convert the physical impact in magical reserve).
3. **Movement:** Is the basis for the [[en/Magic/School-of-Flight|Flight School]]'s take-off spells, providing the push needed to overcome gravitational pull.

# IV. Operational Limits

Manipulation is subject to the law of conservation (however flexible in the magical context): the denser the matter or the greater the weight, the greater the psychic effort and consumption of mana required. Prolonged use of solid domination, in particular, can lead to rapid psychic exhaustion, requiring careful energy planning.
