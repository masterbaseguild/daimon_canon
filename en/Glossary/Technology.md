# MasterVerse Infrastructure and Society

The MasterVerse is governed by three interdependent infrastructure networks — **Travel, Resources, and Information** — that support a society profoundly transformed by **Transhumanism** and the conquest of the space frontier.

---

# I. Transport and Travel Network

The journey is structured in a hierarchy of levels, from the planetary surface to deep space:

* **Tier 1: Intraplanetary:** The surface network based on roads, railways, shipping and air routes. It is the last mile network, managed through toll booths, stations and ports.
* **Level 2: Intrasystemic:** The orbital network. It connects planetary orbits to spaceports, ensuring logistics between nodes of a single star system.
* **Tier 3: Interstellar/Warp:** The heart of the MasterVerse. The **FTL (Faster Than Light)** journey is made possible by the **Warp Engine** (patented in 2010, in the public domain since 2012). These ships travel through predefined **Warp Routes** between **Warp Gates**, allowing transits at unimaginable speeds. *Note: Intergalactic travel remains a hard limit for standard warp drives.*

---

# II. Resource Network

The distribution of raw materials is as vital as the movement of people:

* **Traditional Carriers:** Merchant and cargo ships plying transportation routes.
* **Dedicated Infrastructures:** Planetary pipes for fluids (water, hydrocarbons) and energy transmission routes between planets in the form of **light rays (laser-power transmission)**.
* **Continuous Flows:** Some *Warp Gates* are configured as interstellar pumping stations, capable of transferring uninterrupted flows of essential resources between distant systems.

---

# III. Information Network

Parallel to the physical network, there is a galactic internet based on a speed hierarchy:

* **Standard Transmission:** Leverages quantum or satellite communications infrastructure depending on proximity.
* **Warp Transmission:** For long-distance data, the network uses a variant of Warp technology. “Information buffers” are sent at speeds reaching quadrillions of kilometers per hour, making interstellar communication nearly instantaneous.

---

# IV. Network nodes

The social structure is organized around the Nodes: **Planets, Colonies (Residential or Economic) and Space Stations**. While planets and colonies follow fixed, predictable orbits, **Space Stations** (and Guild Starbases) possess independent mobility, requiring dynamic connection protocols and flexible routes to remain synchronized with the rest of the network.

---

# V. Transhumanism: The Evolution of the Species

The definition of "human" has been rewritten. Enhancement technologies (Bionics, AI, Advanced Medicine) have generated a variety of existential states:

| Type | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Cyborg** | Humans with bionic elements by necessity (disability) or choice. |
| **Supercyborg** | Enhanced humans with additional artificial or bionic organs. |
| **Superhumans** | Humans with biological capabilities expanded through organic engineering. |
| **Vampires** | Individuals biologically treated for the elimination of aging. |
| **AI Zombies** | Artificial Intelligences residing in human biological bodies. |
| **Robot Guests** | Original human minds uploaded into robotic shells. |
| **Virtual Guests** | Human minds uploaded into server environments, without physical bodies. |
| **Enhanced Minds** | Humans assisted by a neural advisor AI. |
| **Androids** | Complex bionic constructs driven by artificial intelligence. |

---

#VI. Virtual and Deep Reality

In addition to physical enhancement, humanity has expanded into simulation:
1. **Augmented Reality (AR):** Overlay of data on physical reality.
2. **Virtual Reality (VR):** Worlds simulated via BCI (Brain-Computer Interface) interfaces.
3. **Deep Reality:** States of total immersion in which the boundary between real and simulated sensory input completely vanishes.

---

#VII. Materials Science

Military and civil engineering depends on a wide range of advanced materials. **Carbon, Kevlar and Titanium** alloys dominate the construction of space structures and vehicle bodies, but research continues towards "intelligent" materials capable of thermal self-regulation, active shielding from stellar radiation and kinetic absorption, essential for the survival of *Questers* in the hostile environments of the Void.
