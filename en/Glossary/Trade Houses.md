# TradeHouse

A **Trade House** is a commercial organization originating from [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Gondraw System/Gondraw System|Gondraw system]], characterized by a corporate structure that integrates operational methods typical of organized crime. Born as private mining contractors, Trade Houses quickly evolved into supranational entities that manage resource trade with complete disregard for local jurisdictions.

---

# I. Genesis: From Monarchy to the Black Market

The birth of the Trade Houses is the result of a systemic political failure in the Gondraw System:

* **Resource Depletion:** When Gondoraw's planetary mineral reserves ran out, the ruling monarchy, unable to manage space exploration on its own, delegated the extraction of new resources in the asteroid belt to private individuals.
* **Private Autonomy:** Granted the freedom to operate in deep space, these private consortia quickly developed their own logistics and firepower.
* **The Betrayal of Power:** Once control of the extraction routes was obtained, the consortia stopped recognizing monarchical authority, transforming themselves into Trade Houses: de facto powers that ignore Gondran laws to maximize profit.

---

# II. Modus Operandi and Structure

Although they present themselves as commercial companies, Trade Houses operate like cartels:

* **Extortion Economy:** Control of asteroid trade routes allows Trade Houses to impose "protection taxes" on anyone transiting their zones of influence.
* **Methods of Organized Crime:** Dispute resolution through sabotage, kidnapping of specialists, hijackings and — when necessary — the physical elimination of competitors.
* **Integration into the Guilds:** Trade Houses often finance or absorb entire [[en/Glossary/Gilde|guilds]] to use them as an armed wing or specialized workforce for high-risk recovery tasks.

---

# III. Geopolitical impact

The Trade Houses have completely redesigned the economy of the Gondraw System and surrounding areas in the Alpha Sphere:

1. **Energy Monopoly:** Having control of the raw resources of the asteroid belt, they dictate the price of fuel and heavy metals for entire trade routes.
2. **Corporation-States:** In many cases, the Trade House directly administers the housing developments of its employees, creating a system of subjection in which the worker is also the consumer of the goods produced by the House itself.
3. **Conflict with Independent Guilds:** There is perennial tension between Trade Houses and independent [[en/Glossary/Quester|Questors]] Guilds, as the latter often act as "unauthorized couriers" that undermine the Houses' control of trade flows.

# IV. Operational Status
Today, Trade Houses are among the main economic players in the MasterVerse. Sebbene siano tecnicamente fuorilegge in molti settori che non siano Gondraw, la loro capacità di fornire risorse rare le rende partner inevitabili persino per le fazioni che ufficialmente le condannano. Their influence is tangible proof of how, in the 21st century universe, capital is the most respected law.
