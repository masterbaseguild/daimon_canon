# Questor

The **Quester** is the archetype of the contemporary adventurer, a figure born between the end of the 20th century and the dawn of the 21st century. It is not a simple profession, but a real lifestyle that responds to the need to explore, control and dominate the new frontiers opened by technological progress and the discovery of the secrets of the [[en/Magic/Aether|Ether]].

---

# I. Origins and Rise

The term "quester" emerged with the technological revolution of the twenty-first century, in a context in which traditional states proved unable (or unwilling) to manage the rapidity of extra-systemic expansion.
* **The Answer to the Void:** While governments remained anchored to slow and backward bureaucratic logic, the Questar became the main actor of the space "Wild West".
* **Autonomy:** Often included in [[en/Glossary/Gilde|guilds]] paramilitaries, the Questars are professionals who sell their expertise - from defense to research, from recovery to exploration - operating where civil authority does not yet have jurisdiction.

---

# II. Culture and Identity: The Battle Name

A distinctive and fundamental element of the Questar culture is the radical detachment from one's civil identity and biological/family past.
* **The Epithet:** It is a universal custom, at the beginning of the Questor journey, to abandon one's baptismal name.
* **Symbolism:** The choice of a *battle name* is not a simple affectation, but a rite of passage. The chosen epithet represents the identity that the Questor intends to forge in the future, a label that will define its reputation, history and legacy in the Void.
* **Operational Anonymity:** This detachment protects the Questor from repercussions on his private and pre-existing life, allowing total immersion in the mission and in the cruel reality of the border.

---

# III. Requirements and Training

With the abolition of the concept of age of majority, the path to becoming a Questar has become meritocratic and rigorous:
* **Self-sufficiency test:** Access to the Questar title is subject to passing very high precision exams managed by artificial intelligence, which evaluate survival skills, tactical skills and attitude to risk.
* **Technical Evolution:** Many Questars integrate their natural abilities with enhancement technologies such as the [[en/Technology/MasterGear|MasterGear]], making their body a work tool optimized for extreme conditions.

---

# IV. Ethics and Life as a Questor

The life of a Questar is intrinsically linked to the "ruthless" morality of the MasterVerse. The concept of loss is attenuated, violence is desensitized and one's existence is constantly balanced between economic gain and the risk of permanent death.

---

# V. Social Role
Today, Questors are the true arbiters of destiny in the MasterVerse. Whether acting as lone mercenaries, members of an elite guild, or scouts for corporations, they define the borders of the [[en/Glossary/Sfera-Cosmica|Cosmic Spheres]], determining with their actions which territories will be colonized and which will be abandoned to oblivion.
