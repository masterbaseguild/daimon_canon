# Ether

The **Ether** is the supernatural dimension that serves as the energetic and spiritual substrate of the universe. It is not just a "place", but the domain where all the energies classified as magical reside and where the entities that govern the metaphysical plane manifest themselves. The Aether serves as a mirror and source: every intelligent being (human or otherwise) possesses a soul that serves as a bridge between physical reality and this realm.

---

# I. Components and Energies of the Aether

The functioning of the Aether is regulated by an almost scientific nature, although its laws are radically different from those of the physical world:

* **Souls:** The projected image of an intelligent being into the Aether. They contain consciousness, memory and profound identity. Physical death marks the transition of the soul into the Ether.
* **Mana:** The raw magical energy inherent in every soul and Ethereal. It is the "fuel" that allows the execution of spells. Mana is gradually regenerated by the soul or Ethereal itself.
* **Tombstones:** A form of ethereal energy dispersed throughout the realm in heterogeneous concentrations. Not usable individually to cast spells, they act as a catalyst/enhancer when integrated with mana.
* **Ethereal:** Supernatural entities (spirits, demons, deities). Many Ethereals derive direct power from human veneration; the most powerful ones are historically venerated as deities by various cultures.
* **Essence:** Pure magical energy present in the Aether in varying quantities, used to amplify the intensity and reach of complex spells.
* **Ethereal Bodies:** Physical forms made entirely of mana, created by converting a physical body or drawing energy from a soul or daemon.

---

# II. The Magic Mechanism: The Bridge Between the Worlds

Magic in the MasterVerse is the process of energy conversion between the real world and the Aether. The human soul acts as a transformer:

1. **Channeling:** The caster channels mana (and possibly tombstones) through his soul.
2. **Conversion:** Aether energy is converted into real energy (kinetic, thermal, etc.).
3. **Regeneration:** After use, mana regenerates naturally. A fraction of the mana consumed is converted into gravestones, returning to the ethereal cycle.

> **Law of the Vector:** Each movement spell reduces or increases the temperature of a body by acting on the vectors that move atomic particles. Slowing down or speeding up molecules actually means manipulating heat.

---

# III. Techniques and Specializations

* **Kinetoplasmic Magic:** An advanced reverse conversion technique. It allows you to convert the kinetic energy released in a physical blow into instant magical energy, enhancing the attack with shock waves or energy fields.
* **Enchanted Containers (Gems):** Any object can be enchanted by binding it to a "container" in the Aether. Gems are favored for their stability and versatility in storing static spells or housing tamed Ethereals, serving as flexible artifacts.
* **Covenant of the Messiahs:** Through veneration and magical knowledge, a human can make a pact with an **Arcana**. The Messiah receives magical facilities to carry out the will of the Arcanum; breaking the pact entails variable and often catastrophic consequences.

---

# IV. Dualism: Technology vs. Magic

In the MasterVerse, technology and magic coexist as separate and often opposing disciplines. While magic is scientifically analyzable, it operates on principles that ignore the conservation of real energy, creating a dynamic where magic may be preferable to technology for specific purposes, and vice versa. The **Definitive Soul** is the only point of convergence, where the two forces stop being opposite to collaborate in a perfect synthesis.

---

# See Technical-Operational Glossary

* **Aether Portal:** Transportation facility that uses the trans-dimensional nature of Aether to move organic matter while keeping the subject's vitality (soul) intact.
* **Kinetic Magic (Bending):** Control of solid, liquid or gaseous masses.
* **Physical Magic:** Direct generation of natural phenomena (lightning, flames, auras).
