# Guilds

**Guilds** are independent paramilitary organizations composed of individuals known as [[en/Glossary/Quester|Questors]]. Born as a private initiative to make up for government inaction, the Guilds have evolved to become the backbone of the new extra-systemic society, offering services ranging from exploration to protection, up to the direct management of settlements and resources.

---

# I. Genesis and the "Wild West" of the 21st Century

The birth of the Guilds is closely linked to the **"Question of the Void"**. At the dawn of the technological revolution, states were unable to manage the rapidity of extra-systemic expansion.

* **State Inaction:** The Aural government did not promptly invest in infrastructure or safeguards for the new borders.
* **The Private Initiative:** Private organizations began sponsoring groups of young explorers, who took control of the Void before the bureaucracy could intervene.
* **The Resistance:** When the government finally attempted to impose regulations, the culture of the Guilds was already established. The result was a series of permissive regulations, described by **Aaron Saava** in his *Treatise on Guilds* (2020) as a true "21st century Wild West".

---

# II. Social Change: The Reformation

A fundamental turning point in the culture of the Guilds was represented by the **Reform on the Age of Majority** promoted by the AUN:

* **Abolition of the Age of Majority:** The concept of chronological age for entry into adult life has been eliminated.
* **Self-Sufficiency Test:** Replaced by a high-precision proficiency test, managed via advanced artificial intelligence. This has allowed young talents to quickly join the Guilds, fueling the continuous growth of the phenomenon.

---

# III. Nature and Corporate Model

The Guilds are no longer simple groups of mercenaries, but real models of alternative society:

* **Paramilitary Model:** Each Guild operates its own command structure, often equipped with its own armed forces and technologies.
* **Diversification of Services:** From planetary reclamation to military defense, from scientific research to commercial management; Guilds sell their skills as a primary source of income.
* **Replacement of the Traditional:** The Guild model is gradually supplanting that of the traditional state society, as it offers greater flexibility, meritocracy and ability to adapt to the extreme conditions of space.

> ### ── Archive Note (from Aaron Saava, 2020) ──
> *«The very existence of starbases is nothing more than an inevitable effect of the slow and backward policy that has characterized the last decade. [...] When the States decided to regulate the new paths, the culture of the frontier had already been written with the blood and audacity of those who did not wait for permission to advance.»*

---

# IV. Current Status

Today the Guilds hold immense power. As States see their influence shrink, Guilds like **MasterBase** or **Trade Houses** operate as full-fledged geopolitical actors, capable of negotiating treaties, managing colonies, and influencing the economies of planetary systems. Their primacy is undisputed in the vacuum of space, where the law is defined by those who have the means to enforce it.
