# Aegift Division

The **Aegift Division** is an elite corps of specialized Masters, established for the defense and garrison of the planet [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Nobi System/Planet Iphiridia/Planet Iphiridia|Iphiridia]]. Named after the ancient founding king of the city of [[en/Location/Arken-City|Arken]], this unit represents the last line of resistance against the biological threats and dangers lurking in the ruins of the infected world.

---

# I. Function and Purpose

The Division does not operate as an expansion force, but as a containment and tactical protection apparatus:
* **Arken's Garrison:** Their primary mission is the defense of Arken's headquarters, ensuring that the sanctuary remains free of the zombie plague.
* **Rift Security:** They are responsible for patrolling and fortifying the rifts to the subsurface and asteroid orbits, critical points for the base's survival.
* **Expedition Support:** They provide armed escort to recovery or search teams sent to contaminated areas, acting as an "anchor" for the safe return of operators.

---

# II. Dedicated Equipment

Due to Iphiridia's extreme environment, the Aegift Division has a unique arsenal and equipment, designed to maximize survivability:
* **Sealed Containment Suits:** Armor that offers protection against airborne pathogens and superior physical resistance to the bites and claws of the infected.
* **Kinetic Impact Weapons:** Focused on quickly stopping targets (often lacking self-preservation), they favor weapons capable of instantly dismembering or neutralizing the threat, minimizing the possibility of close-range contamination.
* **Purification Kit:** Each member is equipped with tools for quickly sanitizing the gates, essential for preventing the spread of the disease within fortified structures.

---

# III. Ethos and Tradition

The name of the division recalls the legacy of **King Aegift**, symbol of firm authority and paternal protection on Iphiridia.
> ### ── Archive Note ──
> *«A member of the Aegift Division does not strive for glory or the spoils of the Trade Houses. He fights because, on Iphiridia, the border between life and oblivion is marked only by the thickness of his armor and the precision of his blow. They are the guardians of a graveyard that refuses to stay that way.”* — MasterBase command report.

---

# IV. Operational Status

The Aegift Division remains on constant alert. With the zombie plague continuing to manifest aggressive variants and the underground proving to be an ever-changing enigma, the role of this unit has become increasingly critical, effectively transforming it into the only true deterrent force left on the planet after its abandonment by other guilds.
