#8 Founding Masters

The **8 Founding Masters** represent the pillar on which the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Starbases/MasterBase|MasterBase]] was built. They were not only the first to unite under one banner, but their original contributions defined the identity, philosophy and operations of the guild. Even after some time, the prestige they enjoy within the organization remains unmatched.

# II. Heritage and Prestige

The concept of "8 Founding Masters" transcends simple historical chronology; it serves as a mythological architrave for the guild:

* **Historical Prestige:** The respect given to these eight individuals does not derive only from seniority, but from the recognition of having transformed an idea into an operational reality capable of moving between systems.
* **Continuity:** Despite the evolution of the guild and the expansion of the number of members, the name of the 8 Founders is recalled in every important decision or official occasion, acting as a moral and strategic compass.

---

# III. Current Relevance

Although the paths of the individual founders may have split, changed or intertwined with complex events (as in the case of **Garo FrankyRose** and his defection from the Frankys or the role of **Emily FrankyRose**), their impact remains unchanged. Today's MasterBase owes its existence to the tactical and diplomatic vision that this small group shared at the origins of extra-systemic colonization.
