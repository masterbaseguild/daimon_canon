#Hiro

**Hiro** is a Questor with an eccentric personality and unpredictable behavior, included in the ranks of the **Lignum Nobili**. A gifted athlete, his defining characteristic—which makes him both a formidable ally and a constant source of anxiety for his teammates—is his decision-making philosophy based entirely on chance.

---

# I. The Dice Method

Hiro doesn't make decisions based on tactical analysis or guild orders. His every action - from the path to follow during an infiltration to the weapon to use in combat - is subordinated to the **roll of a die** that he always carries with him.

* **Indeterminate Free Will:** For Hiro, the dice represents a form of purification from human prejudice. He believes that by letting chance govern his choices, he can operate with "absolute objectivity," free of moral or emotional hesitation.
* **Tactical Impact:** While the method is frustrating for guild leaders, Hiro's success rate remains statistically in line with that of much more conventional operators. This statistical paradox has fueled legends about his "lucky star" (or his ability to manipulate probabilities).

---

# II. Aesthetic Profile

Despite the unpredictability of his actions, Hiro's look is impeccable and deeply cared for, almost in contrast with the chaotic nature of his lifestyle:

* **Outfit:** He regularly wears an outfit consisting of an elegant **shirt**, a tailored **vest** and a **bow tie**, which never seems to get out of place even after the most violent fights.
* **Detail:** He wears a single **earring on his right lobe**, a small distinguishing mark that often serves as a "totem" when he must consult his nut before a critical decision.

---

# III. Role in the Lignum Nobili

Within the **Lignum Nobili** guild, Hiro acts as a "breaking element":

* **Tactical Athlete:** His athletic skills allow him to perform complex acrobatic maneuvers. When the die indicates an aggressive approach, his agility makes him an unpredictable threat to anyone who tries to anticipate his moves.
* **The "X" Factor:** Often assigned to missions where the situation is blocked or stalemated. Hiro's unpredictability acts as a catalyst that forces the enemy to react in a disorderly manner, breaking otherwise impenetrable defensive patterns.

> ### ── Archive Note (Mission Report) ──
> *«I asked him why he decided to throw a smoke grenade instead of covering the right flank. He showed me the dice: a six. He smiled, fixed my bow tie and said that fate had decided to go on the offensive. I don't know if he's a genius or just crazy, but as long as the dice keeps pointing the right way, I won't be the one to stop him."* — Lignum Nobili teammate.

---

# IV. Operational Status
Hiro continues to be an enigma to the Lignum Nobili bureaucracy. It's an asset too valuable to walk away, but too chaotic to manage. His presence is a constant reminder to the guild: in the MasterVerse, sometimes, the best strategy is to let chance guide the warrior's hand.
