# Zayron

**Zayron** is a high-profile frontline operator, renowned for his versatility in close combat and his competitive background. Former member of the renowned **Warpigs** guild during **Master League Zero**, his figure is now inextricably linked to the recent history of tactical competitions in the MasterVerse.

---

# I. Profile and Background

* **The Sports Era:** Before he embraced life as a professional Questor, Zayron was a professional sports duellist. This training forged in him impeccable technique, iron discipline and an above-average ability to read his opponent.
* **Guild Experience:** His time in the *Warpigs* during *Master League Zero* exposed him to high-intensity combat scenarios, helping to transform the duelist's precise, choreographed style into an effective method of tactical neutralization.

---

# II. Role in the MasterBase

Currently, Zayron holds a fluid position within the organization:

* **Frontline Operative:** Despite his fame, Zayron is not an administrative worker. Its place is on the battlefield, where it serves as the spearhead in raid or critical defense missions.
* **Current Status:** Not a permanent member; he appears and disappears aboard the **MasterBase** with a regularity that suggests some sort of privileged arrangement. He is considered a "resource of free initiative": he intervenes where his sword and his experience are needed, often in operations that require surgical precision that other operators cannot guarantee.

---

# III. Fighting Style

Zayron's signature on the court is his **blade**. While warfare technology has shifted towards kinetic ranged combat and digital support, Zayron maintains an anachronistic and lethal martial approach:

* **Martial Mastery:** His sword, despite being a white weapon, is often integrated or supported by enhancements of his [[en/Technology/MasterGear|MasterGear]], making each swing capable of breaking kinetic barriers or standard energy protections.
* **Sporting Approach:** He fights with a duelist's mentality: he is calm, analytical and capable of isolating the main target amidst the chaos of combat, an invaluable skill for the Aegift Division when it comes to dealing with high-level individual threats.

> ### ── Archive Note (from Operational Report) ──
> *«Zayron doesn't fight for the cause, he fights for the gesture. It's strange to see him move through the rubble of an enemy base with the same elegance with which he walked the Master League arenas. But when you see that sword come down, its sporting background no longer matters. It's just the most efficient operator we have in this industry."*

---

# IV. Relationship with the MasterBase
His sporadic but constant presence has made Zayron an almost legendary figure to the new Questars on board. It represents the bridge between the era of "competitive" guilds and the harsh tactical reality of today's MasterBase. His loyalty, although not sanctioned by a binding contract like other members, is considered unshakable once he agrees to put himself at the service of a goal.
