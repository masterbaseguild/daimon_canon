# Give

**Dare** is a high profile operator, currently accredited as a guest of honor and strategic collaborator of [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]]. Recruited directly from [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Entity|Entity]] in 2021, he quickly distinguished himself with his ability to maintain operational cohesion even in crisis scenarios, earning a reputation for reliability and tactical talent.

---

# I. Engagement and Initiatives

Entity's recruitment of Dare is considered an example of the visionary ability of the MasterBase leader, who was able to identify in him the qualities necessary to integrate the operational core of the guild. Since 2021, Dare has taken part in several sensitive operations, often acting as the linchpin in the guild's field missions.

---

# II. The Quest: Golden Boar DeathMatch

The turning point in Dare's MasterBase career came during the **Golden Boar DeathMatch**. The competition represented an extreme test of his leadership and combat management skills:

* **Entity's Retirement:** During the course of the event, the premature (or contextually forced) defection of Entity, team leader, should have marked the end of the MasterBase team's hopes of success.
* **Dare's Resilience:** Instead of giving in to the chaos, Dare took tactical command, reorganizing his resources and leading his teammates with exceptional clarity.
* **The Podium:** Thanks to his direction, the team managed not only to survive the final stages of the DeathMatch, but to reach the podium, confirming Dare as one of the guild's most valuable elements in times of emergency.

---

# III. Operational Profile

Although his official status remains that of "guest", Dare acts with an autonomy that few full members can boast:
* **Adaptability:** Dare demonstrates an exceptional ability to adapt to different fighting styles, integrating rudiments of magic and advanced technologies into a fluid and lethal style.
* **Leadership in Crises:** Its most distinctive characteristic is mental resilience. In an unforgiving moral environment like that of the MasterVerse, Dare is able to operate under extreme pressure without the absence of a rigid chain of command compromising his effectiveness.

> ### ── Archive Note ──
> *«There are those who need orders to fight and those who need a goal. Giving belongs to the second category. When Entity exited the DeathMatch, many saw defeat; he saw only enough space to demonstrate why he was chosen.”* — Internal report, Questors Evaluation Office.

---

# IV. Current Status
Giving continues to be a critical resource for MasterBase. His success in the Golden Boar DeathMatch has attracted the attention of other factions, making him not only a fundamental asset for the guild, but also an individual of great geopolitical interest in the complex chessboard of alliances and enmities between the factions of the Alpha Sphere.
