### Blaze

#### Origins and the WorldOrder War
Blaze is a former [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] and founder of [[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/SpaceHub|SpaceHub]], as well as a former member of [[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/WorldOrder|WorldOrder]]. He was an active part of the WorldOrder during the first great war. Following the defeat of this faction, its members parted ways; it was at this moment that the character broke away from the group to create his own independent guild: SpaceHub.

#### The Rise of SpaceHub and Olympus Financing
Initially, SpaceHub could count on massive external economic support. Thanks to the large amount of money provided to him by Olympus, Blaze managed to transform his guild into a true military and technological superpower. With this immense power, Blaze set himself the ambitious goal of overthrowing the MasterBase as the absolute point of reference for all the Guilds, an act of open rivalry that sparked the Second Master War.

#### The Decline and Fall of the Guild
Blaze's strategic plan to achieve final victory over MasterBase involved building a massive army of androids, designed to overwhelm enemy forces. However, this geopolitical move turned out to be a double-edged sword: by openly opposing MasterBase, SpaceHub began to directly threaten the interests of some crucial commercial and strategic allies for Olympus. As a result, Olympus cut SpaceHub's supplies, leaving it suddenly starved of funds.

In a desperate attempt to complete the production of the robotic army anyway, Blaze made a fatal mistake: he began to cut more and more funds from the "human" (or biological) forces of his own Guild. This drastic drop in resources triggered deep internal discontent which resulted in a real rebellion, culminating in the destruction of the SpaceHub Operations Base. Taking advantage of the chaos and internal civil war, MasterBase launched a timely and devastating attack, definitively annihilating the Guild's forces.

#### Current Status
Despite the collapse of SpaceHub as a superpower, Blaze and the members of the "higher levels" of the guild managed to survive the enemy attack. They are currently in hiding, trying to rebuild the faction from its ashes.
