# Rubix

**Rubix** is an enigmatic figure who moves on the fringes of **MasterBase**. Ex-[[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] with extensive operational experience, maintains a sporadic but constant collaborative relationship with the organization, while refusing to submit to a formal hierarchical framework.

---

# I. History and Affiliations

* **Orbit Entry (2020):** Rubix makes contact with MasterBase around 2020. From that point, his name regularly appears in mission logs as an external support element or specialist in high-risk operations.
* **The Bond with Giaco:** He is mainly known as **Giaco's** regular companion on operations. Their synergy in the field is legendary among operators, based on years of shared survival across the Void's most dangerous routes.
* **Lone Wolf:** Despite how often he operates alongside the members of MasterBase, Rubix has always maintained his free-hitting status. His refusal to officially join the guild is a deliberate choice, aimed at preserving his decision-making autonomy and freedom of movement.

---

# II. Operational Role

Not being bound by guild contracts, Rubix operates according to its own rules:

* **Highly Flexible Asset:** His willingness to participate in quests of various kinds makes him a valuable asset to the MasterBase, especially when it is necessary to conduct operations that require "off the radar" management or that do not fall within the guild's standard procedures.
* **Tactical Coordination:** In missions shared with Giaco, Rubix often acts as the cynical and pragmatic element, capable of adapting to sudden changes in the tactical scenario.

---

# III. Operational Philosophy

The choice to remain a "lone wolf" defines every aspect of his career:

* **Strategic Independence:** Rubix avoids the ties of logistical dependence that MasterBase imposes on its members, preferring to manage its own vehicles, supplies and sustenance.
* **Reputation:** He is respected not only for his skill, but for the consistency with which he has refused offers of integration, maintaining his identity as an independent Questor intact in a world that increasingly tends towards the centralization of the guilds.

> ### ── Archive Note ──
> *«Rubix does not need MasterBase; negotiate with it. He is a man who understands the value of a guild, but fears the weight of its mark. When he is alongside Giaco, they form a duo that is as good as an entire attack team, but never ask him to sign a permanent contract. The day Rubix asks for an official uniform, it will mean that he has stopped being Rubix.»* — Excerpt from the tactical diary of a MasterBase veteran.

---

# IV. Current Status
He currently continues to operate as an independent mercenary, occasionally lending himself to the needs of MasterBase. His position is that of a highly trusted "external ally", whose presence in a tactical team is often a guarantee of a positive outcome for missions that other personnel would consider too risky or unconventional.
