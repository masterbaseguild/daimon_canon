# Vaximo: The Shadow of the Capital

**Vaximo** was an influential and respected [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] human belonging to the elite ranks of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Olympus Foundation/Olympus Foundation|Olympus Foundation]]. Operating for years as an informant and undercover agent on the planet Gondoraw, Vaximo made history for his crucial role as a mentor to [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Emily|Emily Franky Rose]] and for protecting the secrets of the royal lineage before its tragic fall.

# II. Narrative Arcs and History

### 1. The Gondraw Infiltrator
Equipped with excellent espionage and diplomatic skills, Vaximo is deployed by Olympus on the planet Gondoraw. His official job is to act as a **high-level informant to the Royal Family (the Franky Family)**. Moving in the shadows of the court and the palaces of power, Vaximo has been collecting strategic information on the clan's organization and dealings for years, keeping his cover intact.

### 2. Emily's Mentor
Vaximo's life intersects closely with that of Emily following the girl's dramatic escape from the Ring (the underground fighting circuit controlled by her family). Recognizing the young woman's potential and desperation, Vaximo decides to take her under his wing:
* Acts as her **mentor**, training her to survive and channel her deadly strength in combat.
* Leverages her influences and Olympus channels to **help her make a new name and identity for herself in the Capital**, hiding her tracks from Franky's bounty hunters.

### 3. Ariel's Assignment and Bridget's Custody
The trust placed in Vaximo's abilities pushes Queen Ariel to entrust him with one of the most delicate and secret missions of the Alpha Sphere. Vaximo receives direct orders to **care for, protect and educate young Bridget**, guardian of a heavy inheritance. The Olympus agent fulfills his mandate with absolute dedication, taking charge of the little girl's safety and hiding her from the spies of the royal faction.

---

# III. FrankyRose's Escape and Death

Vaximo's fate is tragically fulfilled during the tumultuous events linked to **Operation FrankyRose** (the cutting-edge stealth mission led by *Garo* to exfiltrate his cousin Emily from Gondraw and free her once and for all from the clutches of her family of origin).

When the assassins and the Franky armies discover the escape plan and close the circle around the fugitives, the situation worsens. To ensure the safety of Emily, Bridget and the Masters involved in the recovery, **Vaximo decides to act as a human shield**. He fights a desperate battle of containment against the overwhelming forces of the criminal clan, ending up **losing his life on the field**.

# IV. Narrative Legacy
