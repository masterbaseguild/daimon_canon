# Lee

**Lee** was the founder and leader of **Netcom**, an organization that marked a dark chapter in the history of the Alpha Sphere. Born with the aim of documenting the news of the guilds, its work has transformed into a widespread system of blackmail and disinformation.

---

## I. The Rise of Netcom
* **Original Objective:** Lee founded Netcom with the stated intent of filling the information void that plagued the Alpha Sphere, where important events were distorted by word of mouth.
* **The Corruption of Power:** Exclusive control over the flow of information soon became a weapon. Lee began using Netcom as an extortion tool, threatening weaker guilds with fake news to destroy their reputations if they did not give in to his demands.

## II. The Clash with the MasterBase
When Lee's illicit activities became unsustainable, **MasterBase** stepped in to restore order.
* **The Counteroffensive:** Lee (often referred to in records as *TGD* in the final stages of the conflict) did not surrender, but responded by launching a massive propaganda campaign, accusing the Masters of being hypocrites thirsting for power and glory.
* **The Role of LGD:** The turning point came thanks to the independent broadcaster **LGD**. Despite pressure from Netcom to align with its distorted narrative, LGD staff chose to maintain their journalistic integrity.

## III. The Collapse and Disappearance
* **LGD's Sacrifice:** Netcom, feeling threatened by the truth brought to light by LGD, ordered the bombing of their station. This act of extreme violence was the point of no return: public opinion turned against Lee, the Masters managed to conclusively document Netcom's lies, and the organization was dismantled.
* **The Epilogue:** After the station's forced closure, Lee and all of his original staff disappeared into thin air. There is no definitive clue as to their fate, fueling theories that they sought refuge in remote corners of the Alpha Sphere or were clandestinely executed.
