# Hyberna

**Hyberna** was a powerful sorceress originally from **Iphiridia**, whose existence was marked by a tragic paradox: forced by the duty to protect the planet's secrets, she ended up becoming exactly the "monster" that the rumors described, in order to preserve a superior truth.

---

# I. The Hermit of Phiclidia

* **Refuge:** Hyberna lived in isolation in the remote and desolate region of **Phiclidia**. His solitude served a higher purpose: to monitor the suspicious activities of the **Xadrium** faction, which had established its headquarters nearby.
* **Xadrium's Strategy:** To maintain the secrecy of their operations, Xadrium deliberately spread the rumor that Hyberna was a ruthless witch, ready to kill anyone who dared approach her home.

---

# II. The Paradox of Survival

Hyberna was aware of the propaganda plotted against her, but found herself in a deadly trap:
* **The Necessity of Defense:** Many adventurers, attracted by the legends of the "hermit sorceress", came to Phiclidia seeking glory or riches. In order to continue its research into the real intentions of the Xadrium without being discovered or interrupted, Hyberna was forced to systematically eliminate anyone who entered its territory.
* **The Loss of Humanity:** What began as a tactical necessity to protect the planet spiraled into violence, making the initially false rumors of the Xadrium a brutal reality.

---

# III. The Encounter with Entity and the Warning

In her last moments, Hyberna received a visit from **Entity**, who arrived in Phiclidia with the same investigative intentions that had moved the sorceress.
* **The Last Council:** Aware of the impending end, Hyberna chose not violence, but revelation. He urged Entity to abandon Iphiridia immediately, issuing a prescient warning: *"Someone has his eye on us, and that someone has no intention of stopping until he gets what he wants."*

---

# IV. Archive note
> *«Hyberna died not because of magic, but because of a truth she couldn't share. She became the protector of a secret that cost her soul, killing passing heroes to prevent them from entangle the true enemy. If we had heeded her warning sooner, perhaps Iphiridia would not have fallen into the shadows into which she sank after her disappearance.”* — Diary fragment found in the ruins of Phiclidia.
