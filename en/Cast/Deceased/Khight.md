# Khight

**Khight** was a pirate-oriented Questor, known for his treacherous conduct and his failed attempt to sabotage one of the most significant sporting events in the history of Questors: the **Master League Zero**.

---

# I. Failure at Master League Zero

The figure of Khight is inextricably linked to his participation in the *Master League Zero*. Instead of relying on his athletic or strategic skills, Khight chose the path of deception:
* **Attempt to Fraud:** He tried in every way to manipulate the results of the tournament to ensure victory, violating the sporting spirit of the competition. His efforts, however, did not lead to the desired results, being neutralized by the surveillance and integrity of the tournament itself.

---

# II. The Sabotage and the Clash with Dany

Not accepting defeat on the field, Khight chose the path of extremism:
* **The Attack on the League:** In an attempt to cancel the entire tournament and "screw up" the event after his sporting failure, Khight attempted a direct assault on the League.
* **Dany's Intervention:** His sabotage plan was decisively interrupted by **Dany**, who confronted him and stopped him before he could cause irreparable damage, putting an end to his threat and decreeing his final defeat on that occasion.

---

# III. Family ties

* **Brotherhood:** Khight was **Martif's** brother. Their relationship is often cited in the news to contrast the path of the two: while Khight is remembered for his pirate and disloyal behavior, the family bond with Martif remains an essential biographical fact to frame his origin and his personal dynamics.
