# Stelio: The Master of Psychological Warfare

**Stelio** is one of the most insidious figures in the criminal history of the Alpha Sphere. Known for being one of the founding fathers and the undisputed supreme leader of the **CC3** pirate guild (as well as the mastermind behind the *Cancercious* faction), Stelio has focused his entire Questor career on deception, blackmail and mental manipulation, elevating psychological warfare to his primary weapon.

---

# I. Philosophy and Fighting Style

Unlike the classic warlords or mercenary commanders of the Alpha Sphere, Stelio despises brute force for its own sake. He is described in the tactical registers as an **absolute expert in psychological warfare**, a discipline that he masters to the point of being able to rout enemy defense lines and win a battle even before a single initial shot is fired.

Through the obsessive study of the psychological profiles of his adversaries, Stelio identifies traumas, structural weaknesses or cracks in the chain of command, and then hits them with orchestrated threats, mirror games and verbal manipulations. Its constant goal is to push the enemy into panic, unconditional surrender, or fatal error caused by emotional pressure.

---

# II. The Blackmail Attempt and Beat's Intervention

At the height of the CC3's power, Stelio architects a complex extortion operation on an interstellar scale against the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]]. By exploiting confidential information or security gaps in the Entity faction, the pirate leader initiates heavy **political and military blackmail** that risks compromising the very stability of the StarBase.

However, Stelio's perfect plan collapses due to a variable he hadn't calculated: **Beat's turnaround. The elite spy, who had recently chosen to secretly abandon the directives of the *Xadrium Corporation* to protect the Masters, intercepts Stelio's maneuvers. Moving in the shadows with equal cunning, Beat manages to defuse the blackmail vectors, neutralizing the threat before Stelio can capitalize on his advantage and inflicting a heavy diplomatic setback on the CC3.

---

# III. The Boarding of the StarBase and the Military Defeat

The definitive confrontation between Stelio and the guild takes place during the [[en/Timeline/Redemption|Redemption]] era. Taking advantage of the strategic absence of most of the veteran Masters, who left for an external mission, Stelio launches the CC3 assault ships to directly board the undefended MasterBase.

On the command bridge, Stelio unleashes the culmination of his manipulative rhetoric, attempting to terrorize the few defenders remaining on board into surrendering the station's codes. The defense is taken over by **Blu** and **Beat** (at the time still immersed in the Masters' counter-espionage plots), supported by the intervention of *Latency*.

* **The Breaking of the Bluff:** Demonstrating exceptional acumen and firmness, the defense team does not allow itself to be affected by Stelio's psychological pressure. Blu and Beat respond blow for blow to the pirate leader's mental war, unmasking his bluffs and the weak points of his own strategy.
* **The Collapse of CC3:** Deprived of his psychological advantage and forced into a brutal trench battle, Stelio's genius falters. The Masters prove their clear war and technological superiority in the field, massacring the CC3 vanguards and forcing Stelio into a disorderly retreat, marking the beginning of the end for his pirate organization.

---

# IV. Final Status Quo

Following the military defeat suffered on MasterBase and the failure of his blackmail, Stelio's figure drastically loses credibility within the sector's criminal circuits. With the final and official dismantling of CC3, Stelio's name remains etched in the archives as that of a fearsome king of bluff, whose brilliantly perverse mind was ultimately outclassed by the cold determination of the Masters.
