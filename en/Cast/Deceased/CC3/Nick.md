#Nick

**Nick** was a highly influential [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]], known for having played in **CC3** before joining the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]] in its auroral phases. His parable is marked by a tragic internal struggle against the control of others and by the desperate attempt to redeem a pirate past.

---

### I. The Connection with the MasterBase and Mentorship
Having joined MasterBase at its origins, Nick quickly became a point of reference for new recruits. His most significant contribution to the structure was the mentorship with **Garo**: it was Nick who guided Garo in the tactical transition from the use of the *Zweihander* to that of the **harpoon**, teaching him the precision and control techniques that would define Garo's fighting style in the years to come.

---

### II. The Past in CC3 and Stelio's Subplot
Before MasterBase, Nick was affiliated with the **CC3** guild, under the command of **Stelio**. This bond turned out to be an invisible prison: Nick was in fact the victim of a **mind control chip** installed by Stelio, a device that inextricably linked his destiny to the will of the CC3 leader. His entry into MasterBase represented an extreme attempt to change his life and leave his identity as a pirate behind.

---

### III. Tragedy and Escape
Nick's redemption was abruptly interrupted when Stelio, using the mind control chip, remotely activated the subjugation protocol. Forced to become a tool in the hands of his former leader, Nick was faced with an impossible choice: betray his MasterBase comrades or rebel against the system that dominated him.

In order not to cause any harm to the members of the Guild to whom he was linked by deep friendship and loyalty, Nick chose the path of self-exile. In an act of extreme sacrifice, he fled MasterBase to distance himself from those he loved and protect them from Stelio's murderous orders. His fate remains unknown to this day.

---

### IV. Status and Legacy
Nick is remembered by the members of MasterBase not for his tragic exit, but for his role as a mentor and for the loyalty he demonstrated in the act of escape. His story remains a constant reminder of the weight of the chains of the past and the moral integrity needed to break them, even at the cost of one's life.
