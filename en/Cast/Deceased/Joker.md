# Joker

**Joker** is a Questor whose competitive path is deeply intertwined with that of **Xyroh**. His career is marked by participation in some of the most competitive circuits on the scene, where he has been able to stand out for his experience in the field.

---

# I. Competitive path

* **Partnership with Xyroh:** Joker and Xyroh shared the competitive front, playing together in one of the teams that took part in the **SL** (League/Season). Their collaboration was a key element for their team dynamic, based on trust consolidated by years of shared activity.
* **Season Zero Experience:** Participated in **Season Zero**, an experience that allowed him to test his skills in the early stages of what would become a complex competitive landscape. This participation places him among the veterans who have experienced the evolution of the game since its inception.

---

# II. Profile and Connections

* **Alliance:** His identity is strongly linked to that of Xyroh. The fact that they competed together in an official team suggests an uncommon tactical synergy and a loyalty that goes beyond the simple sporting sphere.
* **Status:** Although his name is associated with highly competitive contexts, his figure is that of a veteran who prefers direct action to media fame, maintaining a constant but measured presence.
