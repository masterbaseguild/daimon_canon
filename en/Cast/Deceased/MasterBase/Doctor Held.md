# Doctor Held

**Doctor Held** was one of the brightest and most respected engineers at **MasterBase**. His work was crucial to the guild's technological development during the Alpha Sphere's consolidation period.

---

## I. The Technological Contribution
* **Role:** As chief engineer, Held oversaw the design and maintenance of critical infrastructure for MasterBase.
* **The Legacy:** Many of the defensive and logistical systems that ensured the base's survival during its formative years bear his signature. His ability to innovate under limited resources made him an invaluable asset to Masters leadership.

## II. The First War Master
The conflict that would mark the history of MasterBase required a sacrifice that went beyond simple technical advice.
* **Commitment to the Resistance:** When things came to a head with the outbreak of the **First Master War**, Held didn't just work in the laboratories. He took the front line with the **Resistance**, actively contributing to the strategic defense of the front.
* **Fallen in Battle:** His life tragically came to an end during the most agitated phases of the conflict. His death was not only the loss of a brilliant mind, but a moral blow for the entire faction, which saw one of its constructive pillars fall.

---

## III. Archive note
> *«Doctor Held didn't just build machines; he built the confidence on which the Masters' hopes rested. When the First Master War broke out, he put aside plans to take up the arms of the resistance. His passing left a void in laboratories that, even today, many engineers struggle to fill. He died defending the future he helped design.”* — MasterBase Historical Obituary.

---

## IV. Operational Status
Deceased. Killed in action during the First Master War.
