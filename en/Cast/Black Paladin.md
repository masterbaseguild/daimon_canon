Black Paladin is a former [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]].

Joins MasterBase after the First Master War
In an attempt to advance his career, he brings on board his old acquaintances, Beat and Blu, and becomes friends with Tecmov
Unmasked, he exiles himself; however, every now and then he appears on board, covered by a cloak, without revealing himself to anyone.
