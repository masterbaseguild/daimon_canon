Albe is a [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] and athlete of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Lignum Nobili/Lignum Nobili|Lignum Nobili]], historic ex-member of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]] within which he still resides as an external consultant.

# Narrative Profile and Timeline

### The Entrance to the MasterBase
An old and trusted acquaintance of **Aledge**, Albe makes his official entry into the MasterBase in the phases following the conclusion of the [[en/Timeline/Master War II|Second War Master]], joining the ranks of the Questors in the moment of reconstruction.

### Involvement in the Uprising
During his stay on board, Albe remains fatally involved in the tumultuous events of the Uprising (the coup d'état plotted by Beat against Entity). Following the investigations and purges following the revolt, his position was carefully examined: recognized as innocent of the most serious crimes, he was officially acquitted of all charges and freed.

### The Sporting Turn in Lignum Nobili
Deeply affected by the guild's internal tensions, Albe decides to officially withdraw from MasterBase. Taking advantage of his athletic skills, he joins the [[en/Cast/Groups/Lignum Nobili/Lignum Nobili|Lignum Nobili]], a faction that under the leadership of Keia is becoming a competitive powerhouse. Here Albe stands out for a time as one of their star athletes.

### The Return and Current Role
After concluding his active competitive stint, Albe leaves Lignum Nobili. He then decides to return to where his career began, settling back to live aboard the MasterBase. Although he is no longer a front-line operational Questor, he provides the guild with the wealth of skills he has gained in sport, covering an important role of **strategic consultancy on the organization of tournaments**.
