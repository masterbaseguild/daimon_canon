#FrostVincy

**Frost Vincy** is an ex-[[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] and high-level athlete, famous for having played in **Romita**, one of the most iconic formations in the extra-systemic competitive panorama. His career is inextricably linked to his team's rise on the professional circuit.

---

### I. Sports Career and Master League Zero
Frost Vincy held the role of **Marksman** within the Romitas. Under his technical guidance and thanks to his ballistic precision, the team reached the top of **Master League Zero**, establishing itself as one of the "Top Teams" of the entire system. His fighting style, characterized by an icy coldness and millimetric control of distances, made him a point of reference for anyone who wanted to approach the role of sharpshooter in the context of guild competitions.

---

### II. The Bond with the Romitas
Within the Romitas, Frost Vincy was not just a tactical performer, but a pillar of the team's identity. His ability to manage high-pressure situations during Master League Zero matches has helped define the team's reputation as a dominant force, capable of overturning seemingly compromised outcomes thanks to individual play of the highest caliber.

---

### III. Inheritance
Despite the Romitas' subsequent dissolution, Frost Vincy's impact on the industry continues to be a subject of study for young Questors. His ability to combine the discipline of a professional athlete with the pragmatic mentality of a Questor has left an indelible mark, making him an almost mythological figure in the chronicles of Master League Zero.
