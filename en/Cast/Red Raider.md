# RedRaider

**Red Raider** is an ex-[[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] of **MasterBase**, specializing in silent infiltration tactics and surgical sabotage. Its reputation is built on its ability to neutralize entire enemy garrisons without resorting to immediate lethality, preferring to leave its targets in a state of total sensory vulnerability.

---

# I. Operational Profile

Red Raider is not a pitched combat fighter, but a tactical predator that prefers to strike from the shadows:

* **Infiltration:** His specialty is accessing high-security zones, where stealth is the only way to overcome defense systems.
* **Sabotage:** Once inside, its goal is to cripple the enemy's chain of command or critical infrastructure, setting the stage for assault units or extracting sensitive data.

---

# II. Equipment and Fighting Style

Red Raider's skill set is unique among the Questars:

* **Sensory Wave Emitters:** This is its distinctive feature. Through devices mounted on his armor or integrated into his equipment, Red Raider is able to project pulses that directly interfere with his opponents' nervous systems. These impulses induce dizziness, disorientation and temporary sensory paralysis, rendering the target unable to react or call for help.
* **Short-Range Blade:** In combination with emitters, Red Raider uses a sword to finish the fight. His technique is rapid and precise: after stunning the opponent, the blade intervenes only to definitively neutralize the threat or to destroy enemy technological equipment.
* **Tactical Vulnerability:** Its true strength is leaving enemies "vulnerable". Once stunned, targets remain helpless, allowing Red Raider to proceed with his sabotage without disturbance.

---

# III. Past and Current State

* **The Connection to MasterBase:** A former member of the guild, Red Raider served MasterBase during times of great instability in the frontier. His contribution was fundamental in missions where the use of brute force would have compromised the success of the mission.
* **Leaving the Guild:** His transition to independent activity occurred in a discreet manner. It was not a traumatic breakup, but a voluntary departure due to the desire to operate independently, far from bureaucratic red tape or overly broad team missions that limited his range of action.

# IV. Operational Notes
Today, Red Raider remains a ghost operator. His collaboration with MasterBase is sporadic; he is often contacted for "deniability" operations (in which the guild must not appear to be involved) due to his ability to operate with surgical precision, leaving very few traces behind.
