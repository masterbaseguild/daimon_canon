# Mr. Jey

**Mr. Jey** is a living legend within **MasterBase**. As a member of the **8 Founding Masters**, his figure transcends the simple role of operator: he is one of the pillars on which the entire organization was built. His past as an elite athlete of the **Olympus Foundation** is still reflected today in his methodical and disciplined approach to fighting.

---

# I. Origins: From the Ring to the Void

Mr. Jey's parable begins far from guild life:

* **The Olympus Era:** Served at the Olympus Foundation for three years. During this time, he distinguished himself as a gladiator in internal tournaments, becoming a name known for his hand-to-hand skill and stoic tenacity.
* **Encounter with Entity:** During one of the Foundation's flagship tournaments, Mr. Jey crossed paths with **Entity**. Sensing not only his athletic but strategic potential, Entity personally recruited him into the nascent MasterBase, marking the beginning of his true career.

---

# II. Magical Evolution: The Renunciation of the Blade

One of the most unique aspects of Mr. Jey's biography is his drastic war paradigm shift:

* **Master Nephasto:** Not satisfied with his own skill level, Mr. Jey sought a greater challenge. He turned to **Nephasto**, asking him to be trained in the magical arts.
* **The Choice to Abandon:** To dedicate himself body and soul to this new path, he made the drastic decision to abandon the art of the sword, the discipline that had made him famous in Olympus. This transition from physical warrior to energy manipulator has redefined his identity as a *Master*.

---

# III. Fighting Style and Skill

His current arsenal is based exclusively on the manipulation of **Light Magic**:

* **Solar Beam:** A focused beam attack of pure light energy, capable of piercing kinetic defenses.
* **Empower:** A support skill that enhances the physical or magical abilities of your comrades-in-arms, making them a tactical linchpin in group battles.
* **Conceal:** An art of concealment based on the refraction of light, allowing him to disappear from sight or create optical illusions to disorient the enemy.

---

# IV. Key Events

* **The Mastercon Campaign:** During this large-scale conflict, Mr. Jey came close to dying. The event profoundly affected his psyche, transforming him from an athlete seeking challenges to a thoughtful and pragmatic leader, aware of the fragility of life in the Void.

> *«Mr. Jey gave up his sword to learn how to bend light. Many thought it was madness, but look at him now: there is no shadow for MasterBase's enemies to hide in when he decides to light up the battlefield. He is our lighthouse, but he is also the most dangerous among us.”* — Founders' Report.

---

# V. Status and Personality
Although his personality is undergoing narrative evolution, Mr. Jey is perceived as a silent, authoritative figure. His membership in the group of 8 Founders gives him considerable political weight, but his true strength lies in the constant search to overcome his own limits, a legacy of his past as a gladiator which has not yet abandoned him.
