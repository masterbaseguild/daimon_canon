# Donny

**Donny** is a notable ex-[[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] and founder of [[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/MadHeadz|MadHeadz]]. His parable, marked by an aggressive rise and a decline marked by deception, represents one of the most controversial chapters in the inter-guild relations of the Alpha Sphere.

---

### I. Rise and Alliance Strategy
As the Founding Leader of MadHeadz, Donny understood the strategic value of [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]] early on. As soon as it emerged as the dominant power, Donny orchestrated a policy of close rapprochement. His goal was not disinterested loyalty, but rather targeted infiltration: he sought to gain exclusive access to MasterBase's knowledge, technological protocols and patents to elevate the MadHeadz above any other rival Guild.

---

### II. Deception and the Keyplexity Crisis
The true nature of Donny's plans emerged following discovery by **Hooneex**, leader of the **Keyplexity** guild. Hooneex discovered that a number of technology patents, legally held by Keyplexity and ostensibly "granted" to MasterBase, had in fact been clandestinely stolen by Donny. The MadHeadz leader was exploiting this intellectual property without any authorization, passing it off as internal innovation to curry favor with the Masters.

This scandal triggered a devastating diplomatic and operational crisis:
* **Resentment:** Instead of acknowledging his guilt, Donny developed a deep resentment towards MasterBase, accusing them of "exposing" him or failing to protect his position.
* **Sabotage:** From an ally, Donny transformed into a silent antagonist, starting to actively plot to hinder the Masters' operations, trying to undermine their internal stability from the shadows.

---

### III. The Fall: Neiro's Backdoor
Donny's plan suffered a definitive setback due to the intervention of **Neiro**. Through a digital backdoor operation conducted with pinpoint precision, Neiro was able to penetrate the MadHeadz's security systems, exposing not only the patent thefts, but also the entire sabotage network orchestrated by Donny against MasterBase.

Stripped of their digital infrastructure and discredited in front of the entire Questars community, MadHeadz were forced into unconditional capitulation.

---

### IV. Epilogue: The Exile
After the capitulation, the guild lost all bargaining power. To avoid legal retaliation, the humiliation of forced subordination, or the complete destruction of their legacy, MadHeadz made the final decision to remove themselves from the territories under the influence of MasterBase. Their exit marked the end of Donny's ambition and the dissolution of what had been one of the most promising groups on the frontier.
