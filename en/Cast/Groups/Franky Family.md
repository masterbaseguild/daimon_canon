# History

Originating on [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Gondraw System/Planet Rose|planet Rose]], the Franky family very quickly rose to the top of Gondran society, thanks to the ability of its members to maintain alliance relationships with important figures of the time. In particular, the friendship with the Rose family proved to be very useful, as this family would soon become one of the main vassals of the royal family.

After the drying up of the three planets and the consequent need for new commercial expansions, the two families lent themselves to the cause, building a massive fleet of shuttles and migrating towards the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Gondraw System/Asteroid Belt|asteroid belt]].

However, while the other trade houses could enjoy total independence from the royal family, the influence of the Roses prevented the Frankys from doing the same, thus causing a general discontent between the members of the two families, which culminated in the schism of 2009, when following some violent episodes the Rose family was forced to flee, also leaving the Franky family carte blanche.

Later, thanks to their new independence and a mysterious ally, the Franky family grew significantly in influence, becoming the most feared and respected trade house in the entire asteroid belt.

The Frankys are one of the mafia families of Luna Gondrana. Among the members are found

- Garo Franky
- Zzb Franky
- Bzz Franky
- Nekta Franky (who managed to break away from his origins, and made his fortune as a manufacturer and trader abroad)

This family aspires to control the entire planet, and is willing to do anything to achieve it, even to come to terms with Xadrium and become its province.
The deal is simple: the Franky will become the absolute rulers of Gondora, and in exchange they will submit to the Coalition as a province. To begin diplomatic relations, the Frankys send a diplomatic embassy, ​​escorted by three Hypers (Garo, Bzz and Zzb) to a meeting with representatives of the coalition and the red faction on aura.
During this meeting, an incursion by the White Faction takes place, backed by new allies: the first Masters, some of whom have decided to participate in the operation.
During the fighting, Entity is isolated by Zzb and Bzz, but manages to get the better of them thanks to the Golden Soul; when Garo finds out what happened to his brothers, he goes berserk and tries to kill Entity in turn, who however prefers to talk instead of fighting.
Garo is persuaded by Entity to join his cause, and once the operation is finished he will not return home.
