The Blue Owls are an influential and fearsome [[en/Glossary/Guild|guild]] of the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Alpha Sphere|Alpha Sphere]]. Born as a rebel faction in exile, they subsequently evolved into an independent organization, officially allied with the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]], known as much for its undisputed war prowess as for the irreverent character and fierce competitiveness of its members.

# History

### The Uprising and Exile of the Faction
The origins of the guild are inextricably linked to the dramatic events of the [[en/Timeline/Uprising|Uprising]]. Following Alone's expulsion from his rank as Master, a violent insurrection breaks out within MasterBase. The coup fails and Alone, together with a large circle of friends, supporters and insurgents who had taken an active part in the rebellion, is sentenced to immediate exile from the station.

### The Birth on the Arpeggio
Finding themselves without a base, the dissidents obtain temporary political asylum aboard the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Starbases/Arpeggio|Arpeggio]] (Navirad's starbase). During this short stay, the group decides to band together and formalize their union so as not to disperse: thus the **Blue Owls** were born, a guild founded with the specific initial intent of making direct and open opposition to the MasterBase.

### The Expulsion and Reconciliation
However, the stay on the Arpeggio turns out to be unstable and the Blue Owls are finally chased away from the second starbase as well. Once again nomadic, the guild's fortunes turn around thanks to a slow and progressive improvement in diplomatic relations with the MasterBase. Through negotiations and overcoming old hostilities, Master command grants the Blue Owls permission to rejoin the main [[en/Glossary/StarBase|starbase]]. The guild thus obtains the right to establish itself and operate within the structure, while maintaining total and absolute independence as an autonomous organization.

### Current Status
Nowadays, the Blue Owls are considered to all intents and purposes front-line strategic allies of the MasterBase, to which they provide military support by taking part in almost all of its missions, campaigns and initiatives.

Although the alliance is solid, their presence on board is often a source of strong tensions: the other allied factions and guilds regularly complain about the Owls' disrespectful, provocative attitude and exasperated competitive spirit. Nonetheless, their deadly effectiveness on the battlefield remains beyond question, cementing them as one of the most feared and avoided military powers in the entire Alpha Sphere.
