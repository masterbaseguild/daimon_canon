Alone is a [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] engineer of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Blue Owls/Blue Owls|Blue Owls]], ex-member of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]], ex-founder of [[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/NextWare|NextWare]] and ex-member of [[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/SpaceFox|SpaceFox]].

He had an Emo haircut, like Aphelios or The Dark Knight.
He fought using gadgets.
Chaotic Personality
Haughty and Flirty
Founding Leader of NextWare
When NextWare is burned to the ground by the Young Fighters, he follows Aledge and joins MasterBase
Internally, it contributes greatly to the A.R.C. System, as well as resuming the Arc-X project, abandoned by Held and Alex after their deaths.
However, his mental instability leads him to become a dangerous subject for the Base, which is why Entity decides to remove him from the Team. This event will trigger the Uprising, although Alone was not directly responsible.
Afterwards, Alone abandons MasterBase.
After being exiled from MasterBase, he requests asylum on the Arpeggio, one of MasterBase's subsidiary ships, and Kurymi, against Entity's wishes, hosts him, along with his other companions.
Following further disagreements, however, Alone and his group are also kicked out of Arpeggio, and decide to found their own guild, the Blue Owls.
During this time, Alone learns the basics of fighting.
Back in the MasterBase, Alone experiments on his body and installs cybernetic upgrades.
He dresses extremely casually, without any criteria (neko hat, ski goggles, anything)
Computer engineer, author of the arc project, the masterbase's on-board computer, is equipped with a biotech arm equipped with crowd control hacking abilities, capable of causing any electronic system to go haywire. in battle, it specializes in blocking targets and disrupting enemy systems.
