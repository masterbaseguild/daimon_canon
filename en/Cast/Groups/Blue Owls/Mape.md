#Mape

**Mape** is an enigmatic [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] athlete, founding member of [[en/Cast/Groups/Blue Owls/Blue Owls|Blue Owls]]. An iconic figure for his peculiar aesthetics and for an electrical power still in the process of being understood, Mape is known for his nomadic nature and for a constant streak of defiance towards authority, in particular towards the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]].

---

### I. Nature of Powers
Mape's warfare potential is fueled by a mysterious energy source.
* **Lightning Charges:** He is capable of generating devastating electrical discharges, similar to pure lightning.
* **Accumulation:** He possesses the ability to store these charges at will, making his energy reserves unpredictable.
* **Visual Manifestation:** As a side effect of his bio-electrical activity, his eyes constantly glow with a blinding white light, identical to that of his lightning bolts.

---

### II. Aesthetics and Iconography
Mape is instantly recognizable on the battlefield. His skin is pure white, in stark contrast to all the rest of his clothing and equipment, which are completely black.
* **The Badge of Heresy:** Wear the official MasterBase badge strictly upside down. This gesture is not a mistake, but a deliberate act of provocation towards **Entity**, aimed at underlining his contempt for the Master hierarchy.

---

### III. Biography and Event Arpeggio
Mape's past remains shrouded in darkness. It is known that he met **Alone** long ago and took an active part in the turbulent period of the **Uprising**, before disappearing from the radar.
* **The Arpeggio Incident:** While temporarily in the Space Hall of the guild known as *Arpeggio*, Mape witnessed Alone and Christian's verbal and political confrontation with Kurymi, culminating in their expulsion. Mape decided to openly oppose the decision taken, consequently suffering the same expulsion.
* **The Birth of the Blue Owls:** Following this event, Mape helped found the Blue Owls, the guild in which he currently operates.

---

### IV. Current Status
Mape is a nomad by vocation, perpetually traveling through the folds of the Void. He currently lives in a state of exile, far from the dominant circles of power, continuing to explore the limits of his power and cultivating his role as a "loose cannon" within the political geography of the Questars.
