# Gianco

**Gianco** is a [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] highly experienced athlete, known for having played in several leading formations in the extra-systemic panorama. His career is marked by a dynamic path, characterized by crucial transitions between some of the most influential organizations in the Alpha Sphere and by a complex political epilogue.

---

### I. Rise into the Whitedemons and Master League Zero
Gianco initially gained notoriety as a member of the **Whitedemons**, a high-level competitive team. Together with them he took to the stages of the **Master League Zero**, standing out for his athletic abilities and tactical flexibility. During the agitated phases of the League, Gianco's talent attracted the attention of the observers of [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]], who saw in him the potential for a more strategic management role.

---

### II. The Entrance to the MasterBase
Following the excellent performance offered in the Master League Zero, Gianco was officially recruited by MasterBase. It was not a simple insertion into the operational ranks: Gianco was integrated into the **League Organizational Team**, taking on direct responsibilities in the administration and management of the competition's operational flows. In this capacity, he represented the face of MasterBase within the League structure, acting as a connection point between the Masters organization and the athletes.

---

### III. The Decline: The Uprising and the Falling Away
The negative turning point in Gianco's trajectory coincided with the closing of Master League Zero. The period of political instability that followed saw his direct involvement in the so-called **Uprising**. Due to his actions and role during this insurrection, trust between Gianco and the MasterBase hierarchy came to an abrupt end. The result was the athlete's definitive removal from the Guild, a measure that marked the end of his connection with the Masters and his subsequent relocation to the [[en/Cast/Groups/Blue Owls/Blue Owls|Blue Owls]].

---

### IV. Current Status
Today Gianco operates under the aegis of the Blue Owls, bringing with him the wealth of skills acquired during his stay in the MasterBase. His figure remains a warning about the volatility of alliances in the Questars system, where a prestigious role in the organization can be instantly revoked in the face of divergent political choices.
