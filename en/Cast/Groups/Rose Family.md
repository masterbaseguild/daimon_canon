The Rose family is a high-ranking family [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Gondraw System/Gondraw System|gondrana]], previously allied with the [[en/Cast/Groups/Franky Family|Franky family]].

# History

At the dawn of the conquest of the other two Gondran planets, the royal family needed all the support they could get to maintain their control over the entire system. This support came in the form of vassal families, who swore fealty to the royal family in exchange for prestige and privileges on newly conquered planets. One of the main vassal families was the Rose family, whose name comes from the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Gondraw System/Planet Rose|planet of the same name]], of which this family became the protector.

In turn, to maintain their prestige, the Rose family became friends with the [[en/Cast/Groups/Franky Family|Franky family]], a well-known merchant house. Through a series of arranged marriages, the two families became increasingly amalgamated, although their identities remained firmly distinct throughout.

Decades later, when the resource crisis brought the need to further expand the scope of the Gondrana production machine, the two families embarked on this venture together, partly for profit, partly out of loyalty.

However, among the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Gondraw System/Asteroid Belt|Gondran asteroids]], where the eyes of the royal family did not reach, the presence of the vassals became less and less welcome, and the desire of the Franky family to have complete freedom like the others [[en/Glossary/Trade Houses|trade houses]] became increasingly stronger, until it led to the schism between the two families. Later, the Roses abandoned the asteroids and returned to the planet Rose.
