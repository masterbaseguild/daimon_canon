# Asgard Foundation

The **Asgard Foundation** is the most powerful and influential multi-tasking organization on the planet [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/Planet Aura/Planet Aura|Aura]]. Born as a dissident entity from the **Olympus Foundation**, Asgard was able to capitalize on the political discontent and administrative needs of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Aura United Nations|AUN]] to quickly climb to the top of aural power, transforming itself from a simple opposition movement into a de facto operational arm of the established order.

---

# I. History and Genesis: The Fracture

The genesis of Asgard is marked by the rejection of the authoritarian drift imposed by the Olympus Foundation:

* **The Haude Era:** When [[en/Haude.|Haude]] assumed absolute control of the Olympus Foundation, imposing brutal management methods and a hegemonic vision, a group of the organization's veterans chose to resign in open protest.
* **The Split:** These veterans, bringing with them an intimate knowledge of Olympus' tactics and resources, founded Asgard. The primary objective was to systematically counter Haude's activities and restore a more "integrated" guild model into the AUN system.

---

# II. Rise to Power and Support of the AUN

Asgard's success was rapid due to the convergence of interests with global governance:

* **The Strategic Alliance:** The AUN, frustrated by Olympus' dangerous independence and dark activities, saw Asgard as the ideal tool to balance—and ultimately overthrow—the old guard.
* **Massive Investments:** Thanks to the unlimited flow of resources provided by the AUN, Asgard has managed to achieve a logistics and warfare capacity equal to, if not superior to, that of Olympus in record time.

---

# III. The War between Foundations

With political protection guaranteed, Asgard went on the offensive:

* **Graphic Violence:** The two foundations clashed in a series of violent incidents that rocked the urban and industrial areas of Aura.
* **Judicial Inequality:** The system applied a clear double standard: while every action of Olympus was prosecuted with the utmost rigor by the AUN, Asgard's war maneuvers were ignored or interpreted by the media and authorities as "order maintenance operations" or "preventive defense".
* **The Ban:** The pinnacle of Asgard's strategy was reached with the declaration of **total illegality of the Olympus Foundation**. This act, orchestrated with the complicity of the AUN, formally decreed the decline of Olympus and the investiture of Asgard as a monopolist in the sector.

---

# IV. Operational Status: An Undisputed Power

Today the Asgard Foundation occupies the void left by Olympus:

* **Multi-Activity Monopoly:** Asgard is not limited to security operations; it is active in every crucial sector of Aura, from transportation to mining, managing its network of Questars like a state-legitimized private police force.
* **Forced Legitimacy:** Although the power of Asgard was born from a war between factions, its current position is protected by a veil of legality artfully constructed by AUN propaganda, which presents the foundation as the only possible barrier against the "chaos" represented by the remains of the old Olympus.
