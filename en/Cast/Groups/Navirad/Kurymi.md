# Kurymi

**Kurymi** is a very talented Questor currently working at **MasterBase**. Her career is characterized by a significant transition: born and raised within the ranks of the **Navirad** guild, she found her operational dimension within the MasterBase following a direct invitation from **Winter**.

---

# I. Profile and History

* **From Navirad to MasterBase:** Kurymi's entry into MasterBase was not without hesitation. Initially hesitant about the change of faction and the new power dynamics, he was able to quickly overcome his uncertainties, establishing himself as one of the most promising members of the guild.
* **Undeniable Talent:** Although his arrival was dictated by an external call, his stay was consolidated by merit. Kurymi quickly gained notice for her versatility, earning the respect of MasterBase veterans.

---

# II. Fighting Skills and Style

Kurymi is defined by the trait **"Jack of All Trades"**, which makes her an extremely adaptable Questor in any operational scenario:

* **Weaponry (Master Zweihander):** He wields an imposing *Master Zweihander*, a weapon that requires great strength and mastery, in stark contrast to the agility required for his other duties.
* **Magic (Soul Magic):** She has a specialization in the mystical arts through **Soul Stealer**, a *Soul Magic* technique that allows her to interact with the vital essence of her opponents. This fighting style makes each fight not only a test of physical strength, but a battle for control of one's spirit.
