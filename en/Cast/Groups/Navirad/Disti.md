# Disti

**Disti** is a technical and tactical pillar of the **MasterBase**. A Questor with an engineering background, he was able to combine the mechanical precision of metalworking with the brutal effectiveness of a marksman, cementing his role as one of the most reliable members of the organization.

---

# I. Physical and Distinctive Profile

Disti is an individual whose aesthetic reflects sleepless nights spent designing or repairing gear:
* **Race:** Aural.
* **Distinguishing Features:** Characterized by intense purple hair (*Lower Side Brush*) and marked dark circles - which give him a perpetually tired, almost enigmatic look.
* **Style:** Regularly wears the official MasterBase uniform, maintaining a pragmatic appearance that sacrifices aesthetics in favor of operational functionality.

---

# II. Role and Skills

Disti is not just a fighter, but the beating heart of the MasterBase workshop:

* **Arken Guard Leader:** He holds the prestigious role of leader of the Arken Guard, coordinating defensive strategies and the tactical use of resources.
* **Master of Engineering and Smithing:** His skill as a blacksmith and engineer allows him to maintain and upgrade the Base's arsenal. He is capable of transforming a simple project into an instrument of death or insurmountable defense.
* **Marksman:** In combat, his signature weapon is the **Master Crossbow**, a precision crossbow that he uses with unfailing lethality. His ability to calculate ballistics and mechanical dimensions makes him a superior marksman.

---

# III. Background and Loyalty

* **The SpaceFox Era:** Disti has an important military background as a former member of **SpaceFox**. During the SF-WG War, he served as **Aledge's** right-hand man, earning the respect of his fellow soldiers in the field.
* **Integration into MasterBase:** Its transition to MasterBase occurred directly through the connection with Aledge. Despite his origins being rooted in a now disbanded guild, his dedication to MasterBase is total and unquestioned.
