The Kenny Family was an important dynasty of scientists and researchers, known for attempting to break the taboos of the convergence of technology and divinatory magic. The family unit was brutally exterminated by the Asgard Foundation, leaving a sole survivor as custodian (unbeknownst to her) of their entire scientific legacy.

# Family Members

* **Oliver Kenny:** Head of the family, world-famous scientist of Irish descent. Deceased.
* **Rah'el Kenny:** Bridget's mother, of Jewish origins and former member of a magical-religious sect. Deceased.
* [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Bridget|Bridget]] **(Yosef Kenny):** Proven only daughter and sole survivor of the family massacre.
* **Brothers/Sisters:** Any data regarding other children has been completely lost; there is no trace in the remaining records.

# Biographical Profile and History

### The Meeting and the Flight (The Origins)
The family was born from the union between **Oliver Kenny** and **Rah'el**.
Oliver was an extremely renowned scientist who was feared in the environment due to the specificity and dangerousness of his research, which was also highly coveted by organized crime syndicates. Eager to get away from the spotlight, Oliver decided to temporarily interrupt his studies to dedicate himself to his private life.

It was around this time that he met Rah'el. The woman was on the run from a secret sect (an extremist and restrictive variant of Jehovah's Witnesses) that preached the use of magic through the dogmatic worship of deities. Having renounced the sect in order to live a normal life, Rah'el began to wander as a "cosmopolitan" in search of freedom, meeting Oliver during a national meeting on scientific research.

### The Taboo and the Clandestine Laboratory
Combining their knowledge, the pair set an ambitious new goal: to fuse Oliver's cutting-edge technology with Rah'el's mystical magic to create a vaunted new power to improve people's lives. Soon, however, the two collided with a harsh reality: this union represented a true **universal taboo**, impossible to resolve through the normal human knowledge of the time.

Aware that the world and crime would use false pretenses to get their hands on such dangerous discoveries, Oliver faked his own death and that of his wife. The couple moved in secret to **Italy**, in the **South Regium** region, founding a clandestine underground laboratory to continue their studies away from prying eyes.

### Bridget's Birth and the Massacre (14 Years Later)
In this climate of isolation, Bridget (whose original name is *Yosef Kenny*) was born. Although strongly desired out of love and the desire to have an heir, the little girl was also conceived by her parents with an ulterior motive: to represent the lifeline and the biological key to preserving their research. Bridget grew up in the unknown, convinced that her parents were simple scientists serving the community.

Shortly after Bridget turned 14, things came home to roost. Oliver and Rah'el sensed that their systems had been compromised and that their days were now numbered. Putting a contingency plan into action, they encrypted the entire research inside Bridget's Neural Chip without her knowledge and hired the mercenary Sales with a single task: to protect the little girl and the secret she held at the cost of his life.

A few weeks later, the **Asgard** Foundation raided the clandestine laboratory, barbarically slaughtering Oliver and Rah'el before their daughter's eyes. The goal of the assault was to take over the Kennys' majestic research on behalf of **Xadrium**. Sales' timely intervention saved Bridget from the firing squad, allowing her to escape and leaving her as the sole, unwitting heir to the Kenny dynasty.
