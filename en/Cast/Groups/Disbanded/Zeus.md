# Zeus

**Zeus** was a notable guild within the **Alpha Sphere**, remembered primarily for its role as a loyal ally during the oldest and most brutal conflicts that shaped the region's geopolitical structure.

---

# I. Foundation and Development

* **Shalida's Imprint:** The guild was founded by **Shalida**, a charismatic figure who managed to create an organization of a fair size, capable of carving out a significant space for itself in a competitive landscape such as that of the Alpha Sphere.
* **Profile of the Guild:** Although not an economic giant, Zeus stood out for the cohesion of its members and a solid operational structure, which allowed it to participate in crucial events in the history of the Questors.

---

# II. The Conflict: Early War

The moment of maximum exposure (and the beginning of the end) for Zeus came with the **War of the Dawn** (*SpaceFox vs WorldOrder*):

* **The Choice of Side:** Faithful to its ideals and the alliances made over time, Zeus decided to openly side with **SpaceFox**.
* **War impact:** The conflict proved devastating. The guild suffered significant human losses among its ranks and, even more seriously, its **StarBase** - the beating heart of the guild's activity and logistics - suffered irreparable structural damage during the most heated phases of the fighting.

---

# III. Decline and Dissolution

The end of the war did not bring the hoped-for peace for the members of Zeus:

* **The Unsustainability:** The resources were exhausted and the StarBase, now worn out by the fighting, was no longer able to guarantee the operations necessary to sustain the guild.
* **Epilogue:** Shortly after the end of the conflict, aware of the impossibility of reconstruction, the leadership made the difficult decision to disband the guild, allowing the survivors to seek refuge or new opportunities elsewhere.

---

# IV. Archive note
> *«The Zeus paid the ultimate price for a loyalty that, in hindsight, cost them their entire existence. They fought the Early War not out of lust for power, but out of conviction. When the dust settled and their StarBase stopped responding to signals, it was clear to everyone that the guild was no longer a political force, but just a glorious memory. Shalida led her men to the end, but unfortunately the end came too soon.»* — Chronicles of the Alpha Sphere.

---

# See Operational Status
Deceased. Zeus no longer exists as an entity, but its participation in the War of the Dawn remains a historical point of reference for those who study the alliances and conflicts between ancient guilds.
