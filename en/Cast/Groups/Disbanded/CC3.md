#CC3

**CC3** was one of the most feared and controversial pirate guilds in the **Alpha Sphere**. Defined by a radical ideology and aggressive behavior, the guild has for years represented a constant threat to the established order and the hierarchy of other space organizations.

---

# I. Philosophy and Mission

* **Foundation:** Created by **Stelio**, the CC3 was not a guild dedicated to mere profit, but rather a group with a strong ideological orientation: militant anarchy.
* **Objective:** The ultimate goal of CC3 was radical destabilization. The guild aimed to "free" other organizations from their hierarchical structures, raiding the bases and wreaking havoc to overthrow internal power and convert members to their anarchist vision.

---

# II. Outlaws and Wanted

Due to their destructive *modus operandi*, CC3 quickly earned the enmity of nearly every entity operating in the Alpha Sphere:
* **Blacklist:** The guild became a prime target for numerous government agencies and trade guilds, who saw their incursions as a direct threat to economic and social stability.
* **Arrest Warrants:** Most of the high-profile members — including **Stelio** and **Nick** — were the recipients of active arrest warrants, making their existence one of constant escape.

---

# III. A Cycle of Destruction and Rebirth

CC3 is known for its incredible resilience:
* **Resilience:** The guild has been destroyed, dismantled, and then refounded multiple times throughout its existence, thanks to the tenacity of its members and the decentralized nature of their ideology. However, this continuous state of alert and reconstruction progressively wore down the group's resources and morale.

---

# IV. The Epilogue

The final chapter of CC3 closed with an act of symbolic violence by the authorities:
* **The Fall:** The hunt for the guild members culminated in the capture of **Stelio**. His subsequent **public execution** served as a warning to anyone wishing to continue the anarchist crusade.
* **Dispersion:** Without Stelio's leadership, and under the constant pressure of the bounty hunters who hunted down every survivor, the CC3 finally disintegrated, marking the end of their mission of subversion.

#VI. Operational Status
Deceased. Despite its tenacious nature, the dispersal of the surviving members and the death of the leaders have removed the CC3 from the list of active threats in the Alpha Sphere.
