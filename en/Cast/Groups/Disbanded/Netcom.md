# Netcom

The **Netcom** was an influential and controversial organization of the **Alpha Sphere**, born with the ambitious aim of centralizing and documenting the chaotic history of the local guilds, but quickly transformed into an organ of control and propaganda.

---

# I. The Origins: A Noble Purpose

Founded by **Lee**, Netcom was born at a time when the history of the guilds was entrusted exclusively to oral tradition and word of mouth. The initial goal was to create a central, impartial and accurate archive that would serve as a "historical memory" for the entire Alpha Sphere.

---

# II. Evolution into an Instrument of Power

What started out as a public utility quickly became corrupted:
* **Abuse of Power:** Exploiting its position as the "arbiter of truth", the organization began manipulating information to influence the decisions of other guilds.
* **Propaganda and Defamation:** Netcom responded to any accusations of bias by resorting to disinformation campaigns and personal attacks against anyone who dared to question its version of events.

---

# III. The Information War

When some guilds began creating their own independent stations to counter Netcom's hegemony, the reaction was brutal:
* **Punishment Raids:** Netcom did not limit itself to verbal denial. He organized punitive expeditions against "dissident" guilds, using force to force them into silence or submission.
* **Public Opinion Reaction:** This policy of repression, however, had the opposite effect to that hoped for, uniting the victims in an increasingly widespread network of information resistance.

---

# IV. The Decline and the End

The point of no return was marked by the exponential growth of alternative voices:
* **Loss of Control:** As the prestige and strength of rival guilds grew, Netcom found itself isolated and unable to maintain its information monopoly by force.
* **Dissolution:** Forced to abandon its scare tactics, the organization quickly lost its relevance. Without control over the narrative, the decline was inexorable, leading to the organization's eventual dissolution and Lee's disappearance from the public eye.

---

#VI. Inheritance
The collapse of Netcom left a void in the information system, but it also demonstrated the fundamental importance of freedom of expression in the Alpha Sphere, making future guilds much more cautious about trusting anyone who claims to be the sole holder of "historical truth".
