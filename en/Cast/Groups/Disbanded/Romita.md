# The Romitas

**The Romita** were a peculiar guild in the panorama of the **Alpha Sphere**, standing out for an operational philosophy focused almost exclusively on sporting excellence rather than on the war or political maneuvers that characterized the other guilds.

---

# I. Philosophy and Foundation

* **Endemion's Footprint:** The guild was founded by **Endemion**, who chose to detach himself from the dynamics of power and conflict to create an entity dedicated solely to athletic and competitive competition.
* **Sporting Identity:** Unlike guilds based on mercenaries or engineers, the Romitas established themselves as sports professionals, participating in numerous tournaments and defining very high standards of athletic preparation for the time.

---

# II. Success at MasterBase

The guild's moment of greatest glory coincided with its participation in the league organized by **MasterBase**:
* **Tournament Victory:** The Romitas managed to win the **opening tournament of the fourth league** of the MasterBase. This victory not only cemented their reputation, but confirmed the validity of Endemion's approach to treating the quest life as a form of sporting discipline.

---

# III. The Dissolution

The life of the guild was closely linked to the existence of the sports league in which they competed:
* **The end of the league:** The dissolution of the MasterBase league deprived the Romitas of their main playing field and their social purpose.
* **The dissolution:** Without a competitive context in which to compete, the Romitas did not try to convert themselves into a war or commercial organization. The guild disbanded naturally, with members choosing to follow individual paths, carrying with them the legacy of athletes who had preferred the playing field to the battlefield.

---

# IV. Archive note
> *«There was something refreshing about seeing The Romitas. While everyone else in the Alpha Sphere was busy building crossbows, stealing resources, or plotting behind their backs, they were training to win the next trophy. Endemion didn't want to conquer solar systems, he just wanted to prove that his men were the best in the field. When the league collapsed, they simply vanished. They were not defeated, they just finished their game.”* — Annotation by a neutral observer.

---

# See Operational Status
Deceased. The guild no longer exists, but remains in the historical records of MasterBase as an example of how a guild could successfully operate outside of the Alpha Sphere's usual norms of violence and domination.
