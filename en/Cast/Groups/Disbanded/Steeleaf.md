# Steeleaf

**Steeleaf** was a notable guild originating from the planet **Iphiridia**, known for turning its cultural heritage into a strategic asset before internal betrayal spelled its demise.

---

# I. The Origins and Iphiridian Heritage

* **Foundation:** Born during the turbulent **Iphiridia Campaign** on the initiative of **Niko**, the guild was born from the partnership of a group of young Questars who were looking for a common identity in a hostile territory.
* **The City of Steeleaf:** The group managed to build a city of the same name, which quickly became a vital hub for communications and trade in the northwest quadrant of the planet.

---

# II. Exile and Commercial Conversion

After the disaster that struck Iphiridia, the guild was forced to abandon its earthly headquarters. Despite the loss of territory, Steeleaf members were able to reinvent themselves:
* **The StarBase:** They acquired a *StarBase* dedicated entirely to the conservation and breeding of flora and fauna typical of Iphiridia. This "space botanical garden" became their main source of livelihood and commercial prestige.

---

# III. The Alliance with MasterBase

To ensure its security and finance the development of its StarBase, Steeleaf entered into a strategic agreement with **MasterBase**:
* **Terms of the agreement:** The guild provided rare and biological materials essential for the manufacture of advanced artifacts, receiving in exchange military protection and capital necessary to manage their complex infrastructure.

---

# IV. Betrayal and Fall

Despite external protection, Steeleaf's end came from within:
* **Mattex's Betrayal:** Member **Mattex** acted as an infiltrator for **SpaceHub**, orchestrating sabotage maneuvers that undermined the guild's cohesion from within.
* **The Implosion:** The betrayal was so profound that it caused the total collapse of the structure. The Steeleaf guild, unable to handle the broken bond of trust, quickly disbanded.

---

# V. Epilogue
With the end of the guild, the survivors took different paths. **Niko**, the founder, brought his experience and knowledge of Iphiridian resources directly into **MasterBase**, marking the definitive end of Steeleaf as an independent entity.
