SpaceHub was a powerful and influential [[en/Glossary/Guild|guild]] of the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Alpha Sphere|Alpha Sphere]]. Born from the ashes of the WorldOrder, it was one of the protagonist factions of the Second Master War before finally collapsing due to internal conflicts and civil wars.

# Relevant Members

* [[en/Cast/Blaze|Blaze]] (Founder and Supreme Leader, currently deceased)

---

# Historical Profile

### Foundation and Rise
The guild is founded by Blaze following the dramatic collapse of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/WorldOrder|WorldOrder]]. Welcoming numerous veterans and experienced soldiers from the old faction into its ranks, SpaceHub immediately boasted considerable strategic and military experience. This enormous logistical advantage allows the guild to expand at record rates, quickly controlling a large and hegemonic zone of influence within the Alpha Sphere.

### Friction with the MasterBase
Initially, SpaceHub maintains excellent diplomatic relations with the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Starbases/MasterBase|MasterBase]], even initiating a close military collaboration for the development and coordination of an interstellar anti-pirate league.

Relations, however, deteriorated abruptly when MasterBase leaders leveled heavy accusations at SpaceHub of plagiarism and theft of technological intellectual property. Although SpaceHub firmly proclaims its innocence, the diplomatic damage is now irreparable.

### The Second Master War and the Collapse (2020 - 2022)
The degeneracy of relationships inevitably leads to [[en/Timeline/Master War II|Second War Master]]. The conflict consumed both factions for about two years, until a series of violent conspiracy rumors against the leader Blaze began to circulate within SpaceHub.

The suspicions spark a massive internal revolt that splits the ranks of the guild; due to the devastating structural and logistical damage suffered due to the sedition, SpaceHub is forced to suddenly abandon the conflict, saving itself at the last minute from MasterBase's final counteroffensive.

### The Civil War and the End
In the chaos following his withdrawal from the conflict, Blaze mysteriously vanishes into thin air without leaving a trace. Left without leadership, SpaceHub falls into an abyss of power struggles and ferocious internal civil wars. This spiral of violence will culminate with the total destruction of their main [[en/Glossary/StarBase|starbase]] and the subsequent, definitive dispersion of the few survivors remaining in the sector.

# Military Forces and Arsenal

SpaceHub was renowned for the unique design of its warfare technologies and the effectiveness of its rapid strikes.

### Special Troops: The Raiders
**Raiders** were the guild's assault elite. They were special troops trained to carry out lightning-fast guerrilla attacks aboard light vehicles, sowing panic in enemy lines before retreating.

### Equipment and Means
* **SpaceHub Bow:** The Raiders' service weapon, specifically designed to deliver precise long-range shots using the guild's technology.
* **Ghost Shuttles:** The light aircraft used by the Raiders for assaults and reconnaissance. They featured a floating, cubic design reminiscent of Minecraft's *Ghasts*.
* **Arachna Mecha:** Mechanized heavy assault and ground support units equipped with a multi-legged walking frame.
