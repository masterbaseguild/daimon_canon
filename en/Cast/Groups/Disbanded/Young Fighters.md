# YoungFighters

The **Young Fighters** were a guild operating in the **Alpha Sphere**, characterized by a rapid rise and traumatic fall. Founded on ambitions of totalitarian rule, they marked the landscape of inter-guild conflicts in the period 2018-2019 before being disbanded by the authorities.

---

# I. The Rise (2018)

* **Foundation:** Created by **Loris** in 2018, the guild was born with the declared intent of climbing the heights of power in the Alpha Sphere.
* **Structure:** Under Loris' leadership, the Young Fighters invested heavily in infrastructure, building a solid base of operations and equipping themselves with a defensive and offensive arsenal superior to the average guild of the time.

---

# II. The Strategy of Terror

To consolidate its rule without attracting political attention, the guild adopted a double face:
* **Sabotage and Infiltration:** The guild operated in the shadows, conducting systematic night raids against rival guilds and nearby **StarBases**.
* **The Deception:** Members acted incognito, concealing their identities to maintain a façade of legitimacy while, in fact, they acted as organized pirates.

---

# III. The Clash with the MasterBase

The fall of the illusion began with the birth of the anti-pirate league promoted by **MasterBase**.
* **The Dilemma:** When MasterBase invited the Young Fighters to join the league, Loris found himself faced with an impossible choice: reveal his criminal nature or submit to the control of the Masters.
* **Open War:** The guild's hypocrisy was exposed shortly thereafter. The Masters' response was lightning-fast and uncompromising: an immediate declaration of war that put the Young Fighters on the defensive.

---

# IV. The Collapse: Loris's Experiments

The final blow came when Loris' conduct violated the international treaties of the **AUN (Aura United Nations)**:
* **Technology Violations:** To close the technological gap with his adversaries, Loris undertook a series of illegal experiments in robotics, a practice strictly prohibited by UN Aural regulations.
* **The Final Raid:** The AUN's issuing of an arrest warrant turned the guild into a legitimate tier 1 target. During a massive raid, AUN forces neutralized the facility.
* **Epilogue:** **Loris** was captured and executed on the spot. Without their charismatic leader and with the guild under siege, the remaining members surrendered, marking the final dissolution of the Young Fighters.
