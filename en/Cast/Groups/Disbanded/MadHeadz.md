# MadHeadz

The **MadHeadz** were a versatile and daring guild of the **Alpha Sphere**, known for their ability to excel in diametrically opposed fields: from mercenary ferocity to precision war manufacturing, to athletic competition.

---

# I. Identity and Ascension

* **Foundation:** Created by **Donny**, the guild quickly distinguished itself with a multi-faceted portfolio of skills.
* **Specializations:**
* **Mercenary:** Performing high-risk missions.
* **Armaments:** Specialized development and production of long-range weapons.
* **Sports:** Active participation in Alpha Sphere tournaments.
* **Expansion:** Thanks to Donny's energy and the support of figures like **Neiro**, MadHeadz became one of the most visible acts, constantly attracting new members and ambitious guests.

---

# II. The Conflict with the MasterBase

The guild entered **MasterBase**'s orbit as an ally, but the partnership was put to the test by a serious incident of conduct:
* **The Scandal:** Donny illicitly stole proprietary projects from **Hooneex** ([[en/Cast/Groups/Keyplexity/Keyplexity|Keyplexity]]), creating a deep rift between the organizations.
* **Degeneration:** Diplomatic relations rapidly deteriorated, resulting in skirmishes that threatened the stability of the region.
* **Reconciliation:** The diplomacy was successful: an agreement was reached that involved the official sharing of the stolen intellectual property and the payment of a substantial sum of money to Hooneex as compensation. The guilds, having overcome the obstacle, returned to collaborate successfully in various missions.

---

# III. Decline and Disappearance

The MadHeadz parable did not have a tragic ending in war, but a closure shrouded in mystery:
* **Donny's Eclipse:** The guild gradually lost momentum. The definitive breaking point was represented by the mysterious disappearance of the leader, Donny.
* **Dissolution:** Without its central figure, the guild was unable to regenerate and was officially disbanded.
* **The Legacy:** Today, their proprietary StarBase still floats in the void of the Alpha Sphere, a technically functional but ghostly facility now mostly deserted.

---
