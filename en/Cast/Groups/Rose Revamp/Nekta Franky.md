# NektaFranky

**Nekta Franky** is a multifaceted figure from the Alpha Sphere, known for having been able to combine belonging to one of the most influential families in the region, the **Franky Family**, with a nomadic entrepreneurial vocation that led him to found **Rose Revamp**.

---

## I. The Wandering Merchant
* **The Choice of Independence:** Despite being a member of the Franky Family, Nekta used the cover of his business activity to avoid being involved in the more violent and bloody dynamics that his family legacy would inevitably demand.
* **The Rose Revamp:** For years, Nekta has led a life of solitary exploration aboard her shop shuttle, the *Rose Revamp*. This was not just a base of operations, but a traveling market that allowed him to weave a unique network of contacts with guilds of all orientations, becoming a respected seller throughout the Alpha Sphere.

## II. The Return and the Alliance
* **Reunion with Garo and Emily:** Nekta's fate has once again become intertwined with that of her companions following their escape from **Gondraw**. The meeting marked the end of his solitary wanderings.
* **The Entrance to the MasterBase:** Accepting Garo and Emily's invitation, Nekta decided to bring her experience and resources into the **MasterBase**, transforming her individual mission into a collective project.

## III. Current Status: FrankyRose
* **Shared Management:** Today, *Rose Revamp* is no longer an individual reality, but the fulcrum of the activity of **FrankyRose**, a group that manages trade and exchanges in synergy.
* **Operation:** The shuttle makes regular stops at MasterBase, serving as a vital logistical and commercial hub for the guild, seamlessly integrating Nekta's mercantile spirit into the base system.
