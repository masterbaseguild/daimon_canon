# Rose Revamp

**Rose Revamp** is an independent business originating from the **Gondraw System**, known throughout the Alpha Sphere for the artisanal quality of its products and the distinctive style that characterizes each of its creations.

---

## I. Origins and Identity
* **Foundation:** Created by **Nekta Franky**, Rose Revamp began as a nomadic project aboard her own space shuttle, transforming the concept of a "shop" into a traveling reality that challenges the boundaries of star systems.
* **Specialization:** The business focuses on the sale of **custom weapons and equipment**. The trademark of the Rose Revamp is not only its war effectiveness, but a refined aesthetic and a unique style that makes each piece an object sought after by mercenaries and collectors.

## II. Structure and Members
Although born as a solitary enterprise, Rose Revamp has evolved into a close-knit team managing the commercial and technical operations:
* **Nekta Franky:** Founder and beating heart of nomadic logistics.
* **Mia:** Key member, contributes to the development and management of operations.
* **Peridot:** An integral part of the staff, essential to the growth and reputation of the brand.

## III. Operational Evolution
* **Gondran Independence:** Despite its original link with the Gondraw System, the Rose Revamp has maintained a strict independence, managing to navigate the political and military disputes of the Alpha Sphere by offering its services to anyone who can afford them, without ever becoming a political faction.
* **Quality Standards:** "Revamp's" attention to detail has allowed the business to survive in a market saturated with mass-produced weapons, transforming the traveling shop into a prestigious point of reference for every high-level Questor.
