#Hooneex

**Hooneex** is the founder of the **Keyplexity** guild and a major figure in the aural landscape. His trajectory — which began as a member of **MasterBase** and continued with the creation of his own guild — has made him a strategic point of reference for organizations operating in the shadows.

---

# I. Capabilities and Combat

Hooneex is not a leader who gives orders from behind; it is a force of nature that dominates the battlefield through its unique physiology:

* **Shapeshifter:** His main ability lies in the ability to alter his own shape. In combat, he transforms into a **gigantic faun**, a configuration that maximizes his already impressive size.
* **Fighting Style:** As a faunus, Hooneex harnesses a brutal mix of **devastating physical strength** and surprising **dexterity** for his size. He is a frontline fighter capable of breaking through enemy defensive lines and managing close quarters combat against multiple opponents or armored threats.

---

# II. Background

* **Origins at MasterBase:** Before founding Keyplexity, Hooneex was part of MasterBase. This training experience provided him with the tactical skills and in-depth knowledge of the Questor mechanisms that he today applies to the leadership of his guild.
* **Founder of Keyplexity:** After his separation from MasterBase, he gave life to Keyplexity, transforming it into a reality capable of supporting its allies - in particular the ex-SpaceFox and Aledge - in moments of the darkest crisis, as demonstrated during the post-MasterCon reorganization.

# IV. Operational Profile
* **Role:** Leader of Keyplexity, Assault Fighter.
* **Specialization:** Transformation into faun form, frontline combat, tactical resource management.
* **Status:** Operational, focused on maintaining the secrecy of their guild and providing strategic support to clandestine partners of the Olympus Foundation and MasterBase.
