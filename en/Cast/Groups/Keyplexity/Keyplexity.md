# Keyplexity

**Keyplexity** is a premier aural guild, known for its ability to act as a "stabilizing force" and strategic logistical partner in the darkest corners of the planet's politics [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/Planet Aura/Planet Aura|Aura]].

---

# I. History and Evolution

* **The Origins:** Founded by [[en/Cast/Groups/Keyplexity/Hooneex|Hooneex]], the guild has experienced rapid expansion, quickly climbing the ranks thanks to excellent resource management.
* **The Nexus Campaign (2017):** Keyplexity consolidated its reputation by actively participating in the historic *Nexus Campaign*, an event that thrust the guild into the spotlight of the aural landscape.
* **The Bond with SpaceFox:** Historic allies of **SpaceFox**, the members of Keyplexity have maintained an unwavering loyalty to their old comrades even after the guild's dissolution. This relationship has remained strong over time, facilitating Keyplexity's support of [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Aledge|Aledge]] on its path to **MasterBase**.

---

# II. The Role of Salvation (2019)

The most critical intervention in Keyplexity's recent history came in 2019, in the aftermath of the **MasterCon Campaign** disaster. At a time when the MasterBase was close to total collapse, Keyplexity intervened by providing the resources, support and coverage necessary to allow the base to reorganize and survive, effectively transforming itself into its pillar of support.

---

# III. Current Situation and Clandestine

Today, Keyplexity operates with extreme caution:

* **Low Profile:** Due to its close partnerships with **MasterBase** and the **Olympus Foundation** — entities both labeled outlawed by Aura authorities — the guild has adopted a policy of total discretion.
* **Strategy:** Keyplexity acts as a "shadow" for its allies, preventing diplomatic and operational ties to banned organizations from being discovered by government forces or the **Asgard Foundation**.
