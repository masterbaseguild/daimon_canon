# Xadrium Corporation

The **Xadrium Corporation** is the most powerful and dangerous shadow entity operating within the **Alpha Sphere**. Officially non-existent for almost the entire galactic population, the Xadrium acts as a systemic puppet master, orchestrating the fate of entire civilizations to pursue a single, chilling goal: the total elimination of free will and the establishment of a collectivist utopia under an artificial *hivemind*.

---

# I. The Shadow in the Alpha Sphere

* **Unknown Headquarters:** Although its influence is widespread, Xadrium's headquarters remains an absolute mystery. This invisibility strategy allows it to operate without ever suffering diplomatic or military repercussions.
* **Architects of Disaster:** Documented speculation links the Corporation to key events, including the **Iphiridia Campaign** and the rise of the **Asgard Foundation**. It is widely believed that the chaos unleashed by such events was intended to destabilize local governments to make them more susceptible to Xadrium control.

---

# II. Modus Operandi: "The Pact"

Xadrium expands through corrupt diplomacy. He proposes to the dominant (or emerging) factions of each planet a contract known simply as **"The Pact"**:
* **The Proposal:** The Xadrium provides the military, technological, or political support needed to gain or maintain absolute power.
* **The Condition:** In exchange, the recipient of the Pact agrees to provide the Corporation with every necessary resource, effectively making the planet an extractive outpost under the occult control of the Xadrium.

---

# III. Domain Projects

The Corporation is dedicated to various technological and biological programs aimed at bending the will of others:

* **Mecha Project:** Transforms the *Masters* into bio-mechanical beings deprived of will, reduced to "flesh and blood mechas" piloted by special units of the Xadrium.
* **Project Zombie:** An airborne virus designed to eliminate physical resistance. The victims, after death, are resuscitated with predatory instincts. The virus is extremely versatile, as the behavior of "victims" can be manipulated in real time via inhalable pheromones.
* **Project Plague:** The final initiative, which seeks a neurological restructuring of the galactic population to permanently eliminate free will.

---

# IV. Hierarchy and the Artificial Ethereal

At the top of the pyramid are the **Supreme Commanders**, a small number of individuals - almost all Oracles equipped with Daimons - who orchestrate the creation of an **Artificial Ethereal**.

* **The Hivemind:** This Ethereal serves as the core for the hive mind that the Xadrium is trying to impose on the universe.
* **The Internal Rebellion:** Paradoxically, the Ethereal possesses a form of self-awareness and constantly "dreams" of the day when it will be able to free itself from its creators.
* **The Destiny:** Commanders serve as high-level tactical bosses. It is expected that one of them may side - out of necessity or defeat - with the **MasterBase**, while the ultimate fate of the remaining Commanders is to be absorbed or subjugated by their own Ethereal, who will become the final antagonist of the saga.

# V. Narrative Notes
The secretive nature of Xadrium makes fighting it a game of mirrors. Any victory achieved against a manipulated local government may, in fact, be part of the Corporation's plan to "prune" factions that were not effective enough in serving their ultimate goals.
