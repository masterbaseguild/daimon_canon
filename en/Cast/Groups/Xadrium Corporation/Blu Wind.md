Blu Wind is a [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] and elite agent of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Xadrium Corporation/Xadrium Corporation|Xadrium Corporation]]. In the past he worked for a period within the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]] with the official cover of external observer of the corporation.

# Narrative Profile and Biography

### The Infiltration Mission
Under the direct control of Xadrium's top management, Blu Wind is tasked with acting as an industrial and political spy. He is introduced aboard the MasterBase by **Black Paladin** along with **Beat** (Bitto).

The primary objective of his undercover mission is to launch a dense network of secret investigations into the Masters: the Xadrium management suspects that MasterBase is plotting in the shadows to attack or destabilize the corporation, and Blu Wind has the task of finding concrete evidence to support this thesis.

### The Misdirection and the Return
During his stay on board, Blu Wind's intelligence activities attract the attention and distrust of the guild's higher-ups. The Questor **Beat** manages to intervene promptly, orchestrating a complex misdirection operation against the agent. Finding himself faced with false leads and dead ends, and unable to collect further useful elements, Blu Wind decides to interrupt the mission and return to the Xadrium headquarters, where he still continues to operate steadily as a field agent.

---

# Competitive Career and Tournaments

### Act of Rising DeathMatch
Despite his nature as an infiltrator and his espionage duties, Blu Wind took an official part in the **Act of Rising DeathMatch** (known in the first editions as *Rising Masters*), one of the very first and most famous tournaments organized by MasterBase. Demonstrating top-level warfare capabilities, the Xadrium agent managed to rout all the opposing Questors, **winning the final victory of the competition**.
