# Aural United Nations (AUN)

The **Aural United Nations (AUN)** represent the highest political, administrative and economic coordination body of the people [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/Planet Aura/Planet Aura|aural]]. Born in the 2010s of the 21st century as a direct evolution of the ancient terrestrial United Nations, the AUN is the body that ferried aural humanity from planetary isolation to the colonial conquest of the entire Talos System.

---

# I. History and Foundation

The birth of the AUN is inextricably linked to the **Space Revolution**:

* **The Unification of the Species:** With the development of the first warp drives and the beginning of space exploration, the distinction between aural nations blurred in favor of a collective identity. The concept of "one people" has become the foundation of the new global alliance.
* **The Protection of the Talos System:** Assuming the role of enforcer, the AUN has extended its sovereignty far beyond the borders of Aura, colonizing semi-habitable planets and satellites to relieve population pressure on the home planet.
* **The AUN Network:** To manage this immense commercial empire, the administration promoted the creation of the *AUN Network*, a communications and exchange infrastructure that connects every inhabited node in the system.

---

# II. The "Wild West" and the Rise of the Guilds

Rapid and uncontrolled expansion has created an unprecedented governance crisis:

* **The Power Void:** As AUN rule has spread across all of Talos' planets, central control has weakened. The system has slipped into a state of institutional anarchy, often defined as the spatial "Wild West".
* **Birth of the Questor:** In this context of uncertainty, the figure of the **Queller** emerged. The Guilds were initially formed to make up for the shortcomings of the AUN, offering protection, logistics and operational strength where the State could not reach.

---

# III. Laissez-faire Politics and Media Narrative

On Aura, the management of the Guilds has taken on particular contours:

* **Government and Guilds:** National governments, affiliated with the AUN, have adopted a **laissez-faire** policy. Instead of attempting a direct confrontation, often counterproductive, they chose to integrate the Guilds into the social fabric.
* **Myth Building:** The AUN funds a massive media cover-up operation. The Guilds are presented to public opinion as "vigilantes at the service of justice". Any excess is justified or downplayed, while government task forces act covertly to moderate the most extreme or dangerous factions.

---

# IV. External Geopolitics: The Tricorn

The AUN's power projection is not isolated:

* **The Tricorn Coalition:** Recognizing that industry stability depends on cross-system cooperation, the AUN has formed a tripartite alliance with the governing bodies of the **Sophia** and **Turma** systems. While this coalition is still in its infancy, it aims to standardize trade routes and contain the centrifugal forces of large corporations and independent guilds.

---

# V. Status Quo: A Fragile Equilibrium

Currently, the AUN finds itself in an extremely complex position:

* **Executive Power vs. Real Power:** While the AUN maintains formal administration, the corporations dictate economic agendas and the Guilds of Questor dictate de facto security.
* **Corporate Conflict:** Corporations constantly clash on economic and military fronts, often using the Guilds as pawns in power battles.
