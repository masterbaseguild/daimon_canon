The Lignum Nobili are a [[en/Glossary/Guild|guild]] of the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Alpha Sphere|Alpha Sphere]], owner and custodian by [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Starbases/Lignum Nobili|starbase of the same name]]. Originally born as a mercantile and mercenary faction, it subsequently evolved into one of the most important sporting realities in the sphere.

# Guild Profile and Activities

### Economy and Manufacturing
The guild has a unique and diverse livelihood model:
* **Entertainment:** The main commercial and economic activity of the starbase consists of a famous and massive arcade service offered to the population of the sector.
* **Military Manufacturing:** The guild is renowned throughout the sphere for the excellent craftsmanship of long-range weapons, of which the members themselves are masters in the use.
* **Mercenary Tasks:** To supplement the arcade's profits, the Lignum Nobili carry out regular mercenary operations and contract hunting for others.

### The Sports Turning Point
Between 2023 and 2024, founder Dry officially retired from the position of supreme leader, appointing Keia as his successor. Under Keia's new government, the guild has redefined its identity, progressively concentrating its efforts and resources on competitive and athletic activities, to the point of establishing itself as one of the most important sports guilds in the entire Alpha Sphere.

# History and Conflicts

### Foundation and Early Years
The guild was founded by Dry and, thanks to the attractiveness of its activities and its starbase, managed to quickly gather a vast multitude of members in its space sector.

### Hospitality at Entity and the Alliance (2022)
During 2022, Lignum Nobili's starbase becomes the refuge of [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Entity|Entity]]. His long stay on board the station is actually part of his **voluntary exile**, triggered following the coup plotted by Beat within the MasterBase. The hospitality and support offered to Entity in this moment of vulnerability lays the foundation for a solid and lasting military and political collaboration between the two guilds.

### The Third War Master
Thanks to the alliance signed with Entity, Lignum Nobili officially take to the field taking part in the [[en/Timeline/Master War III|Third War Master]]. Using their deadly mastery of long-range weapons, guild members will play a critical and strategic role in repelling the [[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/Crimson Sphere|Crimson Sphere]] incursion forces, saving the sector from invasion.
