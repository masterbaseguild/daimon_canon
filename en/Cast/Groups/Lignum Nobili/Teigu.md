#Teigu

**Teigu** is the charismatic leader of **Lignum Nobili**. An athlete of exceptional value, he leads his faction with a mix of physical rigor and strategic dedication, maintaining a precarious balance within one of the most cohesive realities of the Questor panorama.

---

# I. Identity and Style

Teigu stands out for its iconic presence, capable of combining elegance with operational efficiency:

* **Distinctive Outfit:** His clothing is in perfect harmony ("complementary outfit") with that of **Dry**. Unlike the latter, Teigu prefers an immaculate white shirt, completed with a simple vest that underlines his athletic posture and decisive attitude.
* **Details that matter:** He wears earrings in the unmistakable shape of **kunai**, a subtle reminder of his nature as a fighter ready for rapid action.
* **The Jade Ring:** He wears a finely crafted jade ring, a gift from Dry. Although apparently only a ceremonial ornament, the ring hides an **anti mind-control** protection charm. This secret is jealously guarded: no one, except the donor, is aware of the protective nature of the object.

---

# II. Role in the Lignum Nobili

* **Leadership:** As a leader, Teigu is not a distant ruler. Her nature as an athlete leads her to always be on the front lines, leading training sessions and missions with a discipline that inspires the members of the guild.
* **Vision:** His leadership is characterized by loyalty towards the members of the guild and the ability to read critical situations with the clarity of someone accustomed to managing extreme physical effort.

---

---

# IV. Operational Status
Teigu continues to lead the Lignum Nobili, moving with the knowledge that her role requires absolute mental stability—a need that the enchantment of the jade ring silently satisfies, protecting her from any external manipulation that could undermine her authority and the hold of the guild.
