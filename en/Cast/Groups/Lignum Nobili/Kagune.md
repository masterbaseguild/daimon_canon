# Kagune

**Kagune** is a high-profile Questor within the **Lignum Nobili**. An athlete with an unmistakable stage presence, he combines the physical discipline typical of his faction with an aesthetic that makes him immediately recognizable on the battlefield.

---

# I. Identity and Appearance

Kagune's image is taken care of down to the smallest details, reflecting a personality that leaves nothing to chance:

* **Long Overcoat:** He wears a long overcoat that emphasizes his slender figure during movement, while ensuring adequate protection and an aesthetic that recalls the formal rigor of the Lignum Nobili.
* **Blue contact lenses:** His eyes, covered by intense blue lenses, give him a penetrating and almost artificial gaze, creating a stark contrast with his surroundings and adding a touch of mystery to his expression.

---

# II. Role and Capabilities

As a guild athlete, Kagune has refined her body as if it were a precision weapon:

* **Mobility:** His fighting style takes advantage of the freedom of movement afforded by his coat, which he cleverly uses to hide trajectories or distract opponents before a lunge.
* **Specialization:** He is not just an athlete, but a professional who integrates athletic physical preparation with infiltration and rapid combat techniques. Its presence in the Lignum Nobili is synonymous with reactivity and rhythm control.

# IV. Operational Status
Kagune remains one of the most reliable athletes of the group. His dedication to the guild is total, and his unique style continues to serve as an example for new recruits, perfectly embodying the ideal of aesthetic and physical perfection pursued by the Lignum Nobili.
