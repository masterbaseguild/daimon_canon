Tyrener is a [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Olympus Foundation/Olympus Foundation|Olympus Foundation]], known for his reclusive nature and his deep knowledge of the geo-political dynamics of the Alpha Sphere.

* **Gender:** Male
* **Race:** Human
* **Body & Attire:** To be defined.

# Narrative Profile and Alignment

### Personality
* **Critical and Demanding:** Tyrener has a severe, analytical and unfiltered character. He has no scruples about making harsh constructive criticism and is never satisfied with partial results, always demanding the most from himself and those around him.

### Story Arcs and Biography
* **The Wandering Gladiator:** Tyrener is an expert gladiator and traveling warrior who constantly travels across sectors. Despite his official affiliation with Olympus, he maintains a very independent status which leads him to periodically orbit other factions.
* **Visits to MasterBase:** Tyrener sometimes visits [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]]. Using his maturity and vision, he constantly urges the Masters team to never let their guard down and to always push themselves beyond their limits. This severity is driven by a very specific awareness: Tyrener is one of the few to fully understand the enormous and silent planetary danger constituted by the expansion of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Xadrium Corporation/Xadrium Corporation|Xadrium Corporation]], and encourages the guild to be ready for a possible and inevitable clash.

# Fighting Style and Skills

* **Gladiator Style:** Although his specific techniques (**Skills**) and his arsenal (**Weapons**) are yet to be defined in detail, his background makes him an undisputed master of arena combat, with great physical resistance and excellent one-on-one skills.
