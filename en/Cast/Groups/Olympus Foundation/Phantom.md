#Phantom

**Phantom** is the youngest and least experienced member of the **5 Magisters of the Olympus Foundation**. Despite his age and a shyness that often leads him to remain on the sidelines, he is considered by the Foundation's leaders as one of the rawest and potentially lethal talents of the entire organization.

---

# I. Profile and Personality

Phantom is an anomaly in the context of the Magister, a group usually composed of authoritarian leaders or conflict-hardened veterans:

* **Innate shyness:** His reserved character and little inclination to worldly or political life often makes him invisible during the Magister's councils. Many underestimate him due to this introverted attitude.
* **Incredible Talent:** Beneath the shy surface lies an extraordinary operational potential. His speed of learning and his ability to adapt in complex tactical scenarios make him an individual that the Foundation - and in particular Haude - is banking on for the future.
* **Geopolitical Role:** He is responsible for the sector including **America** and **Oceania**, a vast area that constantly puts him to the test on both the war and administrative fronts, despite his evident inexperience.

---

# II. Operational Skills

* **Race (Elf):** His elven nature gives him a sensory perception and natural grace that translate into fluid movement and pinpoint precision in actions, almost as if he anticipates the movements of his opponents.
* **Fighting Style:** [Data pending definition based on concepts]. His skill is focused on speed and stealth; Phantom tends to favor attacks that end before the target even realizes they are under threat.
* **Potential:** His rise through the Magister ranks has been meteoric. It is the classic example of a "prodigy" that Olympus has chosen to grow and shape, hoping that one day it can become the jewel in the crown of the Foundation.

# IV. Current Status
Phantom continues to navigate the turbulent waters of the Olympus Foundation. Being the youngest of the quintet, he is often the object of protection (or manipulation) by the other Magisters. Although he does not yet have a defined narrative arc, his position in a strategic sector such as the America/Oceania area and his status as a "precocious talent" make him a fundamental character for any future evolution of the plot, especially now that Olympus has gone underground.
