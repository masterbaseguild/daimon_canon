# I. Specialization: Razorbolt Pilot

The core of this Quark Squad member's operation lies in his command ability over **Razorbolts**:

* **Flight Mastery:** She is not simply a pilot, she is an extension of the aircraft. Her ability to fly these craft at high speed and precision — often in prohibitive combat conditions — makes her the lead asset for the team's escort, rapid reconnaissance and surgical strike missions.
* **Technical Symbiosis:** Due to his Cherub nature, his neural interface with onboard systems is extremely deep. The Razorbolts, under his command, seem to move with an almost organic fluidity, as if they were animated by their own intention rather than by digital inputs.
