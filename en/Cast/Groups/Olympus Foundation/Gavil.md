# Narrative Profile and Alignment

### Role and Faction
Gavil is an official member of the Olympus Foundation, for which he carries out operational and field supervisory duties in the Alpha Sphere.

### Story Arcs and Biography
* **The Theft and the Meeting with Theo (2016):** In 2016, Gavil suffered a sensational theft of hi-tech war material owned by Olympus by [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Theo|Theo]], a twelve-year-old boy at the time. Those same stolen weapons will be used by Theo and his companions to found the vigilante guild [[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/Anark|Anark]].
* **Patronage and Forgiveness (2017):** Despite the affront suffered, Gavil crosses paths with the Anark again in 2017, taking part in a violent three-way skirmish that also involves the Asgard Foundation. Impressed by the skills and war potential demonstrated by the young vigilantes led by Theo and Entity, Gavil decides not to proceed against them for the old theft. Instead, he chooses to forgive Theo and officially proposes to the Anark to come under the wing and sponsorship program of Olympus, starting their professional training.
