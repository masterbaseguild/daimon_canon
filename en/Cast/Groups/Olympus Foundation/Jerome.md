#Jerome

**Jerome** is an operator whose career is defined by constant institutional ambiguity. Currently classified as an active member of the **Olympus Foundation**, Jerome has a peculiar past as an undercover observer at **MasterBase**, a position that makes him a living bridge between two of the most influential and opposing realities in the sector.

---

# I. Origins and Background

* **Provenance:** Born and raised in **Frankfurt**, Jerome brings with him a legacy of methodological rigor that he has been able to adapt perfectly to the discipline of the Olympus Foundation.
* **Tactical Infiltration:** His stay in the MasterBase was not dictated by a career choice, but by an official mandate from the Olympus Foundation. As an observer, Jerome was tasked with an inside look at MasterBase's procedures, logistics and operational culture.

---

# II. Operation MasterCon

The turning point in Jerome's biography coincided with the turbulent **Operation MasterCon**:

* **Recruitment:** Following the resolution of the MasterCon conflict, Jerome's position has changed dramatically. In a reversal of roles that raised many doubts about his actual loyalty, he was officially recruited into the ranks of MasterBase.
* **Dual Loyalty:** To date, his official position remains that of a Questor for the Olympus Foundation who has extended his reach to MasterBase. This "dual membership" places him in a gray area: he is seen by his comrades as a valuable resource, but remains constantly under observation by the internal intelligence of both organizations.

---

# III. Operational Profile

* **Specialization:** Jerome excels at data collection and analysis of adversary systems. His ability to observe an organization's weaknesses from the inside — a skill honed during his periods of "double-dealing" — makes him a first-rate tactical asset.
* **Approach:** He is not a front-line fighter, but an analyst who prefers to resolve conflicts by understanding the variables at play. His Teutonic coldness allows him to maintain the detachment necessary to evaluate the most critical situations without being influenced by the emotional dynamics typical of the battlefield.

> ### ── Archive Note ──
> *«Jerome observed MasterBase on behalf of Olympus, and now he looks at Olympus through the eyes of someone who lived among the ranks of MasterBase. He is a man who knows both sides of the glass too well. Don't ask him which side he's on; ask yourself what information he is trying to protect — or sell — at this very moment.”* — Unclassified security report.

---

# IV. Current Status
Jerome operates today as a bridge, or perhaps a spy, depending on your point of view. His presence is tolerated by MasterBase thanks to the usefulness of the information he brings with him, but the fact that he remains an exponent of the Olympus Foundation makes each of his missions a delicate exercise in diplomatic balancing.
