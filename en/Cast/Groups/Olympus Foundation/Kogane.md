# Kogane

**Kogane** is an elite operator of the **Olympus Foundation**, integrated into **Team Quark**. Belonging to the **Leonine** lineage, her presence on the field combines the natural ferocity of felines with the age-old discipline of the ninja arts, making her an infiltrator of rare effectiveness.

---

# I. Identity and Specialization

* **Race (Leonine):** Her leonine nature gives her prodigious agility and superior sensory perception, which integrates perfectly with ninja cloaking techniques.
* **Tactical Role:** Within Squad Quark, Kogane acts as a disruptor. Its specialization is not frontal brute force, but the rapid and silent elimination of critical targets or surgical sabotage of defense systems.
* **"Ninja" style:** His clothing and fighting style recall the ancient traditions of infiltration. Kogane is a master at moving in tight spaces, taking advantage of shadows and her ability to strike and disappear before her opponent can react.

---

# II. Profile under development

Given the recent nature of his appointment to Team Quark, many details are still being worked out:

* **Personality:** An introspective character is hypothesized, almost feline in his way of observing the surrounding environment. His loyalty to Olympus is unquestioned, but his detachment from the Magisters' internal politics suggests a dedication focused solely on mission execution.
* **Narrative Arcs:** His path is a blank page. The challenge will be to see how his ninja code of honor clashes with the Machiavellian and often unscrupulous nature of the Olympus Foundation.

However, there is a loneliness in his eyes that suggests that, one day, the weight of what he does may outweigh his loyalty to his superiors.”* —Olympus Intelligence Division observation.

---

# IV. Operational Status
Currently operating in the Foundation's hottest scenarios, Kogane remains a key asset of Team Quark. His evolution will largely depend on how his rigid personal code is tested by the growing tensions between the Olympus Foundation and the rest of the system.
