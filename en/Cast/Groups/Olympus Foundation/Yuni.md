# Yuni

**Yuni** is a specialized member of the **Quark Squad** within the **Olympus Foundation**. Belonging to the rare lineage of **Driad**, Yuni brings a unique biological and dynamic perspective to the harsh technological environment of the Foundation, which she creatively exploits through her vast arsenal of technological devices.

---

# I. Identity and Specialization

* **Race (Driad):** Being a Dryad gives Yuni a peculiar connection to the environments in which she operates. Despite being a Foundation Questor, he possesses an ecological and structural sensitivity that allows him to "feel" vibrations and alterations in systems—whether natural or artificial—with a precision that borders on intuition.
* **Tactical Role:** Yuni is the team's **gadget** expert. Where other members of Quark Squad rely on strength or piloting, Yuni relies on ingenuity. He is responsible for manipulating battlefields, disabling complex security, and creating technological diversions that make Team Quark virtually impossible to contain.

---

# II. Operational Profile

* **Gadgetry:** His equipment is not standardized. Yuni prefers to create solutions "on the fly" based on the tactical situation. His collection of gadgets includes miniaturized sabotage tools, jamming frequency emitters, and surveillance devices that seem to blend seamlessly into the surrounding architecture.
* **Personality (Being defined):** He emerges as a curious individual, perhaps a little eccentric. Unlike his more disciplined companions, Yuni sees missions as complex puzzles to be solved, rather than simple orders for execution. This nature makes him less inclined to the rigid hierarchy of the Foundation, although he remains loyal to his team.

---

# III. Archive Note (Hangar Technical Report)

It's why Team Quark is still standing: Yuni finds the keystone where we only see a wall."* — Olympus Foundation Analyst.
