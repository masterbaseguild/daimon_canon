# Raal

**Raal** is one of the **5 Magisters of the Olympus Foundation**, in charge of supervising the **America/Oceania** sector. Belonging to the **Cherub** lineage, her presence is a constant balance between a caring, almost maternal nature, and an unshakable pride that makes her a leader respected and feared by her subordinates.

---

# I. The Burden of Love and Manipulation

Raal's story is defined by his visceral bond with the Foundation's leader, **Haude**:

* **The Obsession:** Raal is madly in love with Haude. This feeling is not only romantic, but a pillar of her identity: she has placed every ideal of justice and order in him.
* **The Manipulation:** Haude, knowing Raal's absolute devotion, has skillfully manipulated her *caring* side to make her the executing arm of the Foundation's most ruthless policies, always justifying them as "necessary sacrifices for the greater good".

---

# II. The Fall and the Rebirth

The events leading to the Olympus crisis mark the collapse of Raal's entire world:

* **The Trauma:** When MasterBase exposes Haude's true Machiavellian nature and announces his presumed death, Raal falls into a deep existential crisis. Deprived of her gravitational center, she attempts to take her own life, unable to reconcile the image of her beloved with the reality of her crimes.
* **The Rescue:** It is thanks to the intervention of her teammates - who see in her not only a Magister, but a human resource to be saved - that Raal is brought back from the edge of the abyss.
* **The New Leader:** After a long journey of grieving and accepting the truth, Raal finds a new purpose: leading the remnants of the Olympus Foundation. Under his leadership, the organization transforms into an active **Resistance** against the hegemony of the **Asgard Foundation**.

---

# III. Haude's Return

Destiny reserves a final challenge for Raal:

* **The Inner Conflict:** Haude's unexpected return destabilizes the foundations of his new life. The temptation to return to the shadow of his old leader is strong, but Raal faces this confusion with a maturity he didn't possess before.
* **The Choice:** Raal understands that the blind devotion of the past was a form of captivity. He consciously chooses to move forward, putting the good of the Resistance and his own autonomy above his past feelings. It is the definitive transition from "devotee" to "leader".

---

# IV. Operational Profile
* **Race (Cherub):** Her nature gives her a grace and power of flight that she uses to dominate the battlefield, making her presence a sign of hope for allies and terror for Asgard troops.
* **Personality:** Proud and protective of her people, she has gone from being a tyrant's pawn to becoming the figurehead for those seeking redemption.
