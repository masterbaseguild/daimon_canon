# Olympus Foundation

The **Olympus Foundation** has been the most influential and powerful multi-tasking organization on the planet [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/Planet Aura/Planet Aura|Aura]] for years. Born as an economic giant during the expansion into the Talos System, the Foundation redefined the concept of "corporate power", transforming itself into a complex paramilitary structure that shaped the culture of the [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]].

---

# I. History and Evolution

* **From Resources to Power:** Initially a corporation dedicated to exploiting the resources of the Talos system, Olympus soon realized that market control came through the protection of trade flows. Instead of outsourcing security, the Foundation decided to internalize the war power, creating a proprietary training system for Questar that became the gold standard of the era.
* **The Haude Era:** In 2012, the rise of [[en/Cast/Groups/Olympus Foundation/Haude|Haude]] to the helm of the Foundation marked a point of no return. Olympus ceased to be a simple economic entity to become a truly independent paramilitary entity, capable of directly influencing the political agendas of the planet.
* **The Rift and the Birth of Asgard:** Haude's rigid authoritarianism generated internal dissension. Historic members broke away to form the **Asgard Foundation**, an entity which, thanks to the strategic and financial support of [[en/Cast/Groups/Aura United Nations|AUN]], quickly became the main antagonist of Olympus.

---

# II. Decline and Clandestine

The growing rivalry with Asgard quickly degenerated into open conflict. The AUN authorities, initially neutral, began to favor Asgard, presenting it as a bastion of legality. This double standard led to the systematic delegitimization of Olympus:

* **The Ban:** After years of clashes and incidents, Olympus has been officially declared an illegal organization.
* **Current Status:** Stripped of its public legitimacy and persecuted by the institutions, the Olympus Foundation today operates in total **clandestine**, attempting to maintain its influence through an underground network of loyalists and operatives.

---

# III. Power Structure: The 5 Magisters

The operational leadership of the Foundation is made up of the **5 Magister**, a triumvirate (effectively a quintet) of leaders who administer the Foundation's activities on a continental basis. This structure guarantees Olympus an unparalleled capillarity of action, allowing the Magisters to manage immense territories with almost absolute control.

# V. Narrative Notes
The Olympus Foundation today represents the symbol of Aura's technological and military hubris. Her struggle for survival in the shadows, against a system that has repudiated her, makes each member a potential exile or terrorist, depending on who writes the story.
