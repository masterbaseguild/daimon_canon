# Narrative Profile and Alignment

### Role and Faction
Ariel acts as an official Questor on behalf of the Olympus Foundation, permanently siding with the faction's law enforcement agencies.

### Story Arcs and Biography
* **Bridget's Rescue:** Ariel's most significant narrative link is intertwined with the tragic fate of the Kenny Family. In the hours immediately following the brutal extermination of the family at the hands of the Asgard Foundation, Ariel intervenes in the field by intercepting the young [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Bridget|Bridget]] (Yosef Kenny). Having found the girl on the run and in shock after the death of her parents, Ariel takes her under her protective wing, definitively rescuing her and removing her from the clutches of the enemy hitmen.
