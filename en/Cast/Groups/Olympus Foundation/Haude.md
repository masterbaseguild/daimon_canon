# Haude

**Haude** is a complex and controversial figure, once the undisputed leader of the **Olympus Foundation** and now a soul seeking redemption. A founding member of the **5 Magister** of Olympus, his existence has been marked by a brutal transition between a parasitic synthetic nature and a new, fragile humanity.

---

# I. The Past: The Machiavellian Leader

Before his downfall, Haude was the face of ruthless efficiency:
* **Nature:** Originally, Haude was a **parasitic AI** that occupied a human body as a host.
* **Methods:** Noted for his Machiavellian approach, he did not hesitate to sacrifice innocents or manipulate allies to consolidate the power of the Olympus Foundation.
* **"Olympus Last Dance":** The turning point. During the event, his machinations were finally exposed. In an act of sacrifice as unexpected as it was calculated, Haude saved the day, but lost his original form.

---

# II. Reincarnation and Limits

His consciousness was extracted via a drive and implanted into a new host. However, the process was not painless:
* **Loss of Power:** The new host, by surviving, prevented Haude from exercising direct control over the body. Now it must act in symbiosis with the soul of its host.
* **Moral Rehabilitation:** To operate effectively, Haude must learn to live in harmony with the host. His actions must now receive "ethical" approval from the soul he shares, forcing him to learn the concepts of human morality that he had deliberately ignored in the past.

---

# III. Skills and Fighting Style

* **The Past (AI Instinct):** It once excelled with superhuman speed and an artificial intelligence capable of calculating every combat variable in real time.
* **The Present (Golden Soul):** After losing the firepower afforded by direct AI control, Haude had to embrace magic. The educational experience with **Vasil** and the contact with the **Golden Soul** were fundamental. Now, Haude is the rightful user of this strength, which compensates for his diminished physical agility with a mystical power of rare intensity.
* **Equipment:** True to his sword, his fighting style has evolved from a precise, mechanical dance to a more fluid combat enhanced by magical projections.

---

# IV. Story Arc: Towards Redemption

* **Comparison with the Past:** Haude experiences the weight of his immoral actions as a constant burden. His journey is a race against time to obtain a forgiveness that he knows is not guaranteed.
* **Humanization:** Its ultimate goal is no longer domination, but understanding. Accepting one's humanity means, for Haude, accepting the possibility of making mistakes and the need to fix them.

# V. Appearance
* **Race:** Human/Elf.
* **Distinguishing features:** Medium-length hair, he wears a cloak with a high collar that seems to want to protect, or perhaps hide, the identity of the guest who now hosts him.
