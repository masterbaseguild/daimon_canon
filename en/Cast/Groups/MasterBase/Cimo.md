# Cimo

**Cimo** is a [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] of [[en/Races/Gondran|Gondran]] breed, an expert engineer and a leading figure both in [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]] than in [[en/Cast/Groups/Blue Owls/Blue Owls|Blue Owls]]. Having retired from competitive activity as an athlete, he has established himself as one of the organization's most solid defensive bulwarks, evolving his fighting style from a mechanical approach to a deep connection with organic magic.

---

### I. Appearance and Build
Cimo has an imposing physical presence, characterized by a solid structure (muscle mass level 4/5).
* **Gondran Traits:** It features the distinctive Gondran tail and, as a hallmark of its unique design, bears impressive horns inspired by those of Ornn.
* **Style:** Her hair follows the "Ereh" style (season 4), which frames a strong face. The color of the hair is dark brown, while the eyes have a vivid green hue, in contrast to the light complexion typical of his race.
* **Equipment:** He wears the **Master Heavy Armor**, heavy armor that reflects his role as a field defender.

---

### II. Tactical Evolution: From Metal to Nature
Cimo's career is marked by a fundamental technical transition. In the past, he relied on two mechanical arms mounted on his back to increase his reach and manipulation ability. However, following the death of his great friend **Vaximo**, Cimo inherited the [[en/Items/MasterBase/Seed of Nymaera|Nymaera Seed]].
* **Organic Magic:** Having abandoned the technology of mechanical arms, Cimo dedicated himself to the study of **Plant Magic», using the Seed as a catalyst.
* **Signature Move:** Although the mechanical arms have been removed, it is hypothesized the development of a distinctive technique that recalls their function through the creation of vegetal limbs generated by the Seed.

---

### III. Role in the MasterBase
As a member of the team, Cimo covers essential functions for holding the front:
* **Tank & Fortifier:** Thanks to his physical resistance and plant magic, he is able to create natural barriers and instant fortifications on the battlefield.
* **Guard Leader:** His ability to manage defensive lines makes him a natural leader in protecting critical points.
* **Background:** Arriving at MasterBase as part of the wave of recruits in preparation for the **Second Master War**, he has demonstrated steady growth, rising from a simple soldier to an engineering mainstay of the guild.

---

### IV. Personality and Heritage
Of an extroverted nature, Cimo is a man who carries with him the weight of a solemn commitment: to pass down the arts linked to the Seed of Nymaera in memory of Vaximo. His fighting style, which also integrates the use of a dorsal fang, makes him an opponent capable of instantly switching from static defense to brutal counterattack.
