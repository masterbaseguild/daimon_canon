# Narrative Profile and Philosophy of the Leader

### The evolution of leadership ("Aurea Mediocritas")
Entity's path as leader of MasterBase was marked by a strong self-defeating workaholicism and by three distinct philosophical phases:
1. **The Machiavellian Approach:** At the beginning he adopted ruthless and hyper-detailed management, deliberately ignoring the personal needs of his team in order to achieve the objectives. This caused deep disagreements and the loss of many comrades.
2. **Absolute Permissiveness:** Repenting of past mistakes, he went to the opposite extreme, granting total freedom to the guild and taking on every single political and managerial responsibility alone. The reluctance to delegate and the exasperation resulting from this enormous amount of work led the guild to collapse and dissolution.
3. **The Balance of Maturity:** After years of isolation, Entity has understood that a good leader must be neither tyrannical nor too permissive, but must know how to distribute responsibilities equally within the team.

### Distinctive Traits and Covert Operations
Entity loves to operate in the shadows. He has developed numerous secret identities to conduct undercover operations; among these, the best known is that of **Vasil**, an alias he used to secretly move from one starbase to another at the end of 2020, after being temporarily dethroned by Beat.

# Biography and Timeline

### Militancy in the Anark and the Disaster (2018)
Entity began his career in the Anark guild. Despite his inexperience, his excessive zeal and strong ambition played a key role in the chain of events that led the faction to suffer severe losses during the [[en/Timeline/Chronicles of Iphiridia|Iphiridia disaster]], causing their final dissolution once they returned to Aura.

### The rise and fall of MasterBase
In June 2018, Entity rallied the survivors of Anark and founded MasterBase, serving as leader almost continuously. However, the progressive deterioration of relationships with his companions and his drift towards distrust dragged the guild into a spiral of decay. The crisis culminated in Rattly's infiltration, the near-total destruction of StarBase, and the dispersal of its members.

### Exile and Rebirth
Following the collapse, Entity retreated into a three-year voluntary seclusion on Iphiridia, living in solitude among the ruins of the old base. Having repented and understood his mistakes, Entity is back: he has gathered a new team of companions and is currently working to resurrect the MasterBase from its ashes.

# Fighting Style and Equipment

### Engagement Dynamics
Entity is an agile, fast and specialized fighter in "diving": he loves to dive headlong into the opponent's rear to break up enemy formations. If in the past he relied on biomechanical augments to enhance his reflexes, today his primary resource is **Air / Kinetic Magic** ([[en/Glossary/Kinetic Magic|Kinetic Magic]]), which he uses to move fluidly and in mid-air. He has dedicated much of his life to perfecting flight maneuvers via [[en/Items/MasterBase/Master CrossWings|Master CrossWings]].

### Current Arsenal
* **Double Master Blade:** A single, double-bladed sword that he wields as both a slashing weapon and a "beacon" to channel his kinetic magic.
* **Multiple Master Dagger:** A swarm of 6 throwing knives that Entity levitates and directs remotely through the manipulation of air, coordinating their movements in symbiosis with the slashes of the main sword.

### Evolution of Styles (Abandoned)
The current knife swarm style is the result of numerous practical rethinks and evolutions over the course of his career:
* **Enchanted Blade (Debut Style):** Originally fought with a single sword, enhanced with gems from the [[en/Items/Magic School of Fire|School of Fire (Firebending)]] to deal burn damage.
* **Dual Wielding:** He experimented for a time with using two swords at the same time.
* **The Third Sword:** Soon after learning kinetic magic, he attempted to control three entire swords by levitating them along with the knives. The style was discarded as excessively mentally demanding and impractical on the battlefield.
