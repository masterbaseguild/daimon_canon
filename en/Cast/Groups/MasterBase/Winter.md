Winter is a [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]], within which he operates as an athlete and fighting magician, and a member of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Blue Owls/Blue Owls|Blue Owls]]. A former founding member of Navirad, he is widely recognized as the best pyromancer in the entire guild.

# Physical Characteristics and Appearance

* **Gender:** Male
* **Breed:** [[en/Races/Aural|Aural]]
* **Appearance:** Brown wavy hair.
* **Clothing:** He wears the standard Master uniform, even if he tends to fight bare-chested (*topless*) for a precise reason linked to his magical style.

# Narrative Profile and Alignment

### Role in the Guild
Within the MasterBase, Winter holds the dual role of **Battlemage** (frontline assault mage) and **Athlete** for the faction's sports divisions.

### Personality and Traits
* **Incendiary Pragmatism:** He is an extremely practical fighter. Since the explosive and close-range nature of his flames would end up incinerating his own clothes, he prefers to take to the field by stripping off the upper part of his uniform so as not to ruin it.

### Biography and Timeline
* **The Foundation of Navirad:** Winter travels to Planet Iphiridia with Kurymi. There the two meet Gastic Nox and Black Ace; the understanding between the four pushes them to join forces and found the **Navirad** guild.
* **The Arpeggio and the Farewell:** Having survived the catastrophic disaster of Iphiridia, Winter follows the Navirad during the construction of their new starbase, the *Arpeggio*, definitively abandoning the planet Aura. Subsequently, however, he came into contact with MasterBase: fascinated by the project, he decided to leave Navirad to join Entity. His choice will lead the way, later pushing other members of his old guild to follow him into the MasterBase.
* **Rise as a Pyromancer:** Once established aboard the MasterBase, Winter undergoes grueling training. Taking advantage of the teachings of the guild's veteran magicians and thanks to hard personal work, he refines his technique until he is officially proclaimed the best fire user (*Firebender*) of the faction.
* **The Blue Owls and the Uprising:** During his stay he forms a strong friendship with Dragor and Alone, a bond that leads him to join the **Blue Owls**. Due to his close proximity to Alone's group, Winter becomes mildly involved in the tumultuous events of the Uprising.

# Fighting Style and Skills

Winter fights exclusively with his bare hands (**he does not use weapons**), channeling his immense magical power directly into melee.

### Specialization: Firebending
His style is based on the emission and close manipulation of fire, alternating enhanced physical blows with devastating medium-range explosions:
* **Firepunch:** Concentrates flames around your fists to unleash devastating incendiary melee attacks.
* **Fireball:** Generate and launch classic concentrated fireballs from a distance.
* **Propulsion:** Uses the continuous emission of flame from his hands or feet as kinetic thrust, allowing him to perform lightning-fast sprints, evasive maneuvers, or move nimbly across the battlefield.
* **Ignuke:** His most destructive technique; a large-scale fire detonation with properties similar to a small magical nuclear device, used to clear entire areas of enemies.
