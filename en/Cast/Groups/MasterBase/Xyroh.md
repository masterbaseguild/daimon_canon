# Xyroh

**Xyroh** is a top-ranked Questor of **MasterBase**, known for his lethality on the battlefield and a complex personality that alternates between an iron dedication to his assigned tasks and an impulsive and short-tempered nature.

---

## I. Combat Profile
* **Fighting Style:** Xyroh is a warrior specialized in frontline combat. His signature weapon is a **double-bladed sword**, a tool that requires exceptional coordination and that Xyroh uses with lethal mastery.
* **Skills:** He is considered one of the most capable Questors in the entire Alpha Sphere. His presence on the front is often decisive, thanks to quick reflexes and an offensive power that few opponents can contain.

## II. Personality and Path
* **Character:** His temperament is his Achilles' heel: a strong irascibility that makes him unpredictable, both for his enemies and for his own companions.
* **The Lonely Years:** Before finding a true home in the guild, Xyroh experienced a long period of isolation. He has known **Entity** since they were both early in their careers, but preferred to hang out on MasterBase as a loner for many years, maintaining an emotional distance from the other members.
* **The Integration:** Only after a long journey was he able to overcome his solitary nature, forming deep bonds with the rest of the group and becoming, in all respects, an official and esteemed member of the MasterBase.

## III. Colonial Enterprise
* **The Orbis Plan:** In addition to his combat skills, Xyroh has demonstrated remarkable tactical command skills by directing the **colonization of the planet Orbis**, located in the **Nobi System**. This mission highlighted how, beyond his war instinct, Xyroh also possesses the strategic vision needed to guide the guild's expansion.
