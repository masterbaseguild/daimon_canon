# GaroFrankyRose

**Garo FrankyRose** is a [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] athlete, central figure among the **[[en/Glossary/8 Founding Masters|8 founding masters]]** of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]]. Coming from the historic **Franky family** and the FrankyRose clan, Garo is an individual who has radically redefined his identity, going from a life as a criminal enforcer to a pillar of the new organization.

---

### I. Identity and Bonds
* **Race:** [[en/Races/Gondran|Gondran]] (equipped with a Gondran tail).
* **Family Ties:** Cousin of [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Emily FrankyRose|Emily Franky Rose]], with whom he shares an unbreakable bond.
* **Style:** Featuring the "Long Upper Brush" haircut.

---

### II. Biography and Transformation
Garo's life is a succession of ruptures and rebirths, each marked by the symbolic abandonment of a weapon:
* **The Criminal Past:** For years he was an active member of the Franky family, operating as a gangster and loan shark, carrying with him the weight of a role he never really wanted.
* **Iphiridia's Trauma:** During the Nexus Incident on Iphiridia, she witnesses the death of her two adoptive brothers at the hands of Entity. Returning to the Ring, he is disowned by his family, ending up on their "blacklist".
* **The Entrance into the MasterBase:** He is saved by an infiltration operation led by his cousin Emily. By joining the founders, he embarks on a path of introspection that leads him to constantly change his approach to warfare.

---

### III. Arsenal and Fighting Style
Garo associates his weapons with specific evolutionary periods of his life:
* **Gangster Phase (Scythe):** Franky era weapon, now abandoned.
* **Tank Phase (Zweihander):** Used during the first period in MasterBase. It was broken in half after a duel against Entity.
* **Current Phase (Enchanted Harpoon):** He currently fights with a magical harpoon, known as **Daimon Pact**, which houses an ethereal within it. This transition marks his transition to shorter, more agile weapons, which he said he is more comfortable with.
* **Other Skills:** In addition to the harpoon, he excels at *stealth*, ethereal taming* and close combat through the use of the Gondran **dorsal fang**.

---

### IV. Story Arc
Garo's path is the search for purpose. After spending his life seeking "roles to fulfill" on behalf of others — first for the Franky family, then as a tank for MasterBase — Garo has come to realize that his path is not defined by a preset function, but by the freedom to choose his own individual path.
