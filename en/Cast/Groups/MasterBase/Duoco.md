# Duoco

**Duoco** is a [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] high profile athlete with the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]]. Belonging to the **Aural** race, it stands out for its slender and extremely athletic physicality (Height Level 5), oriented towards maximum agility rather than brute muscle mass.

---

### I. Identity and Physical Profile
* **Breed:** [[en/Races/Aural|Aural]].
* **Build:** His physique is optimized for speed and precision (Muscle Level 2, Fat Level 1). He is a tall, rangy fighter whose effectiveness comes not from striking force, but from his ability to move fluidly across the battlefield.
* **Equipment:** Despite the need for agility, Duoco wears the **Master Heavy Armor**, balancing the weight of heavy armor with exceptional mobility.

---

### II. Fighting Style
Duoco's career has been characterized by continuous technical evolution. After abandoning previous specializations such as *firebending* and combat with *dual blade*, he found his definitive dimension in the use of thrown weapons:
* **Armament:** Uses several **Master Throwing Axes**. His ability to handle multiple hatchets at the same time allows him to maintain constant control over the range.
* **Skills:** Excels in both single-axe and dual-axe combat, making him dangerous both at range and in close combat.
* **Special Ability:** He uses **Dynamic Magic** through the *Leap of Faith* technique, which allows him to perform unpredictable aerial maneuvers and tactical changes of position that undermine enemy defenses.

---

### III. Role and Partnership
Duoco joined MasterBase by direct invitation from **Aledge**, shortly after the conclusion of the First Master War. His initial goal was to contribute to the strengthening of the guild's sports projects, bringing his experience as an athlete.
* **Synergy:** His tactical identity is inextricably linked to his collaboration with **Andreas**. The two routinely fight as a duo, creating a cohesive unit where Duoco's throwing paths mesh perfectly with Andreas' range, making it nearly impossible for opponents to isolate them or handle the pressure of their joint attack.

---

### IV. Operational Status
Currently, Duoco represents one of the most versatile talents of MasterBase, capable of combining the precision required by a professional athlete with the brutality necessary on the battlefield. Although his features (hair, eyes, complexion) are still awaiting a definitive aesthetic definition, his fame as a precision marksman is already firmly consolidated.
