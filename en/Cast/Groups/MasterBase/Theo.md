# Theo

**Theo** is a legendary figure within the **MasterBase**, one of the **8 Founding Masters** who laid the foundation for the order in the Alpha Sphere. His story is a complex path that oscillates between the desire for anarchic subversion and the responsibility for the foundation of a new order.

---

## I. Physical Profile and Combat
* **Race:** Aural.
* **Fighting Style:** Theo is a gunslinger of exceptional precision, specializing in the use of **double pistols**.
* **Peculiar Techniques:** He uses **crosswings hooks**, tactical hooks that allow him three-dimensional mobility on the battlefield, making him an extremely difficult target to hit during firefights.

## II. Background and History
* **The Origin of the Anark:** Theo's connection to the world of crime and surveillance arises by chance. Knowing **Gavil** — a secret member of **Olympus** — Theo had the opportunity to snoop around his private arsenal. The theft of some high-tech weapons in that vault was the spark that allowed him to found the **Anark** guild together with his companions.
* **The Crisis:** After the arrival of **Entity** and the disastrous epilogue on **Iphiridia**, Theo suffered a profound existential crisis that led him to temporarily abandon his life as a vigilante.
* **The Foundation:** His return to the public scene marked a radical turning point: instead of continuing the path of the Anark, he joined those who would later form the **8 Founding Masters** of the **MasterBase**, trying to channel his desire for justice into a more solid and governed structure.

## III. Evolutionary Arc: The Weight of Forgiveness
The heart of Theo's narrative lies in his psychological journey:
* **The conflict with Entity:** His narrative arc is dominated by the complex relationship with Entity. Theo still cannot fully forgive Entity for past events.
* **The Goal:** Theo realizes that his inability to forgive Entity is actually a mirror of his inability to **forgive himself** for the crimes and mistakes he committed during the Anark years and his past choices. Only by facing his own guilt can he hope to find peace with the rest of the group.
