# Tecmov

**Tecmov** is the architect of **MasterBase** and one of the brightest engineering minds in the Alpha Sphere. As a member of the **8 Founding Masters**, he redefined the concept of technological infrastructure and tactical support for the guild.

---

## I. Physical Profile and Hi-Tech Equipment
* **Race:** Aural.
* **Appearance:** Features a *Medium Upper Brush* hairstyle characterized by a distinctive central white stripe. Its aesthetics are intrinsically linked to its hi-tech nature: its standard equipment includes the **Master Headset**, a tactical **Master Drone** and an entire suite of gadgets (viewers, smartwatches, neural interfaces) of its own invention.
* **Future Evolution:** A **holographic cloak** is planned to be implemented to further complement its wearable technology.

## II. Combat Profile
* **Armament:**
* **Custom Blade:** A versatile sword that acts as a conventional solid weapon but capable of instantly transforming into a *Dual Saber*, equipped with an energy laser blade (inspired by dual-emission technology).
* **Master Drone:** An autonomous support unit personally designed to accompany him in battle, providing cover, scans and tactical fire.
* **Skills:** A master of the *dual-blade* technique, he combines fencing with his deep knowledge of robotics, aerospace and digital engineering to master any scenario.

## III. Background and Career
* **The Olympus Era:** Served the **Olympus Foundation** as a top engineer for years. However, his nature (autistic spectrum) and the difficulty in managing the dynamics of social collaboration brought him to the brink of expulsion.
* **The Turning Point:** Olympus assigned him one last, colossal task: the construction of the MasterBase. Once completed, Tecmov chose to sever ties with the Foundation, solidifying his position as the Founding Master of the new faction.

## IV. Evolutionary Arc: Overcoming Isolation
Tecmov's narrative path is a challenge against one's nature:
* **The conflict:** His analytical mind is so quick and precise that it makes it difficult to accept other people's work, which he often perceives as inferior.
* **The Goal:** To learn that "no man is an island." His arc is based on the acceptance that, although the contributions of others may differ from his standards, collaboration is the only way to sustain the MasterBase. He must learn to delegate, not only for the good of the guild, but for his own personal evolution.
