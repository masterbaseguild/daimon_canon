# Proximus

**Proximus** is a front-line Questor of **MasterBase**, renowned for his ability to blend the art of swordsmanship with masterful manipulation of **Dynamic Magic**. He is an assault fighter capable of altering the energy flow of the battlefield.

---

## I. Physical Profile
* **Race:** Aural.
* **Appearance:** Characterized by *Long Messy* hair, reflecting his energetic and unconventional fighting style.

## II. Combat Profile
* **Armament:** He wields a **Master Blade**, which he uses not only as a conventional blade, but as a catalyst for his magical energies.
* **Dynamic Magic:** He is a true master in this discipline. His main techniques include:
* **Shockwaves:** He uses his sword to generate powerful blasts of kinetic energy, capable of repelling or disintegrating enemy defenses.
* **Enhancement Energy Fields:** Creates protective or physically enhancing auras that envelop himself (or nearby allies), drastically increasing strength, speed and resilience in moments of maximum war intensity.
* **Role:** Vanguard fighter. His presence is essential for breaking the opposing defensive lines, creating gaps where his teammates can insert themselves.

## III. Archive note
> *«Seeing Proximus in action is like watching a controlled thunderstorm. He doesn't just hit; overwhelms. Every time his Master Blade whistles through the air, the atmosphere around him distorts. He is the perfect trailblazer: where he arrives, enemy resistance stops being a wall and becomes rubble.»* — Tactical field evaluation.

---

## IV. Operational Status
Active. MasterBase's front-line Questor.
