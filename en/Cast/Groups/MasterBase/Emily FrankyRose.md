# Emily

**Emily** is a [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]] and an apical figure among the **[[en/Glossary/8 Founding Masters|8 founding masters]]**. Coming from the Ring and the **FrankyRose** clan, she represents the spearhead of the guild as regards covert operations and the management of relationship crises within the MasterVerse.

---

### I. Identity and Bonds
* **Breed:** [[en/Races/Gondran|Gondran]].
* **Family Ties:** She is a cousin of [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Garo FrankyRose|Garo FrankyRose]], with whom she shares a fraternal bond that goes beyond the dynamics of the guild, representing a moral and operational point of reference for him.
* **Features:** His physical design and battle outfit specifications are stored in dedicated *concepts* files.

---

### II. Narrative Profile
Emily's path is a constant escape from her criminal roots to find a higher purpose.
* **The Exile:** Raised in the FrankyRose clan, she actively rejected her lineage's criminal lifestyle, fleeing to the planet **Zawa**.
* **The Royal Period:** Under the protection of **Vaximo**, he served the royal family of Zawa, forming a deep bond with **Bridget**, who later became his ally at MasterBase.
* **The Foundation:** Driven by the vision of a new order, she abandoned court life to join **Entity**, becoming one of the eight founding members of MasterBase.
* **Operation FrankyRose:** She was the architect of the mission of the same name, a high-risk raid that led to the rescue of Garo FrankyRose and the subsequent recruitment of Bridget into the ranks of MasterBase.

---

### III. Combat Profile
Emily is an **espionage and assassination** specialist, known for her quiet, methodical approach.
* **Arsenal:** Utilize a bow for long-range surgical engagements and an array of tactical explosives for field control and creating diversions.
* **Dorsal Fang:** Being a Gondran, she integrates the use of her dorsal fang into her close-quarters fighting style, a lethal natural weapon that allows her to close duels in fractions of a second.
* **Legacy Skills:** While he favors pure stealth today, his past is marked by the use of *Waterbending* and *Icebending*. Techniques such as the *Ice armspike* and the *Ice final armor* remain part of its war legacy, preserved as emergency assets.

---

### IV. Strategic Role
Inside the guild, Emily acts as the shadow of the MasterBase. Her ability to infiltrate, strike and vanish makes her essential for missions where brute force - typical of other members - would be counterproductive. She is the guarantor of perimeter security and the invisible arm of the Masters' decisions.
