Wall is a [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]], founding member of [[en/Glossary/8 Founding Masters|8 founding masters]] and former member of the historic guild [[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/Anark|Anark]]. He is the younger brother of [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Entity|Entity]] and the cousin of Honda.

# Physical Characteristics and Appearance

* **Gender:** Male
* **Breed:** [[en/Races/Aural|Aural]]
* **Distinguishing Features:** He has a bionic arm (mechanical prosthesis to replace the left limb).

# Narrative Profile and Alignment

### Role in the Guild
Inside the MasterBase he holds the official role of **Chief Pilot** and manages a special division he himself created: the **Disaster Control** team, an elite corps dedicated exclusively to the safeguarding, evacuation and protection of civilians during galactic conflicts.

### Biography and Story Arcs
* **The Anark era and the trauma:** Dragged into the world of the Questors from a very young age due to the ambition of his brother Entity, Wall joins the Anark at his invitation in 2017. Deeply shaken by the horrors of war and the fragility of biological life, he experiences firsthand the fall of the guild. During the catastrophic disaster on the planet Iphiridia, Wall is seriously injured and loses an arm.
* **The Rebirth:** After the evacuation, scientist Tecmov designs and installs an advanced bionic arm on the boy. At Entity's call, Wall decides not to abandon the camp and joins the survivors to found the MasterBase.
* **Steel and Flesh:** Obsessed by the contrast between the vulnerability of human flesh and the immortality of the steel of his prosthesis, Wall pursues a rigid personal path. His goal is to harden his body through extreme training, until his biological part has reached the same level of power and indestructibility as his metal arm.

# Fighting Style and Skills

### Vehicles and Arsenal
* **Bionic Arm:** Used as a close-impact weapon and augmented logistics tool.
* **Transportation Expertise (Supreme Pilot):** Wall possesses absolute mastery in driving any vehicle. His specializations include:
* *4 Wheels:* Heavy, armored and off-road land vehicles.
* *2 Wheels:* Fast assault and reconnaissance bike.
* *Flight:* Aircraft, space shuttles, and fighter jets.

### Techniques and War Capabilities
* **Martial Arts:** Expert in hand-to-hand combat, optimized to complement the devastating blows of the bionic arm.
* **Gas Bending:** Magical or technical ability to manipulate gaseous currents of air to create diversions, breathing barriers, or alter the surrounding environment for defensive purposes.
