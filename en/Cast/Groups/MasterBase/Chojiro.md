# Chojiro

**Chojiro** is an expert [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] currently working for [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]], with a past linked to **SpaceFox**. His figure is a unique example of operational dualism: a healer devoted to life who, in the field, transforms into a silent predator.

---

### I. Ascension and Transition
Chojiro began his career as a member of **SpaceFox**. During the turbulent time of the **First Master War**, he chose to follow the leader **Aledge** in his transition to the MasterBase. This decision proved to be strategic not only for his career, but for the future of the organization itself, becoming one of the main promoters of the **MasterCon** operation, an event that marked a turning point in the cohesion of the group.

---

### II. Medical and Tactical Role
Chojiro plays a vital role within the structure, balancing the care of his companions with the protection of critical areas:
* **Onboard Doctor:** He is responsible for the physical well-being of MasterBase members. His dedication to keeping the team running smoothly between battles is critical to the Guild's continued operations.
* **Rear Defender:** Despite his medical vocation, Chojiro does not hesitate to act lethally in combat. His task is to neutralize any opponent who attempts to infiltrate the rear - where the most vulnerable members or long-range supports operate - by expertly using **throwing knives**.

---

### III. Operational Profile
Chojiro's ability to alternate between the roles of savior and hitman makes his fighting style extremely unpredictable. He is the "silent guardian" of the rear lines: his presence guarantees that no enemy can threaten comrades who are wounded or engaged in technical tasks without facing a quick and precise death.
