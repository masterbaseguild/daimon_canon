# Samset

**Samset** is one of the most unique Questors of **MasterBase**, specializing in spatial manipulation. His mastery of transportation technologies makes him a vital asset to the guild's logistics and tactics.

---

## I. Physical Profile and Equipment
* **Race:** Aural.
* **Appearance:** He wears the official MasterBase uniform, enriched by his **Warp Gauntlets**, indispensable tools to channel and stabilize his dimensional manipulation abilities.

## II. Combat Profile
* **Armament:** Does not use conventional weapons; his power lies entirely in his magical and technological abilities.
* **Skills (Metamaterial Magic):** Samset is a master at creating and managing **Teleportation Gates**. This ability allows him to instantly move allies, equipment, or himself across the battlefield, making the MasterBase's mobility superior to that of any other group in the Alpha Sphere.

## III. Background and History
* **The Olympus Experience:** Samset began his journey within the **Olympus Foundation**, where he dedicated himself to advanced research on portal technology and dimensional physics.
* **The transition to MasterBase:** During his research period, he formed a strong friendship with **Davrin**. It was precisely thanks to this relationship of trust and the sharing of ideals that Samset decided to abandon Olympus to officially join **MasterBase**, bringing with him his precious wealth of scientific knowledge.

## IV. Evolutionary Arc (In the process of being defined)
* *Note:* The Samset path is still in development. Given his nature as an action researcher, his narrative arc could focus on the moral responsibility of wielding technologies capable of bending space-time, or on the psychological impact that abandoning the Olympus Foundation has had on his perception of "correct science".
