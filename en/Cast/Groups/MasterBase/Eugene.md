Eugene (also known as Euge) is a former member of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/Anark|Anark]] guild and a [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]], within which he retired from the battlefield to work permanently as a scientific researcher.

# Physical Characteristics and Appearance

* **Build:** Robust.
* **Historical Clothing:** During his years as a fighter, he loved to cover himself with a long cape and hide his face behind a mask.

---

# Narrative Profile and Biography

### The origins and the Anark era
Eugene grows up in the same town as **Theo**. From their strong bond of fraternal friendship was born the idea of ​​founding Anark, the guild in which Eugene took his first steps as a Questor.

### The parenthesis in the Twin Hounds
Following the tragic dissolution of Anark, Eugene continues his career by joining the **Twin Hounds**. Within this faction he holds the role of squadron commander and, at the same time, begins to distinguish himself as a researcher specialized in *organic magic*. Despite the distance, he maintains close contact with his old companions Nephasto and Theo.

### The birth of the Archivists and the MasterBase
A few years later, Eugene forms a strong friendship with **Beat**. Together with him, **Nephasto** and **Dave**, he forms a private scientific research club.

On the occasion of the coup plotted by Beat against Entity, this circle officially evolves into the **Council of Archivists** of the MasterBase. Eugene finally joins the guild and becomes a cornerstone of the team, contributing greatly to studies of magical field anomalies for military purposes.

### The Schism and the transfer to Aura
Upon Entity's return from exile and due to the latter's growing hostility towards their research, the group decides to split from the parent guild. Eugene follows the rest of the Archivists in their transfer to **Planet Aura**, where he continues to carry out scientific operations connected to the MasterBase, but safe from potential spite or retaliation from the Entity.

# Fighting Style (Abandoned)

With his official entry into the Council of Archivists, Eugene has **permanently abandoned the path of the warrior**, dedicating himself exclusively to research and magical science.

His historical warfare skills include:
* **Long Range Combat:** Specialist in using a variety of firearms to cover rear lines.
* **Close Engagement:** Used a war scythe as his primary melee weapon in close quarters engagements.
