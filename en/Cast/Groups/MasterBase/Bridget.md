# Bridget (Yosef Kenny)

**Bridget**, born **Yosef Kenny**, is a young scientist and member of **The Guard** within the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]]. Having survived the tragic massacre of his own house, his path is a relentless search for scientific truths and personal justice, marked by bonds with key figures of the MasterVerse and the legacy of the Kenny Family.

---

### I. The Origins and the Massacre
At the age of 14, Yosef was the sole survivor of the **Kenny Family** massacre. In a desperate attempt to protect him, **Ariel** saved him by sending him to **Gondraw**. There, he was taken into custody by **Vaximo** — already known for having assisted Emily Rose in similar circumstances — who provided him with protection and guidance. After years, Vaximo revealed to Bridget a part of his hidden truth: the existence of an integrated **Neural Chip**, a secret that would forever change the perception of his own identity.

---

### II. The Scientific Heritage
Discovering the research left behind by her parents, Bridget embraced her family's scientific vocation. The Kennys' primary objective was ambitious and dangerous: **to break the taboo that separated Technology and Magic**, trying to merge them in a revolutionary synthesis. Bridget has dedicated years to continuing these studies, which today represent the key to understanding both her past and the dark plots linked to Asgard.

---

### III. The Meeting with Emily and the Entrance into the MasterBase
During her time in the royal circles of Gondraw, Bridget met **Emily Rose**. A year after the latter's departure from the royal family, Emily returned to Gondoraw with a decisive proposal: entry into **MasterBase**. For Bridget, this was the only concrete opportunity to access confidential data and shed light on both the massacre of her parents and the mysteries related to Asgard.

---

### IV. Role in The Guard and the Path of Vengeance
Aboard the MasterBase, Bridget joined **The Guard**, maintaining the facility and managing the complex security systems. However, its trajectory is destined to change radically:
* **The Awareness:** In the near future, Bridget will understand the real motive behind the massacre of her family.
* **The Transformation:** This revelation is making a deep and devastating feeling develop in him: **revenge**. This inner fire is pushing Bridget to abandon her purely technical nature to take up the path of combat, transforming the Kennys' knowledge into a weapon.
