# Bede

**Bede** is a powerful [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] specialized in the magical arts, operating under the aegis of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]]. Recognized as an excellent **Light Wizard**, Bede fills a key offensive support role, acting as the architect of long-range damage in the guild's field operations.

---

### I. Spell-like Abilities
Bede channels ethereal energy in the form of pure light radiation. His magic is not only visually impressive, but is designed for specific tactical purposes:
* **Light Manipulation:** Able to shape light into projectiles, focused beams, or area-of-effect blasts.
* **Offensive Versatility:** Thanks to the control of his element, Bede can vary between focused impact damage (to eliminate single armored targets) and wide-range bombardment (to saturate entire areas).

---

### II. Tactical Role: The Bomber
In battle, Bede is a key element who operates strictly from the **backlines**. Its task is to:
* **Saturation Bombardment:** Take advantage of the superior range of your spells to hit the enemy deployment before they can even come into contact with the front lines of the MasterBase.
* **Field Control:** With his light blasts, Bede not only deals direct damage, but is able to blind, disorientate and break the opposing formation, creating gaps where the guild's *skirmishers* and duellists can penetrate.
* **Strategic Support:** Operating safely behind the lines, it provides constant cover, forcing enemies to make a difficult choice: suffer its relentless fire or attempt a risky sortie to reach it, exposing themselves to fire from the base's defenders.

---

### III. Operational Profile
Bede is a distance pro. Its presence is crucial in attrition battles, where its ability to project power without being exposed to enemy fire allows the MasterBase to maintain control of the energy flows of combat. It is considered one of the most reliable magical artillery units in the organization.
