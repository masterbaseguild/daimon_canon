# Finix

**Finix** is a Questor of **MasterBase** who embodies a rare duality: deadly precision on the battlefield and a deep artistic talent that makes her the musical face of the guild. Ex-member of the **SpaceFox** guild, he found his new home and professional balance in the MasterBase.

---

## I. Physical Profile and System
* **Race:** Aural.
* **Appearance:** Dark skin contrasted by "flaming" red hair, characterized by a distinctive blond tuft. He regularly wears the MasterBase tactical uniform.

## II. Combat Profile
* **Armament:** Specialist in precision and saturation shooting, he uses a heavy **machine gun** with which he is able to control entire battle areas.
* **Magic:** Possesses a unique tactical combination:
* **Firebending:** Used for both offensive support and territory management via fire barriers.
* **Healing:** Thanks to his healing ability, he fills a vital role as a **Battle Healer**, ensuring that his frontline comrades can remain operational even under pressure.
* **Role:** Marksman / Battle Healer.

## III. History and Path
* **The SpaceFox Era:** He gained his initial experience in the ranks of *SpaceFox*, where he met **Aledge**. The bond established with him proved decisive for his future.
* **Joining MasterBase:** After the fall of *SpaceFox*, Finix joined Aledge at **MasterBase in 2020**, quickly integrating as a full member.
* **Social Expansion:** In 2022, he expanded his radius of influence by forging strong friendships with several members of the **Lignum Nobili**, enriching the guild's network of alliances.

## IV. The Voice of the MasterBase
* **Singing Talent:** Finix has exceptional vocal talents. He is an active member of the **Official MasterBase Band** and spends much of his free time at the *Masteria*, where his performances act as a social glue, bringing some warmth and comfort after the most arduous missions.
