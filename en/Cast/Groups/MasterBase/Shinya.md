# Shinya

**Shinya** is a **MasterBase** Questor known for his lightning speed and a fighting philosophy based on efficiency and moderation. Former leader of the **New Group** guild, he found the ideal context in the MasterBase to put his technical skills at the service of justice.

---

## I. Physical Profile and Race
* **Race:** Vulture.
* **Features:** (Being defined). Shinya maintains an agile and athletic appearance, consistent with his fighting style based on speed of movement.

## II. Fighting Style and Magic
* **Pragmatic Approach:** Shinya's combat is guided by maximum efficiency: avoiding the superfluous. He limits himself to drawing his **Master Blade** only when facing high-ranking (official) opponents, preferring to neutralize subordinates by resorting to submission techniques with the weapon still in its sheath.
* **Dynamic Magic:** Despite his talent, he has chosen to limit his magical arsenal. He specializes in a single technique, the **Super Dash**, mastering it so versatilely that it is extremely unpredictable in battle.
* **Tactical Inspiration:** His fighting style is reminiscent of that of the masters of rapid swordsmanship (such as Zenitsu Agatsuma), where every movement is calculated and lightning-fast.

## III. History and Path
* **The New Group Era:** Shinya approached the world of guilds at a very young age, founding *New Group* in 2018. He led the organization for about three years before internal dynamics pushed him to definitively leave.
* **The Attempt at Normality:** He tried to take refuge on the planet **Aura**, trying to leave his life as a Questor behind, but the call of adventure proved stronger than any desire for tranquility.
* **The Alliance with the MasterBase:** The chance meeting in **North Regium** with Masters **Theo**, **Nephasto** and **Dave** was the catalyst for his return. Recognizing them as mentors and ideal companions, Shinya abandoned civilian life to join the MasterBase.

## IV. Personality
* **Temperament:** Inspired by Natsu Dragneel's vitality and outspokenness, Shinya possesses a fiery soul and a strong sense of duty. Despite his pragmatic efficiency in combat, he retains a jovial and impulsive attitude off the battlefield, making him a leading member of the group.
