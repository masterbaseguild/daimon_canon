# DoctorLatency

**Doctor Latency** is a [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] and high-profile researcher at [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]]. Belonging to the **Aural** race, Latency represents the academic mind of the guild, dedicating its existence to the study of the magical and historical mechanisms that govern the MasterVerse.

---

### I. Identity and Appearance
* **Breed:** [[en/Races/Aural|Aural]]. The biological and physical characteristics of the Aurals give Latency a detached and analytical presence, typical of those who observe the phenomena of the world through the filter of the scientific method.
* **Clothing:** He wears the standard MasterBase researchers' coat, a symbol of his institutional commitment and dedication to academic research within the organization.

---

### II. Skills and Research
Doctor Latency is not a melee fighter; its strength lies in the intellect and the ability to decode the invisible:
* **Magic Research:** He specializes in the analysis of ethereal currents and the laws that regulate magical energy. His work allows MasterBase to better understand the nature of individual members' powers and optimize their use in a tactical context.
* **Historical Research:** Latency acts as the custodian of the guild's historical memory. His research into past texts and events is critical to contextualizing current crises and preventing mistakes made during the Master Wars from being repeated.

---

### III. Operational Role
Within MasterBase, his role is that of "architect of knowledge". While other Questors deal with war execution, Doctor Latency provides the theoretical support necessary for missions of exploration or recovery of ancient artifacts. Its ability to integrate historical research with magical research makes it an irreplaceable point of reference for understanding the mysteries of the system.
