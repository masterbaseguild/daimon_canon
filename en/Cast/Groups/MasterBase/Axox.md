# Axox

**Axox** is an ex-[[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] who operated within the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]]. Specializing in close combat, Axox has distinguished himself as one of the most agile and lethal operators in the guild, playing a fundamental role in the immediate engagement phases.

---

### I. Combat Profile
Axox is a pure duelist, whose style reflects a philosophy based on speed and surgical aggression.
* **Specialization:** A dual-sword fighter, Axox favors a dynamic fencing style that combines acrobatic dodging and precision thrusts.
* **Tactical Role:** Operates primarily as a frontline **skirmisher**. Its function is to destabilize enemy lines, engage multiple targets extremely quickly and maintain high tactical pressure, making it a valuable asset in clashes that require a rapid change of front.

---

### II. Permanence in the MasterBase
During its militancy in the MasterBase, Axox represented the prototype of the front line fighter. His ability to read the flow of battle and isolate individual opponents to bring them to a duel in conditions of numerical inferiority for the enemy made him famous during several field operations.

---

### III. Inheritance
While no longer part of the guild's active ranks, Axox's name remains associated with an era of MasterBase operational aggression. His legacy is that of a duellist who sought not brute head-on confrontation, but the methodical elimination of threats through mastery in the wielding of his twin blades.
