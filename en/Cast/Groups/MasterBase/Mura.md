# Walls

**Mura** is one of the newest and most enigmatic recruits to join **MasterBase**. A member of the **Sadrian** race (also known as *Vernians*), Mura represents a classic draconid archetype — powerful, hurled, and tailed — that stands apart from other draconic lifeforms in the Alpha Sphere.

---

## I. Physical Profile and Race
* **Race:** Sadrian (Vernian). Draconid humanoid with protective scales and tail.
*   **Characteristics:**
* **Height:** 2/5 (Nimble but compact).
* **Build:** Defined muscles (2/5) with a low percentage of fat mass (1/5).
* **Appearance:** Dark blonde hair with a "northern" style hairstyle, intense green eyes.
* **Equipment:** Wear heavy armor designed to balance protection and mobility during melee.

## II. Combat Profile
* **Armament:** Although initially associated with a hammer, Mura has shown a clear preference for a **two-handed greatsword**, a tool that best suits his brutal and effective fighting style.
* **Magic:**
* **Dynamic Magic:** Specializes in enhanced attacks that maximize the kinetic impact of his greatsword strikes.
* **Metamaterial Magic:** He possesses the ability to use teleportation, an ability that makes him an extremely dangerous fighter: he is able to instantly close the distance to the target, bringing his greatsword into contact before the opponent can react.

## III. History and Insertion
* **The Recruitment:** Mura did not arrive at MasterBase via standard channels; his entry was the result of a **direct invitation from Entity**, which recognized in him a unique war potential.
* **Current Status:** As one of the newest members, its history is still largely shrouded in mystery and its integration within the base's dynamics is an ongoing process.
