# Aledge

**Aledge** is a high-ranking [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]] and former founding leader of **SpaceFox**. His figure represents one of the most complex cases of biotypological and narrative evolution within the MasterVerse, his past being closely linked to the metamorphoses of the **Primorde** race.

---

### I. Physical Appearance and Identity
Aledge belongs to the **Primorde** race. Although its final design is currently being revised (in line with the racial makeover which includes smaller dimensions compared to humans and innate electrical powers), its image is characterized by:
* **Somatic features:** Brown hair that follows the style of his real counterpart, on a body whose size is the subject of post-retcon redefinition.
* **Equipment:** Wear the standard MasterBase tactical uniform, complemented by the iconic Master helmet.
* **The Helm Logo:** While he currently bears the MasterBase logo, his helmet is historically crafted to house the **Edmension** symbol. This brand represents the symbolic catalyst for all his extra-MasterBase projects (Late MCL, Edmension, Edmension LOL, PSAF), serving as a visual record of his independent activities.

---

### II. Fighting Style
Aledge has been able to harmonize his origins with the pragmatism of the modern Questar, developing a hybrid skill set:
* **Electrical Magic:** Being a Primorde, Aledge possesses a native bond with electrical energy. In the past, this was his only weapon.
* **Dual Gunblade:** During the human transition period, Aledge integrated two gunblades into his arsenal, transforming himself into a versatile fighter.
* **Synergy:** His current style blends electric projections and ballistic swordsmanship, making him a *battlemage* capable of managing the battlefield with extreme mobility.

---

### III. Operational Role
Within the MasterBase, Aledge holds a crucial tactical function:
* **Battlemage:** Focused on combined magical offensive.
* **Disengager:** Specialist in breaking enemy blocks and managing the tactical withdrawal of allied units, thanks to his ability to generate electrical disturbance fields.

---

### IV. Metatestimony Note: The Metamorphosis of the Primorde
Aledge's story is a reflection of MasterVerse lore:
1. **Archaic Age:** Member of the first version of the Primorde (small lemur-like beings with bladed tails).
2. **Standardization Era:** Total retcon to human form, loss of tail, adoption of gunblades.
3. **Contemporary Era:** Return to the Primordial race. The design is awaiting definitive racial standardization (the restoration of the bladed tail is still being discussed).
This biological instability makes him a unique character, capable of drawing on fighting styles born in different eras of his development.
