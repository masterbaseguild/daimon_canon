#Dave

**Dave** is a high-ranking [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] and a top researcher within the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]]. Recognized as the most experienced ethereal trainer in the guild, Dave has transformed the management of magical creatures into a tactical discipline of the highest precision.

---

### I. Tactical Role: The Ethereal Dominator
Unlike fighters who choose bladed weapons or direct magic, Dave operates as a **strategic summoner**. His fighting style is characterized by the controlled release of entire hosts of magical monsters onto the battlefield.
* **Training:** His deep knowledge of ethereal biology allows him to create symbiotic bonds with creatures of various nature, enhancing their war instinct.
* **Distance Control:** Dave never exposes himself directly to physical contact, preferring to manage the flow of combat through the tactical positioning of his creatures, which act as an extension of his will.

---

### II. Research and Contribution to the MasterBase
In addition to his operational duties, Dave is a cornerstone of MasterBase's research division. His work focuses on the study of ethereals, analyzing how these creatures can be integrated into the guild's security protocols. His expertise is fundamental for:
* **Pattern Analysis:** Study the behavior of ethereal savages to predict their reactions in battle.
* **Development of Containment Protocols:** Create systems to manage magical entities that would otherwise be unmanageable or dangerous to the guild itself.

---

### III. Operational Profile
In battle, Dave's presence is perceived as an asymmetric threat: his opponents face not a single warrior, but a hostile and unpredictable ecosystem that responds to his commands. Dave's ability to simultaneously wield dozens of ethereal entities makes him one of the most feared tacticians, capable of saturating any area with the raw force of the magical fauna he has trained.
