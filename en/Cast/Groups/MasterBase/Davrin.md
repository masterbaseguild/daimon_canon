# Narrative Profile and Personality

### Combat Dynamics
Davrin is a nightmare for enemies in the initial stages of a conflict: he aggressively attacks the battlefield, both in the air and on the ground, alternating between colossal constructs. However, he has a huge visual and tactical deficit: **he must be in close proximity to the metal to control it**, which forces him to ride his creations directly, exposing himself to ranged attacks. Before each battle he analyzes the enemy together with his companions to calculate the exact amount of metal to take with him.

### Power Mechanics: Peak and Passive Phase
His magic combines the [[en/Glossary/Solid Bending|Solid Bending]] ([[en/Items/Magic School of Metal|Metal School]]) and the [[en/Glossary/Soul Magic|Soul/Life Magic]] to instantly manipulate any nearby metallic material, provided you know every structural detail. However, this maneuver requires immense physical and mental effort:
* **Assault Phase:** He can control small constructs for days, but colossal variants reduce his autonomy to a few minutes, leading to temporary blindness from the effort.
* **Passive Phase:** Once his energy is exhausted, Davrin enters a state of total incapacity and partial blindness that lasts from a few minutes to an hour. At this juncture, he gathers all the metal in the "Scarab" build to protect himself, completely entrusting his life to the defense of his guild mates.

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
