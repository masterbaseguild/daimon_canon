# Dragor

**Dragor** is a [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] prominent athlete, member of both the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]] and the [[en/Cast/Groups/Blue Owls/Blue Owls|Blue Owls]]. A multifaceted figure, Dragor embodies the perfect transition between sporting excellence and war effectiveness, operating as a fundamental pillar in the MasterBase athlete management system.

---

### I. Rise and Social Network
Dragor joined the MasterBase during the wave of recruits who arrived at the height of the **Second Master War**, in a period of serious crisis marked by the aural pandemic. Despite the instability of the period, he quickly managed to forge bonds of deep brotherhood with key figures in the organization, including **Alone**, **Cimo**, **Mape** and **Gianco**. Thanks to these connections, he has become one of the most influential members in the management of the virtual sports league on **Novadia**, solidifying his reputation as one of the architects of the Guild's sporting success.

---

### II. Operational Profile: Battle Archer
Although his career is defined by athletic talent, Dragor is no mere sports theorist. On the battlefield, his role is that of **battle archer**:
* **Unfailing Accuracy:** From behind the lines, Dragor is capable of performing ballistic bombardments with surgical precision.
* **Tactical Support:** His ability to "rain bolts" on the enemy acts as an offensive barrier, decimating opposing lines before they can even engage front-line units.
* **Mentorship:** Despite having no conventional combat history in the MasterBase, Dragor uses his extensive biomechanical and athletic knowledge to personally train the Guild's athletes and fighters, optimizing the Masters' physical performance.

---

### III. Legacy and Current Role
Dragor represents the evolution of the modern Questar: no longer just a war machine, but a professional who understands the physiology of movement and the importance of preparation. Whether it's a tournament on Novadia or a territorial skirmish, his presence ensures that every arrow fired and every training plan is guided by flawless technical perfection.
