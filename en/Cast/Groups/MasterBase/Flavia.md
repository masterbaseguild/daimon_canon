# Flavia

**Flavia** is an ex-quester of the **MasterBase** of Gondran origins. Her career has been characterized by a unique and brutal approach to combat, derived from her peculiar physiology which has allowed her to excel in high-intensity combat contexts.

---

## I. Physical Profile and Ability
* **Origin:** Gondraw system.
* **Distinguishing Trait:** It is equipped with tentacles that possess a contraction force and mechanical resistance exceptionally superior to the average of its species. This mutation (or genetic variant) has become his trademark on the battlefield.

## II. Fighting Style
* **Role:** Frontline fighter.
* **Technique:** Instead of relying solely on conventional weapons, Flavia has integrated her tentacles into her offensive repertoire. She is capable of using them to pin down enemies, disarm armored opponents, or generate devastating pressure attacks, making her a formidable opponent in close-quarters combat.

## III. History and Operational Status
* **Career:** Served as a Questor at the **MasterBase**, actively contributing to the guild's defense and offense operations.
* **Current Status:** **Ex-quester**. The reasons behind his departure from MasterBase are not fully documented in public records, leaving room for speculation about his current lifestyle away from the front.
