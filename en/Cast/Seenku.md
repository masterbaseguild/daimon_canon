# Seenku

**Seenku** is one of the most impressive impact forces in **MasterBase**. Member of the **Vulture** race, he combines the natural agility of flight with brutal physical resistance, filling the roles of *Tank* and *Fighter* within tactical teams. Having joined the organization at Aledge's direct invitation, she quickly became the defensive linchpin of high-risk operations.

---

# I. Physical Profile and Appearance

Seenku possesses a stage presence that inspires fear:

* **Race:** Vulture (Vulture). Her physical characteristics inherited from the Avar lineage give her a natural wingspan, enhanced by technological equipment.
* **Somatic features:**
* **Hair:** Long, wavy and unkempt, a bright burnt orange color (![■](https://placehold.co/15x15/f17c39/f17c39)).
* **Eyes:** Gradient earth color (![■](https://placehold.co/15x15/ae8279/ae8279)).
* **Skin:** Light, peach/cream shade (![■](https://placehold.co/15x15/ffc9ad/ffc9ad)).
* **Outfit:** Wears a combination of custom **heavy armor** and the classic **official MasterBase uniform**. Her back is equipped with the **[[en/Items/MasterBase/Master Wing Support|Master Wing Support]]** system, which allows her to support the weight of the armor during flight without sacrificing aerial mobility.

---

# II. Equipment and Fighting Style

Seenku prefers direct confrontation, where his size and kinetic power become unstoppable:

* **Main Armament:** He wields a **Zweihander** (two-handed greatsword) of majestic proportions. The blade is balanced to work in synergy with his brute strength, capable of mowing down enemy formations or breaching kinetic defenses with a single well-placed blow.
* **Dynamic Magic:** Not limited to physical strength; she masters *Dynamic Magic* to optimize her stability in flight during battle, allowing her to "charge" the greatsword's blows with ethereal energy to increase their destructive impact.
* **Combat Skills:** The combination of **Flight** and mastery of the **Zweihander** allows her to instantly switch from an aerial evasive maneuver to a devastating ground lunge, making her an unpredictable opponent.

---

# III. Role in the MasterBase

Like MasterBase *Tank* and *Fighter*:

* **Frontline Defense:** Its role is to absorb enemy fire and act as a human (and armored) shield for more vulnerable comrades.
* **Joining the Staff:** Personally recruited by Aledge, her loyalty to MasterBase is absolute. Aledge saw in her not just a fighter, but a strategic pillar capable of supporting the weight of entire defensive lines.
