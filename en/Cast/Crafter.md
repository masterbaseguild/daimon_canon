### Crafter

#### Origins and the Movement Away from the MasterBase
Crafter is a former [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] of [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]]. His entry into the guild was driven by opportunistic motivations: Crafter had in fact joined with the only real interest in accumulating personal power.

However, his real intentions did not remain hidden for long. Crafter was quickly unmasked by Beat, who, understanding his personal goals, decided to permanently remove him and ban him from MasterBase.
