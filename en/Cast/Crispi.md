### Crispi

#### The Regime in MiddleEarth and Master League Zero
Crispi was the tyrant of MiddleEarth, a faction that managed and governed with an iron fist on behalf of the "Higher Planes". During his time in command, he was the key figure and main contact with whom MasterBase maintained all diplomatic and logistical relations related to the **Master League Zero** collaboration.

However, Crispi's authoritarian methods and ruthless modus operandi did not go unnoticed: observing his management, the Masters began to have serious doubts and perplexities about the actual positivity and ethics of this alliance.

#### The Fall and Closure of MiddleEarth
Crispi's fate was closely linked to the inter-guild event. Immediately after the end of Master League Zero, the MiddleEarth faction (presumably driven by the Higher Plans or by an internal revolt tired of tyranny) managed to definitively get rid of Crispi. Shortly after his deposition, the MiddleEarth guild closed its doors, ceasing to exist.
