#Dry

**Dry** is a high-ranking [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]], founder and former leader of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Lignum Nobili/Lignum Nobili|Lignum Nobili]] guild. An iconic figure for his refined style and his bravado in combat, he represents the aristocratic aesthetics and tactical arrogance that characterize the leaders of his organization.

---

### I. Identity and Style
Dry has been able to transform his presence on the battlefield into a trademark, combining martial efficiency and extremely refined aesthetics:
* **Outfit:** Wear ceremonial and tactical clothing in perfect harmony ("complementary outfit") with that of **Keia**.
* **Iconography:** His status is underlined by a necklace bearing a crown of lin, a distinctive symbol of his lineage and his affiliation with the Lignum Nobili.
* **Armament:** He uses a finely crafted inlaid staff in battle, which serves as both a catalyst for his magical abilities and a symbol of authority.

---

### II. Modus Operandi and Distinctive Traits
Dry is known among Questors for his dismissive attitude towards defeated opponents. His most famous gesture - and at the same time the most humiliating for those who suffer it - consists of **throwing a golden chip** towards the body or position of the opponent he has just beaten. This gesture serves as a signature, a mark of "liquidation" that emphasizes Dry's overwhelming superiority and total lack of respect for those who do not live up to his standard.

---

### III. Role in the Lignum Nobili
As founder and former leader, Dry imbued Lignum Nobili with a vision based on exclusivity and surgical precision. Under his leadership, the guild has adopted engagement protocols that prioritize territorial control and the psychological annihilation of rivals. Although he has relinquished formal leadership, his influence within the structure remains profound, acting as a sort of eminence grise that defines the aesthetic and behavioral standards of the guild.
