_A MasterBase Story_

Summoned by their patrons, the MasterBase, a renowned guild of space adventurers, returns to planet Aura. The Masters will have to defend the Olympus Foundation from the attacks of their rivals, the power-hungry Asgard Foundation, and their newest asset, the infamous duelist Thera. In the meantime, however, they must also investigate on the terrifying leader of the Olympus, Haude, and reveal the secrets he is hiding.
