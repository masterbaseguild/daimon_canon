# Gaia

**Gaia** is a lush, pristine planet located within the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Pyreus System/Pyreus System|Pyreus system]]. Known for its lush biodiversity and constant temperate climate, the planet has been a closed system for millennia, where the local population has developed a civilization based on profound respect for the ecosystem and the search for a spiritual and natural balance.

---

# I. Society and Philosophy

Life on Gaia is marked by natural cycles. The inhabitants have internalized a philosophy of **absolute peace and harmony**, living in symbiosis with the planetary flora and fauna.
* **Cultural Isolationism:** For generations, Gaia-nians showed no interest in what happened outside their atmosphere. The vastness of the Alpha Sphere and the power struggles between the guilds were considered alien and irrelevant concepts.
* **Sustainability:** Every structure on Gaia is built with biodegradable or organic materials, seamlessly integrated into the landscape, making it difficult to distinguish between a human settlement and a natural formation.

---

# II. The Impact of the Guilds

The tranquility of Gaia has been shattered by the expansion of the great guilds of the Alpha Sphere. With the landing of the first commercial and military ships in the Pyreus system, the planet became a target of interest due to its natural resources and strategic position.
* **Culture Shock:** The arrival of extractive technologies, interstellar bureaucracies, and warring factions has created a rift in the social fabric of the planet.
* **The Exodus of Adventurers:** A part of the population, mainly composed of curious young people or restless individuals, reacted to the "peaceful" invasion of the guilds by deciding to abandon their bucolic lifestyle. These individuals, driven by a sense of discovery or the desire to protect their world from the outside, have chosen to join the guilds, embarking on a **life of adventure** among the stars.

---

# III. Role in the MasterVerse

Gaia finds itself in a delicate position today. Despite the desire of most of the population to remain isolated, the planet has become a crossroads for merchants, explorers and refugees.
* **Recruitment Center:** Guilds, intrigued by the locals' physical endurance and deep ecological knowledge, often seek to recruit Gaia-nians for exploration missions into wild environments, where survival skills learned on Gaia become invaluable.
* **Protected Area:** Many of the Gaia-nians who have chosen the life of adventure operate as "external guardians", seeking to stem the pollution or savage exploitation that the guilds inevitably bring with them.
