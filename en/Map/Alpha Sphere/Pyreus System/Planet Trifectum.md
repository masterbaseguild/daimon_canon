# Trifectum

**Trifectum** is a one-of-a-kind habitable planet located in the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Pyreus System/Pyreus System|Pyreus system]]. Its bizarre geography — composed of a vast Pangea, a vast global ocean, and a massive continent suspended magnetically in the atmosphere — has fostered the divergent evolution of three distinct sentient species.

---

# I. The Three Races and the Evolution of Power

For centuries, the planet has been dominated by a brutal hierarchy based on control of the airspace:

* **The Avians ([[en/Race/Radahi|Radahi]]):** Inhabitants of the flying continent, they have exercised unchallenged dominance for generations, preying on other races as inferior species and maintaining control of trade routes and resources from above.
* **The Lamie ([[en/Race/Viverni|Wyverns]]):** Serpentine draconic species that populated the coastal areas and deep caves of Pangea, constantly hunted by the Avians.
* **The Sirens ([[en/Race/Mavei|Mavei]]):** Aquatic race residing in the depths of the global ocean, also a victim of air raids.

---

# II. The Turning Point: The Great Revolt

With the advent of technology, the situation has changed rapidly. The **Lamie** and the **Sirens**, driven by the need for survival, have broken down the geographical and cultural barriers that separated them, forming an unprecedented strategic alliance.

* **The War of Liberation:** Thanks to the combination of the Lamie's serpentine shock power and the Sirens' knowledge of hydro-magnetic systems, the coalition neutralized the Avians' strategic advantages.
* **The Reversal:** In a series of decisive campaigns, Allied forces overthrew the Radahi's air supremacy, forcing them to negotiate a surrender that ended centuries of enslavement and persecution.

---

# III. The Era of Peace and Integration in the Questor

Today, Trifectum experiences a peace rebuilt on mutual understanding. The past of domination has become a warning, and the three races actively collaborate to manage the planet's unique ecosystem.

* **The Arrival of the Questors:** When the **Questers** guild arrived on Trifectum, they found a civilization that was mature and aware of the cost of freedom. The three races no longer see strangers as threats, but as opportunities.
* **Tripartite Recruitment:** Currently, Questors draw from all three populations:
* The **Radahi** offer their unrivaled aerial reconnaissance abilities.
* **Wyverns** serve as kinetic strike forces in hostile environments.
* **Mavei** are experts in navigation, logistics and resource management under extreme pressure conditions.
