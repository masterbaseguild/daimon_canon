# Quitera

**Quitera** is a habitable planet located in [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Pyreus System/Pyreus System|Pyreus system]]. The planet is characterized by a clear geographical dichotomy: two continents separated by immense ocean basins, each dominated by one of the two native sentient species: the **Termi** (canine race descended from jackals) and the **Quiladi** (feline race descended from jaguars).

---

# I. Geography and Conflict

The planet is the theater of a **perennial war** that has lasted for countless generations. The conflict is not only territorial, but reflects profound biological and philosophical differences between the two species.

* **Northern Continent (Termi Domain):** A land of arid plains, rocky plateaus and dense undergrowth. The Thermi, organized into packs and tribal coalitions, have transformed survival into a tactical science.
* **Southern Continent (Quilad Domain):** An expanse of monumental rainforests and mountain peaks. The Quiladi have built complex arboreal societies there, based on clans of hunter-architects.
* **The Eternal War:** The rivalry between the two continents is fueled by ancestral legends and clashes for control of the sea routes that connect the few neutral fertile areas. Tribal alliances form and dissolve with dizzying speed, keeping the planet in a constant state of warlike tension.

---

# II. The Arrival of the Questors

The arrival of the interstellar ships of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Quester|Questor's guild]] represented an event of epochal disruption for the precarious balance of Quitera. The introduction of advanced technology and a galactic perspective radically changed the two races' perception of their eternal conflict.

* **The New Way:** Despite the war, some individuals—tired of the endless cycle of bloodshed or eager to understand the wonders of the Alpha Sphere—have chosen to abandon their side.
* **The Union:** Many of these deserters have decided to join the Questars. For these individuals, the guild is not just a job, but an escape that allows them to put into practice the hunter and warrior skills learned over millennia of conflict on Quitera in galactic contexts.

---

# III. Role in the MasterVerse

Quitera is monitored with caution today. The Questors' Guild uses the planet as a unique natural training ground, where recruiters seek out the brightest minds and fiercest warriors among the Thermi and Quiladi.

* **Technological Influence:** The presence of the Questors has created "truce zones" around the spaceports, where, due to logistical necessity, members of both races find themselves having to work side by side, leading to latent tensions but also to unexpected forms of pragmatic cooperation.
