# Nobi system

The **Nobi System** is a planetary complex located within the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Alpha Sphere|Alpha Sphere]], which orbits the star Nobi. Although star maps indicate the presence of various celestial bodies, the system is currently considered a "dead zone" for research and colonization operations, following the cataclysm that struck its only known habitable world: [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Nobi System/Planet Iphiridia/Planet Iphiridia|Iphiridia]].

---

# I. System Structure

* **Nobi Star:** The central star of the system, around which the various celestial bodies rotate.
* **Planet Iphiridia:** The only planet confirmed as habitable. His current condition is critical due to catastrophic biological events in the past.
* **Other Celestial Bodies:** There are other planets in the system, but their habitability or geological characteristics remain shrouded in uncertainty. Complete mapping operations were suspended indefinitely as, after the Iphiridia disaster, the system was left without logistical support points, making any exploration mission excessively risky.

---

# II. The Disaster of Iphiridia

The story of Iphiridia is a bitter lesson on the fragility of civilizations. The planet was once home to an ancient and thriving civilization, whose architectural and technological remains today lie abandoned under a blanket of silence.

* **The Plague:** Civilization has been annihilated by a virulent **zombie plague** that has spread with unstoppable speed, infecting the entire population.
* **Current Status:** There is no longer any trace of human inhabitants on the planet. Iphiridia is now a desolate world, an open-air funerary monument where the infrastructure of the ancients has been reclaimed by time and the infected who wander the ruins.

---

# III. Note on Operational Restrictions

For reasons of preserving the integrity of the lore and in compliance with the narrative constraints imposed by the planet's affiliation with external interactive projects, the Nobi System is subject to severe exploration limitations:

> ### ── Archive Note ──
> *«The Nobi System is not a place for fortune seekers. Iphiridia is a reminder of what happens when a civilization loses control of its own biology. Until a safe foothold is established—or until the threat on Iphiridia is neutralized—the system remains off-limits to any Alpha Sphere operations.”* — MasterBase security protocol.

---

# IV. Future prospects

Although research is currently stalled, interest in the Nobi System remains alive in certain academic circles. The idea that there are other potentially habitable planets in this system continues to fuel speculation, but without a base of operations on Iphiridia, any attempt to expand to the outer planets of the Nobi system is currently considered a suicidal undertaking.
