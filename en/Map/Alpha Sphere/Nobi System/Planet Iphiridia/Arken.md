#Arken

**Arken** is a historic city located on the planet [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Nobi System/Planet Iphiridia/Planet Iphiridia|Iphiridia]]. Suspended between the memories of a royal past and the wounds of the zombie cataclysm, Arken represents one of the few settlements capable of constantly rising from its ashes. Today it is primarily known as MasterBase's operational stronghold on the planet.

---

# I. Historical Chronology

Arken's story is divided into four crucial phases, which reflect the political transformations of Iphiridia:

1. **Royal Foundation:** The origins of the city date back to the laying of the foundation stone by the [[en/Cast/Deceased/King Aegift|King Aegift]], who chose the main peak as a strategic position to erect an impregnable fortress.
2. **The Anark Age:** Centuries later, the [[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/Anark|Anark]] guild located the ruins of the original fortress. Through a massive restoration project, the Anark transformed the gloomy fortress into a truly habitable city, expanding its walls and infrastructure.
3. **The Collapse:** Following the [[en/Timeline/Chronicles of Iphiridia|Iphiridia disaster]], Arken was not spared from the chaos. The city fell into ruin, abandoned and overwhelmed by the infected hordes that infested the planet.
4. **Master Rebirth:** In recent years, MasterBase has identified Arken as the ideal site for its headquarters. The city was once again restored, fortified, and repopulated to support the guild's operations on Iphiridia.

---

# II. Organization and Defense

To preserve order and protect operations in such a hostile environment, security management is entrusted to a dedicated structure:

* **Aegift Division:** In homage to the ancient founding ruler, Arken's garrison corps has been named the **Aegift Division**. This elite unit is responsible for patrolling the walls, controlling gates, and protecting civilians and Masters operating within the city.

---

# III. Strategic Role

Arken acts as a lung for the presence of the MasterBase on Iphiridia. Being one of the few strongholds "sanitized" by the plague, the city is the nerve center from which expeditions depart towards the infected areas and where the survivors of the explorations take refuge.

> *«Arken's stones are reminiscent of King Aegift, but his walls today belong to the MasterBase. It is a place where the history of the past and the struggle for the future meet every day under the watchful eye of the Aegift Division.”* — Excerpt from the MasterBase records.

---

# IV. Current Status

Currently, Arken is a fortress city on high alert. While the outer reaches of Iphiridia remain infested, Arken's perimeter is protected by advanced barrier technologies and constant vigilance. It is the "home" of the Masters on the planet, a symbol of resistance in a world that seems unwilling to grant any respite to the living.
