# Iphiridia

**Iphiridia** is a habitable planet located in [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Nobi System/Nobi System|Nobi system]]. Characterized by a complex morphology that includes an orbiting asteroid belt and a vast semi-viable subsurface, Iphiridia was an early stage in extra-systemic aural colonization. However, its history is marked by cycles of mass extinction and an unstoppable zombie plague that has made it a hostile and desolate world.

---

# I. Historical and Evolutionary Stratification

The history of Iphiridia is a succession of eras marked by biological ascents and climatic cataclysms that have shaped the face of the planet:

* **Age of the Ornithoids:** Rulers of the planet for centuries, exterminated by a climate disaster.
* **Age of the Sauroids:** Rulers of the underworld, also erased by a global environmental change.
* **Age of the Suinoids (Keroids):** Builders of temples and cities, pioneers of the passages to the underworld. A rise in the seas submerged their works, forcing them into exile in the depths.
* **Age of the Humanoids (Stemiates):** An advanced civilization that has fortified the gateways to the asteroids and the underworld. Pirates, desert, jungle and northern tribes have marked the landscape with their architecture.
* **Era of the Rhinoids (Testians) and the Forsworn:** A peaceful culture that faced the internal split of the "Forsworn", a violent faction eager for revenge against the humanoids.

---

# II. The Cataclysm and the Plague

The end of civilization on Iphiridia was brought about by the nefarious intervention of Forsworn sorcerers, who used **subterranean totems** to spread a zombie plague.
* **Origin of the Plague:** It is hypothesized that the plague was a pre-existing entity, "animated" and enhanced by the substance known as **Xadrium**.
* **Consequences:** The humanoids, to escape the contagion, fled towards the asteroids. Permanence in those environments has altered their essence, transforming them into a new transcendent race. The Rhinoids attempted to resist by creating **Iron Golems**, but the plague spared no one, leading the Forsworn to rampage against their own kind.

---

# III. Colonization Attempts and the "Iphiridia Disaster"

Iphiridia was the scene of titanic reclamation efforts, often ending in tragedy:

1. **Operation Nexus (2017):** The [[en/Cast/Groups/Nexus|Nexus Guild]] issued a call to all Systems Guilds to transform Iphiridia into a federation of colonies.
2. **The Disaster of Iphiridia:** A mysterious and sudden increase in the aggressiveness of the zombies led to the collapse of the defenses. The lack of evacuation infrastructure turned the operation into a massacre.
3. **The Failure of the MasterBase:** After the collapse of the Nexus, other Guilds (including the MasterBase) attempted to settle, but the constant rivalries and hostility endemic to the planet forced the Masters to abandon the project definitively to search for new worlds.
