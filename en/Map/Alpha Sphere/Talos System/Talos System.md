### System Description
Located within the **[[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Alpha Sphere|Alpha Sphere]]**, the Talos System orbits the central star of the same name. The system includes eight planets and numerous asteroid fields. Although **Aura** is the only naturally habitable planet, the entire system is heavily colonized and bustling with activity.

### Organization and Infrastructure
The system serves as a hub for trade and communications:
* **Central Hub:** [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/Planet Aura/Planet Aura|Aura]] serves as the hub for a vast transportation and communications network.
* **Colonization:** The more hospitable planets are home to numerous settlements, while the resource-rich ones are dotted with mining outposts and logistics hubs for aural cargo routes.
* **Deep Space:** Talos' interplanetary space is densely populated by independent [[en/Glossary/StarBase|starbase]], corporate fleets, intersystem transportation networks, and space patrols. [[en/Cast/Groups/Aura United Nations|Aural government]].
* **[[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/AUN Network|AUN network]]:** The system is integrated into the AUN network, which serves as the backbone for logistics and information management.

### Society and Culture
The social structure presents a clear distinction between the common population and the operational elites:
* **The Middle Aural:** Despite the profound connectivity of the system, the life of the average individual remains confined to their home planet. Most citizens do not have the means, nor the interest, to undertake interplanetary travel.
* **Higher Level:** True intercultural exchange takes place exclusively in the circles of **[[en/Glossary/Guild|guilds]]**, **Corporations** and **Government Institutions**. It is in these high-profile areas that the presence of individuals from different planets is constant, making the social landscape of cutting-edge infrastructures (such as StarBase) a multicultural crossroads.
