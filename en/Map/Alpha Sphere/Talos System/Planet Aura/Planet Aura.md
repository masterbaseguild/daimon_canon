# Aura

**Aura** is a habitable planet of considerable importance located within the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/Talos System|Talos system]]. Thanks to its resources and the stability of its ecosystem, it hosts settlements and strategic outposts of some of the most influential organizations in the galaxy. It is a world in which a complex historical evolution is reflected in the coexistence between the natural biosphere and the impressive technological infrastructures of the large Foundations.

---

# Historical Notes and Political Evolution

The origins of civilization on Aura are rooted in the ancient, generational conflict between the peoples of the planets **[[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Turma System/Planet Cathum|Cathum]]** and **[[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Sophia System/Planet Cyria|Cyria]]**. During the war, both factions install warp gates in their respective systems and discover Aura, starting its colonization to collect resources and effectively moving the theater of battle to this world.

The geopolitical turning point occurs when a group of settlers defectes from both sides to found a pacifist colony on a protected island. From the union of these members the **[[en/Races/Aural|Aural race]]** was born. After repeated and unsuccessful invasion attempts by the two parent powers, Cathum and Cyria give up, leading the planet to take on a triple-state structure. Thanks to the subsequent diplomatic mediation of the Aural nation, the three peoples stipulate a peace pact that opens the borders. Over time, the three races mix between the various planets, and the Aurals rapidly multiply to become the majority population on the planet.

With the subsequent fragmentation of Cathum and Cyria into various sub-nations, **[[en/Cast/Groups/Aura United Nations|AUN (Aura United Nations)]]** was founded with the aim of definitively overcoming the ancient historical gaps.

# I. The [[en/Glossary/Guild|guilds]] Phenomenon and the Modern Age

Under the aegis of the AUN, guilds spread: independent organizations regulated by a code of honor, created to assist in maintaining order and support the expansion of the AUN into non-civilized territories. However, the progressive growth of their logistical and military power undermines this balance; the guilds that abuse their position become impossible to contain for the AUN alone, undergoing a gradual transformation into pseudo-criminal organizations.

In the modern era, the **[[en/Items/Olympus Foundation/Olympus Foundation|Olympus]]** guild arises with the intent of restoring the ancient code of honor. Thanks to the virtuousness of his goals, he receives the full support of the AUN and expands to become the largest guild in the world. This rapid rise, however, scares AUN itself, which begins to see Olympus as a potential threat to global stability. Aware of this tension, [[en/Rodras.]], the then leader of Olympus, initiated a policy of strategic downsizing, donating a large part of its resources to **[[en/Cast/Groups/Asgard Foundation|Asgard]]**, a new guild founded by a close ally of his.

Currently, Olympus, Asgard and the AUN are bound by a mutual control pact: neither of the two guilds can rearm beyond the established limits and, should one of the two violate the agreement, the AUN will actively collaborate with the other to restore the geopolitical balance.

# II. Relevant Places

Aura serves as an important operational and diplomatic hub in the system. The main structures on the planet reflect the current political structure:

* **[[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/Planet Aura/Olympus Headquarters|Olympus Headquarters]]**: The administrative and commercial stronghold of the Olympus Foundation, the heart of its logistics operations and restoration projects.
* **[[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/Planet Aura/Asgard Headquarters|Asgard Headquarters]]**: The scientific and technological hub of the Asgard Foundation, which grew rapidly thanks to the transfer of resources agreed with Olympus.
* **[[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/Planet Aura/MasterHall|MasterHall]]**: The aural colony and operational base of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]], used as a reference point for missions in the Talos System and a rest center for [[en/Glossary/Master|Master]].

# III. Guild Specializations

Aura's operational landscape is characterized by organizations that do not follow a rigid taxonomy, but rather distinguish themselves based on their functional specializations. Many factions operate multifacetedly on multiple fronts, while others focus on a single objective.

| Category | Description |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Public Order | Factions dedicated to maintaining public order and protecting the treaties of the AUN, often supported by the virtuous guilds.                |
| Business and Economy | Companies specialized in the management of trade flows, planetary resources and technological investments.                           |
| Engineering and Research | Organizations whose main focus lies in scientific development and technological innovation.                                        |
| Intelligence | Networks specializing in information retrieval, data security and shadow operations between various power blocs.                 |
| Crime | Degenerate guilds and cartels that exploit their influence for smuggling or illicit activities, distant from the ancient code of honor. |
| Vigilantes | Groups or former members of other factions who choose to operate outside the official channels of the AUN to act in less protected territories.    |
