# MasterHall

The **MasterHall** is the official aural colony and base of operations of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]] located on the planet **Aura**, within the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/Talos System|Talos system]]. More than just a garrison, the facility serves as a key hub for the guild's operations, offering a unique mix of high-tech weapons infrastructure and services dedicated to member well-being.

---

# I. Operational Function

As the main base on Aura, the MasterHall coordinates all MasterBase activities in the Talos system.
* **Logistics Node:** The structure is perfectly integrated with the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/AUN Network|AUN Network]], allowing rapid deployment of troops and the constant supply of materials necessary for missions.
* **Strategic Control:** From its command center, MasterBase leaders monitor the sectors under their jurisdiction, planning patrol operations and the protection of trade routes vital to Aura.

---

# II. The Leisure Center: A Parenthesis of Peace

What distinguishes MasterHall from any other military colony in the system is its vocation for rest. Aware of the extreme stress to which Masters are subjected during war campaigns, the structure integrates a high-level **Leisure Center**, designed to promote physical and psychological recovery:
* **Regeneration Zones:** Wellness centers based on cellular stimulation technologies that accelerate healing after combat.
* **Recreational Areas:** Rooms for tactical simulation games, libraries of historical data and common spaces where Masters on leave can socialize, far from the tension of active missions.

---

# III. Importance for the Aura System

The presence of the MasterHall on Aura not only guarantees security, but also represents a center of stability for the local population. The colony has become the center of social life for the allied forces present on the planet, creating a bridge between the strict military discipline of the guild and the needs of the fighters for rest.

> ### ── Archive Note ──
> *«The MasterHall is the only place where a Master can feel like a front-line soldier and a retired civilian at the same time. It is our safe haven, the place where the war ends as soon as we enter the titanium gates.”* — Excerpt from the diary of a MasterBase veteran.

---

# IV. Current Status

The MasterHall is constantly operational, with a continuous influx of Masters returning from missions in the Talos system. The guild continues to invest in the expansion of the Leisure Center to ensure that every member, regardless of rank, has access to an environment where they can regain the mental balance needed to face the challenges ahead.
