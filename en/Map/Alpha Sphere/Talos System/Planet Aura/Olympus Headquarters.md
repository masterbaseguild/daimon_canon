# Olympus Headquarters

The **Olympus Headquarters** is the monumental structure that serves as the administrative and operational headquarters of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Olympus Foundation/Olympus Foundation|Olympus Foundation]]. Strategically located on the grounds of **Aura**, in [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/Talos System|Talos system]], the complex represents the Foundation's technological ambition and influence in the Alpha Sphere.

---

# I. Architecture and Meaning

Unlike other research facilities on Aura, Olympus HQ stands out for its architecture that evokes grandeur and solidity. Its structure is designed to be a bulwark:
* **Command Center:** Houses the central offices where the global policies of the Olympus Foundation are set.
* **Surveillance Towers:** The site is surrounded by technologically advanced towers that monitor not only the surrounding territory, but also communications in and out of the Talos system.
* **Symbolism:** The choice to settle on Aura — sharing planetary space with the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/Planet Aura/Asgard Headquarters|Asgard Foundation]] and the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/Planet Aura/MasterHall|MasterHall]] — places the Olympus Foundation at the center of the geopolitical game of the system.

---

# II. Operational Functions

The complex is not just an office, but a multifunctional body dedicated to maintaining the Foundation's objectives:

1. **Resource Management:** Given the commercial and research nature of Olympus, HQ manages the data and capital flows that connect the various branches spread across the Alpha Sphere.
2. **Research and Development Center:** In the underground levels there are the high engineering laboratories, where the Foundation studies artifacts and technologies recovered during explorations.
3. **Diplomatic Point:** The facility serves as an embassy for negotiations with other factions, offering dedicated spaces for high-level meetings in a protected and neutral environment.

---

# III. Role in the Balance of the Talos System

The presence of Olympus HQ on Aura creates a unique triangle of influence with other entities on the planet. The Olympus Foundation plays a strategic counterweight role, acting as a force that balances the scientific interests of Asgard and the more operational/military interests of MasterBase.

> ### ── Archive Note ──
> *«Olympus Headquarters is a fortress of data and ambition. Anyone who enters those offices knows that their destiny is subject to precise calculations and long-term planning."* — Intelligence analysis of the Talos system.

---

# IV. Current Status

The site is constantly monitored and the encryption and defense systems are constantly being updated. As activity in the Talos system has grown, Olympus HQ has become an indispensable crossroads for the Foundation, serving as a primary hub for crisis management and the expansion of its interests to the frontiers of the system.
