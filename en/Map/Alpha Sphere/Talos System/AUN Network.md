# AUNNetwork

The **AUN Network** represents the complex system of commercial and military nodes managed by the [[en/Cast/Groups/Aura United Nations|Aura United Nations]] (AUN). Stretching widely across the entire [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/Talos System|Talos system]], the network ensures the AUN's logistical hegemony, connecting peripheral colonies to central command centers and ensuring a constant flow of resources essential to the stability of the entire jurisdiction.

---

# I. Network Architecture

Each node of the AUN Network is designed as a **self-sufficient** entity, capable of operating autonomously even in the event of prolonged isolation from the central nodes. The infrastructure is diversified based on the nature of the celestial bodies colonized:

* **Terrestrial Nodes (Solid Planets):** On solid planets, the node takes the form of a **self-sufficient city** erected directly on the surface. These metropolises are equipped with mining facilities, hydroponic greenhouses, water purification systems, and heat generators (crucial in the coldest sectors of the Talos system).
* **Orbiting Nodes (Gasey Planets):** In the case of gas giants or planets without a habitable crust, the node consists of an orbiting **[[en/Glossary/StarBase|starbase]]**. These stations suspended in the atmosphere or in high orbit capture noble gases and rare isotopes, while serving as refueling hubs and military garrisons for surrounding trade routes.

---

# II. Strategic Functions

The AUN Network is not just an extraction network, but the pillar on which the entire power projection of the AUN rests:

1. **Resource Extraction:** Each node specializes in the extraction of local raw materials, which are processed and sent to the industrial centers of the AUN.
2. **Life Support:** Each colony is equipped with advanced technologies for the generation of food, water, oxygen and heat, ensuring the survival of the colonists even in prohibitive environmental conditions.
3. **Military Garrison:** Since the network is managed directly by AUN forces, each node acts as a garrison. Starbases and colony cities are equipped with perimeter defense systems, hangars for patrol aircraft and encrypted communications systems.

---

# III. Importance in the Talos System

The Talos system, due to its vastness and diversity of celestial bodies, requires widespread management that only the AUN Network can offer. The network acts as a glue between different populations and affiliated guilds, allowing the AUN to monitor every activity in the system in real time.

---

# IV. Current Status

Currently, the AUN Network is experiencing a period of expansion, with the creation of new mining nodes in the unexplored regions of the Talos system. Maintenance of the network is one of the top priorities of the AUN, which employs fleets of elite engineers and guards to ensure the flow of resources is uninterrupted, especially as interstellar tensions in the border regions increase.
