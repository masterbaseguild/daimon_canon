The **Arpeggio** is an impressive [[en/Glossary/StarBase|starbase]] of aural manufacture located within the strategic boundaries of the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Alpha Sphere|Alpha Sphere]]. Born as an orbiting fortress and coordination hub for the Navirad crew, the station went down in history for having altered the political balance of the Alpha Sphere, acting as a clandestine sanctuary for political dissidents during the darkest days of the internal fracture of the Masters.

---

# I. History and Foundation (2020)

The station officially opens in **2020** under the supervision and leadership of [[en/Glossary/Master|Master]] [[en/Cast/Groups/Navirad/Kurymi|Kurymi]]. Designed using the advanced naval engineering of the planet Aura, Arpeggio was originally conceived as the orbiting headquarters and primary gathering point for veterans and survivors of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Navirad/Navirad|Navirad]].

Since its first months of activity, the command bridges and tactical sectors of the StarBase have hosted the elite of the faction, seeing the constant presence of key figures of the caliber of [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Winter|Winter]], [[en/Cast/Groups/Navirad/Black Ace|Black Ace]] and the chief engineer [[en/Cast/Groups/Navirad/Gastic Nox|Gastic Nox]]. Under this leadership, the station operates as an independent heavy cruiser with high technological potential.

---

# II. The Sanctuary of Exile (2021)

Arpeggio's geopolitical role changes drastically in **2021**. Following the violent [[en/Timeline/Uprising|Internal Crisis of the MasterBase]] (culminating in the *September Rebellion*), the supreme leader Entity tightens control over his flagship, relieving Alone of his duties and exiling en masse all the Questors and Masters who had taken part in the revolt.

In open opposition to Entity's authoritarian drift, Kurymi exercises her total sovereignty over the Arpeggio:
* StarBase opens its docking doors, offering **political asylum and safe haven to all exiled Masters**.
* For several months, the Arpeggio transformed into the logistical center of dissidence, allowing the rebels to reorganize, treat their wounded and lay the ideological foundations for what would become the independent guild of the *Blue Owls*. This maneuver makes the Arpeggio one of the priority targets of the secret services and subsequent mercenary reprisals (such as the *CC3* boarding siege during the *Redemption* era).

---

# III. The Conflicts of 2023 and the Current Status Quo

In **2023**, the Arpeggio is shaken by a series of profound and exhausting internal conflicts, linked to the change in the objectives of its founders and the progressive integration of a large part of the Navirad crew within the Global Master Fleet.

Following these strong tensions and the decision to follow the dream of freeing Iphiridia, both commander Kurymi and firebender Winter **definitely abandon the Arpeggio**, transferring their war potential aboard Entity's flagship.

### The Mystery of Gastic Nox
With the departure of the historic leaders, absolute control and ownership of StarBase is assumed entirely by **Gastic Nox**. From that moment, the chief engineer has armored the station's communications, cutting ties with official diplomatic channels.
