# Lignum Nobili (StarBase)

The **Lignum Nobili** is a large starbase, the pride of the aural manufacture, which strategically orbits within the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Alpha Sphere|Alpha Sphere]]. It is not just a commercial or military outpost, but the headquarters of the namesake [[en/Cast/Groups/Lignum Nobili/Lignum Nobili|guild]]. The station is famous throughout the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/Talos System|Talos system]] as the most exclusive and dangerous casino in the region.

---

# I. Aesthetics and Atmosphere

The interior of the station reflects the soul of the guild: an austere elegance that mixes opulence and discipline.
* **Design:** The interiors are characterized by the use of **precious dark wood and gold inserts**, creating a color contrast that recalls classic luxury aesthetics with a touch of theatricality (*Kakegurui* inspiration).
* **Service:** The dining room staff is made up of **oriental waiters** who move between the gaming tables with millimeter precision.
* **Code of Conduct:** All guild members wear strictly identical uniforms, but each member adds a distinctive **personal gimmick**, reflecting their individual role or philosophy within the hierarchy.

# III. Marto's Paradox and Selection Tournaments

Managing second place from "Knight" is one of the most critical issues for the guild's leadership. **Marto**'s tendency to operate alone, defying death in every battle, pushed Lignum Nobili to an unconventional solution:

The organization of periodic **skill tournaments** within the upper floors of the starbase.
* **The Objective:** Find a fighter worthy of joining Marto, hoping to finally be able to balance him on the battlefield.
* **The Problem:** Marto personally participates in almost every tournament, "tryharding" and defeating every possible suitor. So far, no one has managed to surpass him, leaving the position vacant and Marto's fate hanging dangerously by a thread.

---

# IV. Alignment and Philosophy

Although the facade of Lignum Nobili is that of a guild dedicated to gambling and profiteering, their alignment is considered **Chaotic Good**. Their unpredictable nature, combined with a strict internal code of honor, often leads them to intervene in the affairs of the Talos system to correct injustices in ways that are as spectacular as they are risky.
