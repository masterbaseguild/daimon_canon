# Gondraw system

The **Gondraw System** is a gravitational pole orbiting the star of the same name, composed of three habitable planets — **Eaclar**, **Rose** and **Zawa** — surrounded by a rich asteroid belt. Historically a cradle of expansion, the system is now governed by a central monarchy based on Zawa, whose authority is steadily eroded by the rise of corporate-criminal powers known as **Trade Houses**.

---

# I. Planetary Geography and History

The Gondrano people, a reptilian species with a characteristic tail called *Claw*, have lived a history marked by the intensive exploitation of resources:

* **The Cycle of Consumption:** After draining Zawa, the Gondrans colonized Eaclar and Rose through centuries of bloody warfare. Once the resources of these worlds were also exhausted, attention shifted to the asteroid belt.
* **The Evolution of the Race:** The Gondrans practice annual moulting, where the new skin adapts to the physiological needs dictated by the environment experienced, a trait that has allowed them to survive in extreme conditions.
* **The Current Situation:** Gondran colonialism has been replaced by a coexistence between the three populations, but stability is threatened by the Trade Houses, which control the asteroid belt's mining economy.

---

# II. The Trade Houses and the Franky Family

The monarchy's decision to privatize asteroid mining has created a bureaucratic and criminal monster. Trade Houses operate as legitimate corporations by day and mafia organizations by night.

* **Franky Family:** The most influential and dangerous, known for systematically using *Hypers* (enhanced individuals) as gangsters to extend their dominance. Prominent members include Garo, Zbb, Bbz and Nekta.
* **Power Conflict:** Families constantly attempt to undermine the monarchy. The Franky family, in particular, has sought outside deals (such as the failed **Operation Cyan-Alley** on Aura) to gain absolute control of the system.

---

# III. The Desert and Non-Being

The main planet, transformed into a desert land by wild exploitation, hides a metaphysical danger:

* **Reality Tears:** Tears in the fabric of space caused by the instability of the local mineral, **Spatialite**. These tears lead to **Non-Being**, a "non-place" where the victim's mind builds lucid dreams before falling into madness.
* **The Veiled:** Those who cross the rifts emerge transfigured, with a corrupted psyche, ready to spread the word of the surreality of Non-Being to every intelligent being they encounter.
* **The Gondran Moon:** The system's industrial hub, where radiation from mined materials turns residents' skin pitch black. Here control of the factories is firmly in the hands of the underworld.

---

# IV. Military and Political Culture

* **Art of War:** Gondran military culture is refined and lethal, based on the use of **Katana, Shuriken, Kama, Kunai and Staves**.
* **The Factions:** The Royal Family attempts to maintain control, supported by figures such as **Emily Rose**, a renegade Master who has sworn protection to the crown in opposition to her own family's speculative interests.
* **The Master Context:** Many Masters have been involved in Gondraw's feuds. Garo's recent defection from the Franky in favor of MasterBase, during the failure of Operation Cyan-Alley, has altered the balance of power in the system.
