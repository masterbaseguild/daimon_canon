**Planet Syteel** is a thriving, habitable, and strategically crucial world located within the [[en/Map/Beta Sphere/Radium System/Radium System|Radium system]]. Characterized by a strong contrast between the medieval technological development of its native populations and the futuristic installations of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]], the planet holds ancient secrets linked to temporal paradoxes and buried civilizations.

---

# I. Timeline and Origins (Syteel's Paradox)

In the beginning, Syteel was a barren planet: a habitable shell but totally devoid of biological or auric life. Before evolution could take its natural course, an event occurred that changed the fate of the system forever.

A group of Masters from a futuristic alternate timeline, desperate to escape the destruction of their time plane, used an experimental time machine to travel back through the millennia. The chronological leap dumped them on Syteel in the primordial era. The details of their attempt to alter the original history were lost, but their technology, their aural energy and their remains merged with the original planet, artificially triggering the birth of life and the first mystical civilizations, transforming Syteel into a gigantic living paradox.

---

# II. The Status Quo and the Arrival of the Masters

Before the arrival of the Masters' fleet, Syteel was in a state of **severe technological backwardness**, comparable to Earth's medieval era, dominated by constant feudal wars, slave empires and oceanic piracy.

Following the loss or repair of their flagship, the Masters located the planet in the Radium System and decided to colonize it, establishing their planetary base of operations there. The guild's first and most important tactical outpost is the city of **New Archen**, strategically built on **Tirsia Island**, a frontier land located in the heart of the pirate route, now converted into the political and military heart of the faction.

---

# III. Geographic Mapping and Regions

Syteel is divided into five macro-geographical areas, each characterized by unique ecosystems and political dynamics:

### 1. Exitia
The region of direct influence controlled militarily by the **Masters**. Developed around the capital New Archen and the Isle of Tirsia, it is the only area on the planet to have cutting-edge energy technologies, auric shields and heavy repair industries for the fleet's starbases.

### 2. Azemea
An evocative and unstable archipelago of **flying islands** that float above the skies of Syteel thanks to the density of anti-gravity minerals underground. It is the exclusive native territory of legendary species, entirely inhabited by fiercely territorial dragons and draconids.

### 3. Mordova
An endless and violent oceanic area that surrounds the main continents. Mordova is entirely populated and divided between fleets of **pirates and corsairs** in constant war with each other. The main objective of the maritime factions is the recovery and smuggling of mysterious submerged artifacts, presumably linked to the Masters of the lost timeline.

### 4. Axeda (The Ar'Dvar Desert)
A merciless and boundless desert region. Survival in the area is guaranteed by the presence of the **Adverna League** (or *Advernian League*), a dense coalition of fortified city-states born around natural oases. The undisputed capital of this coalition is the fortress city of **Dvernia**.

### 5. The Underground (The Network of Tunnels)
A mammoth and suffocating network of tunnels, caves and underground tunnels that extends beneath the entire planetary crust. This underground labyrinth is the beating heart of a brutal economy of exploitation, where entire native populations live in a state of total slavery, forced to dig to extract the planet's raw materials.

# IV. Anthropology and People by Syteel

| People / Race | Description and Geopolitical Status |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Saiti (Sytans)** | The ancient and mystically advanced original inhabitants of the planet, whose biological traces are now almost extinct, but whose hi-tech finds are the subject of study. |
| **Aelfirth Empire** | A powerful, ruthless and aristocratic imperial faction. At the time of the ancient capital *New Babel*, the Aelfirth were famous for having perpetrated the mass enslavement of the other ethnic groups on the planet. |
| **Adverni (Adverna League)** | Proud and resilient people of the Ar'Dvar Desert. United in the Advernian League, they organized themselves into city-states and cooperated with the Master resistance and the people of *Xendar* to found Dvernia, historically rebelling against the slavery imposed by the Aelfirth Empire. |
| **Kreyfurians** | Syteel's native warrior species, known for its great physical resistance and adaptability to the planet's hostile climates. |
| **Paqinradi** | Native population characterized by deep tribal or feudal traditions, located mainly in the rural and external areas of Axeda and Mordova. |
| **Myroids** | Unique beings or constructs connected to the nature of the underground or ancient technologies forgotten in Syteel's Paradox. |
