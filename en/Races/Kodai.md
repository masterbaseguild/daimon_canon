# Kodai

The **Kodai** are a human race native to the planet **Zawa**, a world located in the Gondora system. Immediately distinguishable by their physical grandeur, the Kodai have developed a warrior culture deeply linked to their unique physiology, which allows them to manipulate light through their hair.

---

# I. Physical and Biological Characteristics

The Kodai appear as individuals of **large stature**, with a robust build that reflects the environmental conditions of Zawa. Their most extraordinary feature, however, lies in their hair system: a Kodai's hair possesses properties of **photon magic**.

This biological peculiarity transforms their hair into a truly magical catalyst. Thanks to this innate gift, Kodai can channel ambient light energy for defensive or offensive purposes, manifesting complex spells through precise movements of the head or hands.

---

# II. Art of Combat and Traditions

Since the power of photon spells is directly proportional to the length and density of the hair, the Kodai have developed a rigorous tradition related to the care and management of their hair.

* **The Warrior Braid:** It is a universal custom among the Kodai to let their hair grow to considerable lengths, then gathering it into a **complex and robust braid**. This braid is not just an ornament, but the main combat tool: it is used as an extension of the body to channel magical energy, allowing the warrior to generate focused focal projections.
* **War Versatility:** In battle, a Kodai can use its braid to:
* **Light Barriers:** Create shields of solid light to block projectiles or energy attacks.
* **Scoring Rays:** Focus photon energy through the tip of the braid to emit bolts of high-temperature light, capable of piercing enemy armor.

---

# III. Culture and Society

Managing your hair is considered a sacred act. A Kodai who undergoes a forced haircut or who cannot maintain his or her braid is seen as an individual who has temporarily lost his or her connection to Zawa's magic. As a result, braiding techniques are a fundamental part of their martial training from a young age: an experienced Kodai knows how to braid their hair to maximize the dispersion or concentration of photons based on the needs of the duel.

> ### ── Archive Note ──
> While their size may make them appear as fighters focused only on brute strength, the Kodai are renowned for the elegance with which they dance across the battlefield, moving their luminous tresses like whips of pure energy. Their presence in interstellar conflicts is often synonymous with a tactical superiority based on light projection, making them formidable allies and extremely dangerous adversaries in low-light conditions.
