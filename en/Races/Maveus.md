# Mavei

The **Mavei** are a Triton race native to the planet **Trifectum**, located within the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Pyreus System/Pyreus System|Pyreus system]]. Being a species that evolved in an entirely or predominantly aquatic environment, the Mavei have anatomical adaptations that make them excellent swimmers and abyssal predators, clearly distinguishing them from the terrestrial humanoid races of the Alpha Sphere.

---

# I. Physical and Anatomical Characteristics

The physical appearance of the Mavei is defined by their aquatic nature. The most significant morphological difference compared to other humanoid species is the absence of lower limbs: instead of legs, the Mavei are equipped with a **long fish tail**, powerful and muscular, which acts as the main propeller.

* **Aquatic Adaptation:** The structure of the tail allows Mavei lightning-fast movements in water, making them capable of evasive maneuvers impossible for any creature with bipedal limbs.
* **Physiology:** While their upper body vaguely resembles a humanoid form (with torso, arms, and head), their respiratory and sensory systems are optimized for the ocean depths of Trifectum, making them capable of withstanding extreme underwater pressures.

---

# II. Culture and Habitat: Trifectum

The planet Trifectum, cradle of the Mavei civilization, is a world characterized by vast oceans and complex marine ecosystems. The Mavei culture developed around their ability to navigate planetary currents, building submerged cities that integrate harmoniously with coral formations and abyssal chasms.

* **Aquatic Society:** Their social organization is based on the management of currents and knowledge of the Trifectum marine cycles. The Mavei have a deep connection with local fauna, which they often use for defensive or logistical purposes.
* **Interstellar Adaptability:** When operating outside their natural environment, the Mavei require bio-mechanical immersion suits or pressurized stations rich in saline, which limits their long-range expeditions unless they are equipped with advanced life support technology.

---

# III. Role in the MasterVerse

The Mavei are jealous guardians of Trifectum's ocean resources. Despite their geographic isolation due to environmental requirements, they are considered a high-level strategic force for any faction controlling the Pyreus system, especially due to their unrivaled knowledge of submarine defense systems.

> ### ── Archive Note ──
> The mobility of the Mavei in water is superior to that of any conventional underwater drone. However, their dependence on aquatic environments makes them extremely vulnerable in arid or space environments. Guilds wishing to integrate the Mavei must invest heavily in high-pressure life support infrastructure, making enlisting them a significant but tactically rewarding logistical commitment.
