# Radahi

The **Radahi** are an avian race native to the planet **Trifectum**, a world known primarily for its immense oceans. Despite the predominantly aquatic nature of their home planet, the Radahi have evolved to occupy the ecological niches of the immense rock formations that emerge from the waters and the updrafts created by Trifectum's unstable climate.

---

# I. Physical Characteristics

The distinctive element of the Radahi is the presence of **wide bird wings** that branch out from their shoulders. Their anatomy is a perfect example of balance between lightness and resistance:
* **Wing Structure:** Their wings feature an impressive span, lined with water-repellent feathers that allow them to withstand Trifectum's cyclical storms and dive into the sea for short periods without compromising their ability to fly.
* **Adaptations:** In addition to their wing structure, they have hollow bones and a hyper-developed pectoral muscular system, giving them the flying power needed to navigate the planet's violent air currents.

---

# II. Culture and Habitat

Living on a planet dominated by water but gifted with the gift of flight has profoundly shaped the psychology of the Radahi. They consider themselves not only inhabitants of the air, but "sentinels of the oceans".

* **Fortress Nests:** The Radahi build their homes on the highest peaks of the rocky islands of Trifectum, places inaccessible to wingless creatures. These suspended cities, called "Fortress Nests", act as strategic observation points over the entire marine ecosystem below.
* **Philosophy of Flight:** Radahi culture is imbued with a profound spirituality linked to wind and currents. Flying is seen not only as a means of transportation, but as a martial art and a form of artistic expression. Their aerial dances are famous throughout the Pyreus system for their complexity and beauty.

---

# III. Role in the MasterVerse

Radahi have become a key asset for MasterBase, especially for reconnaissance and air patrol operations.

* **Strategic Support:** Due to their ability to cover large distances in short times, they are often used as messengers, vanguard observatories or rapid extraction units in environments where space shuttles would be too bulky or visible.
* **Interaction with the Mavei:** Coexistence with the **Mavei** (the other native race of Trifectum) is historically complex but symbiotic. While the Mavei dominate the abyss, the Radahi dominate the skies; the cooperation between these two species has made it possible to make Trifectum a planet practically impenetrable to external invaders.
