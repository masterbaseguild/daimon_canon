# Quiladi

The **Quilads** are a race of feline humanoids native to the planet **Quitera**, located within the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Pyreus System/Pyreus System|Pyreus system]]. Evolutionary descendants of large arboreal cats similar to terrestrial jaguars, the Quilads have retained the agility, stealth and predatory spirit of their ancestors, elevating them to the rank of sentient civilization capable of dominating the complex ecosystems of their native jungle.

---

# I. Origins and Physiology

The biology of the Quilads clearly reflects their ancestry from jaguars. They have a dense and powerful muscular structure, optimized for explosive leaps, acrobatic climbing and remarkable endurance in short-distance pursuits.

* **Feline Traits:** The body is covered with a thick and spotted fleece, with camouflage patterns that vary depending on the micro-climate of origin. Their vision is exceptional in low light conditions, making them natural predators even in Quitera's dense nocturnal forests.
* **Supreme Agility:** Thanks to an extremely flexible spine and superhuman reflexes, Quilads are able to move through the foliage of Quitera's forests with a grace that appears almost supernatural to outside observers.

---

# II. Culture and Lifestyle on Quitera

The planet Quitera is a world characterized by endless equatorial rainforests and mountain plateaus. Quiladi society is structured into clans based on mastery of hunting and navigating the wilderness.

* **Hunter-Architects:** Unlike many other sentient felines, Quilads do not live only on the ground. They developed sophisticated “hanging cities” built among the monumental trees of Quitera, using natural weavings and bioluminescent materials that blend with the environment.
* **Code of Honor:** Quiladi culture revolves around respect for prey and loyalty to the clan. A Quiladi does not fight out of simple cruelty, but out of necessity or to defend their "hunting ground" (the sector of territory assigned to their clan).

---

# III. Role in the MasterVerse

The Quiladi have been recruited on several occasions by the MasterBase for infiltration operations, reconnaissance in hostile territories and forestry warfare. Their ability to move without making a sound and their natural penchant for close combat make them peerless elite units.

> ### ── Archive Note ──
> Despite their reputation as fearsome warriors, Quilads possess an inquisitive nature and a penchant for natural philosophy. Often, during breaks between missions, they prefer to retreat into meditation or observation of planetary ecosystems. However, their calm should not be mistaken for submission: a Quiladi who feels threatened or perceives a violation of their territory responds with a swift and relentless ferocity worthy of the Pyreus system's top predator.
