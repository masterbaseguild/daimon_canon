# Sadrians

The **Sadrians** are a draconic race that evolved on the planet **Sadrium**, in the system of the same name located in the **Gamma Sphere**. Beings of great grandeur and pride, the Sadrians are known throughout the MasterVerse for their leathery nature, their ancestral bond with telluric energies and the unmistakable golden aesthetic that distinguishes them among all other species.

---

# I. Physical Characteristics

The physiology of the Sadrians clearly reflects their reptilian ancestry. They are robust beings, whose physical structure is optimized for combat and survival in hostile environments.

* **Skin and Armor:** The body is entirely covered in extremely dense **scaly skin**, which acts as a natural armor against physical and energy attacks.
* **Distinguishing Marks:** The most famous aesthetic characteristic of the breed is the coloring of their **golden scales**, which shine with a metallic glare under sunlight, making each Sadrian a figure of great visual impact.
* **Appendages:** They have a long **reptilian tail** that acts as a counterweight during rapid movements and as a whip in melees, and the ends of their limbs are equipped with **clawed fingers**, capable of tearing even the most resistant metal alloys, making them formidable in close combat.

---

# II. Culture and Habitat on Sadrium

The planet Sadrium is a world rich in rare mineral deposits and magnetic rock formations. The Sadrian civilization developed around this geology:

* **Clan Society:** Sadrian culture is divided into clans based on the hue and purity of the reflection of their golden scales. Social prestige is often linked to the hardness of one's natural armor and the history of battles that have marked one's body.
* **Draconic Wisdom:** Despite their warlike nature, Sadrians are known to be custodians of profound ancient wisdom. They tend to speak little and act with surgical precision; their approach to life is stoic, reflecting the solidity of the stone and the timelessness of the gold that covers them.

---

# III. Role in the MasterVerse

In the MasterAlliance hierarchy, Sadrians often fill the roles of **elite guardians and heavy strike forces**.

* **Tactical Advantage:** Their ability to take hits that would obliterate an ordinary humanoid makes them perfect as living shields. They are often deployed on the front lines, where their golden presence alone is enough to intimidate enemy ranks.
* **Integration:** Although their draconic nature leads them to prefer direct command or autonomy, they have sworn loyalty to the MasterBase in exchange for the protection of their trade routes in the Sadrium system, establishing a pact of steel with Entity.
