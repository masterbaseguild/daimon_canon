# Primordials

The **First Ones** are a human race native to the planet **Eaclar**, located in the Gondraw system. Despite their diminutive stature, Primords are feared throughout the sector for their extraordinary ability to manipulate electricity, transforming their bodies into a living weapon capable of generating discharges of devastating power.

---

# I. Physical and Biological Characteristics

The Primords are distinguished by a build of **small stature**, a condition that makes them extremely agile and difficult to hit on the battlefield. The most distinctive aspect of their anatomy is the **thin bladed tail** that extends from the base of the spine.

This tail is not a simple organ of balance or an aesthetic element; it acts as a **biological catalyst for electricity**. The bladed structure of the tail allows the Primordial to accumulate, channel, and discharge high-intensity electrical currents, making it the most important extension of their defensive and offensive arsenal.

---

# II. Electric Magic and Combat

Primordial culture is intrinsically linked to their ability to project lightning and electrify their surroundings. In combat, the tail is used as a voltaic whip:

* **Electric Lashes:** Through rapid movements, the Primordial can project arcs capable of instantly paralyzing an opponent or overheating enemy armor circuits.
* **Area Spells:** By accumulating static charge in their tail and releasing it to the ground or into the air, a group of Primords can create electrified barriers or disturbance fields that make approaching their perimeter prohibitive.
* **Catalyzation:** The tail acts as a natural lightning rod. During thunderstorms or in environments rich in static energy, the Primords are able to amplify their magical power, making their electrical discharges exponentially more dangerous.

---

# III. Society and Habitat on Eaclar

The planet Eaclar, home world of the Primordials, is characterized by constant electromagnetic storms and an atmosphere charged with energy. This environmental condition has driven the evolution of the breed, making them perfectly integrated into an ecosystem where electricity is the primary source of energy.

Primordial society is highly technological and based on efficiency; their ability to interact directly with electrical systems makes them exceptional technicians and saboteurs. However, their warlike nature and mastery of hand-to-hand combat sees them often employed as rapid assault units or specialists in infiltration operations behind enemy lines.

> ### ── Archive Note ──
> Despite its small size, a Primordial in full control of its bladed tail can take down a larger target in seconds. Their fighting technique, defined by the Masters as "lightning dance", is based on sudden shots and precise discharges, making their fighting style fluid, unpredictable and lethal.
