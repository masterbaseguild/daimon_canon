# Wyverns

The **Wyverns** are a draconic race that evolved in the ocean depths of the planet **Trifectum**, in the Pyreus system. Although they share a draconic origin with species such as the Sadrians, the Wyverns have taken a radically different evolutionary direction, adapting to marine life through a serpentine morphology that makes them the most agile and feared predators of the abyssal currents.

---

# I. Physical and Anatomical Characteristics

Wyvern anatomy is designed for fluid movement in variable density environments. The most distinctive morphological characteristic, which differentiates them from the other reptilians of the MasterVerse, is the total absence of lower limbs.

* **Serpentine Structure:** In place of legs, Wyverns possess a massive, muscular **long reptilian tail**, which makes up the majority of their total length. This tail is not just a propulsion organ, but a devastating kinetic weapon, capable of generating lashes in the water that create lethal shock waves.
* **Draconic Traits:** Despite the lack of legs, they maintain the classic characteristics of the draconic lineage: a leathery and scaly skin, a head equipped with powerful jaws and sharp claws on the upper appendages, used to manipulate objects or anchor themselves to coral formations.

---

# II. Habitat and Adaptation

The planet Trifectum, a world of water, is the natural habitat of the Wyverns. They reside in the "Pressure Zones", the ocean depths where sunlight does not penetrate.

* **Predators of the Deep:** Thanks to their reptilian tails, Wyverns can swim at hypersonic speeds in water, exploiting the planet's thermal currents to hunt colossal prey.
* **Biological Symbiosis:** Their skin produces a bio-luminescent mucus that they use to communicate with each other in the abyss of darkness, creating complex light patterns that act as a non-verbal language.

---

# III. Role in the MasterVerse

The integration of the Wyverns into the MasterAlliance has revolutionized the defense strategies of the Pyreus system.

* **Abyssal Special Forces:** They are employed as submarine defense units. No enemy vessel or submarine can hope to escape a pack of Wyverns hunting in the waters of Trifectum.
* **Environmental Management:** Work closely with the **Mavei** and the **Radahi** to maintain the ecological balance of the planet. If the Mavei are the logistical force and the Radahi are the aerial observers, the Wyverns represent the "sword" of the planet: the physical striking force that protects the underwater sanctuaries.

> ### ── Archive Note ──
>Despite their reputation as "sea serpents", Wyverns are creatures of ancient nobility. They possess a very cold and methodical form of analytical intelligence. A Wyvern never attacks out of anger, but out of strategic necessity; when one of them joins a MasterBase mission, they bring with them the relentless discipline of ocean currents, making them ideal partners for submerged base infiltration or naval sabotage operations.
