# Gondrani

The **Gondrans** are a human race native to the planet **Rose**, located within the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Gondraw System/Gondraw System|Gondraw system]]. Although genetically related to primary human stock, Gondrans have developed unique biological and magical adaptations due to the environmental conditions of their home world and the peculiar energetic influences of the surrounding asteroid belt.

---

# I. Physical and Biological Characteristics

The most distinctive aspect of the Gondrans is their skin pigmentation: they have a characteristic complexion, tending towards **greyish tones**, a peculiarity that makes them immediately recognizable compared to other human populations of the Alpha Sphere.

Their biology is intrinsically linked to **organic magic**, a form of energy manipulation that does not require external technological support. This ability manifests primarily through the innate ability to generate and control **tentacles**, which can be projected from one's body or manifested through the use of internal energy. These appendages are used both as a defensive weapon and as an offensive tool, guaranteeing the Gondrani considerable tactical versatility in close combat.

---

# II. The Influence of Gondraw Radiations

It has been scientifically proven that the Gondora system exerts a direct influence on the physiology of this breed, in particular due to the emissions coming from the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Gondraw System/Asteroid Belt|local asteroid belt]].

* **Adaptive Mutations:** Prolonged or direct exposure to radiation emanating from asteroids causes temporary but significant mutations in the Gondrans' physique.
* **Radiation Effects:** During these periods of exposure, the subjects' complexion tends to darken further and, at the same time, their native magical manipulation abilities undergo an **increase in power**.

This peculiarity makes the Gondrans who operate near the asteroid belt formidable adversaries, capable of denser, more resistant and lethal tentacle projections than their conspecifics who live far from the system of origin.

---

# III. Role in the MasterVerse

As a native population of a strategic region such as the Gondraw system, the Gondran have often found themselves at the center of power dynamics between large galactic guilds and factions. Their organic manipulation abilities have attracted the interest of many scholars and military commanders.

> ### ── Archive Note ──
> The adaptive nature of Gondrans makes them a valuable but unstable resource. Guilds seeking to recruit fighters of this race must take into account that their warfare potential fluctuates dramatically based on proximity to their star ecosystem, making them elite warriors if positioned correctly, but vulnerable if removed from the Gondraw system for too long.
