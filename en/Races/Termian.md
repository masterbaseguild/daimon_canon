# Terms

The **Termi** are a humanoid race of canine nature originating from the planet **Quitera**, in the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Pyreus System/Pyreus System|Pyreus system]]. Although they share the same homeworld with the feline Quilads, thermas evolved along an entirely distinct genetic line, descending directly from ancient predators similar to Earth's **jackals**. This ancestry has given them extraordinary social intelligence, an unsurpassed herd instinct, and an ability to adapt that makes them masters of survival in conditions of scarcity.

---

# I. Physiology and Adaptations

Thermi biology is a perfect fusion of the characteristics of an opportunistic predator and a sentient intelligence capable of manipulating the surrounding environment.

* **Canine Traits:** They have an elongated snout, very fine hearing and a sense of smell that allows them to detect organic traces from kilometers away. Their physical structure is lean and muscular, optimized for long patrol marches or prolonged pursuits.
* **Resilience:** Thanks to their lineage from jackals, Thermi have an extremely efficient metabolism, capable of obtaining nourishment even from resources that other species would consider inedible, making them unbeatable logisticians on long-range missions.

---

# II. Culture and Society on Quitera

On Quitera, the Thermi have organized themselves into social structures based on the **pack**, a system that guarantees almost absolute loyalty towards one's own group.

* **Branco hierarchies:** The Termi society is meritocratic. Leadership roles are entrusted to those who demonstrate the greatest clarity in crisis situations, reflecting the role of "seekers" that jackals play in natural dynamics.
* **Lair Architecture:** Unlike the hanging cities of the Quiladi, the Lair favor complex systems of subterranean shelters and semi-buried outposts, designed to withstand Quitera's extreme weather events and serve as protected communications nodes.

---

# III. Role in the MasterVerse

In the MasterAlliance hierarchy, Termes play crucial roles that require acumen, speed, and the ability to operate autonomously.

* **Raiders and Scouts:** Thanks to their nature as opportunistic predators, they are the best specialists in advanced reconnaissance. They are able to infiltrate enemy lines, assess vulnerabilities and retreat before the adversary is even aware of their presence.
* **Communication and Coordination:** Their pack instinct translates, at a tactical level, into an ability to coordinate without the need for complex verbal orders, a gift that the MasterBase exploits to create extremely silent and effective operational units.

> ### ── Archive Note ──
> Despite a historic environmental rivalry with the Quilads for control of hunting grounds on Quitera, the Thermi have shown they can work with the felines when the interests of the Pyreus system are at risk. Their vision of the world is pragmatic and rational: for a Termo, the survival of the pack is the supreme objective, and the MasterAlliance represents, to all intents and purposes, the "definitive pack" in which they have chosen to join.
