# Flight School

**Flying School** is an advanced magical discipline based on the manipulation of the physical laws of motion and energy. Through the skillful use of kinetic and thermal magic, practitioners can overcome gravity, transforming the air itself into a playground and battle ground.

---

# I. Fundamental Theory: The Dynamics of Flight

The main problem of magic flight is prolonged energy consumption. While taking off requires massive thrust, maintaining flight requires efficiency.

* **The Elevation (Take-off) Spell:** Consists of the direct application of kinetic magic to the individual's entire body. It is the purest and most immediate technique, but it is extremely wasteful in terms of mana, making it unsuitable as a sole method for long-range travel or prolonged combat.

---

# II. Maintenance Techniques (Navigation)

To overcome rapid energy exhaustion, flight masters have developed two main methodologies that allow long-duration flights:

* **Air Current Domination:** Instead of lifting his own weight, the mage manipulates surrounding air masses to create lift.
* *Efficiency:* Very high. The handling of gases is energetically less demanding than that of solids.
* *Equipment:* Performance increases exponentially with the use of aerodynamic aids such as wingsuits, powered cloaks or hang gliders, which allow the wizard to "soar" on the currents generated.
* **Burst Propulsion (Thermal Magic):** Uses energy pulses to generate controlled micro-explosions at the extremities (hands or feet).
* *Dynamics:* It exploits the principle of action and reaction to obtain sudden impulses and sudden changes in direction.
* *Risk:* High impact technique. It requires millimeter control to avoid overheating or direct physical damage to the user. It is the choice of choice for rapid air combat.

---

# III. Tactical Applications

The Flight School is not just a means of transportation, but a vital component to tactical superiority in the Talos system and beyond:

1. **Vertical Infiltration:** Ideal for overcoming the walls of strongholds or reaching orbiting platforms inaccessible by land.
2. **Air Superiority:** Mages capable of mastering burst propulsion can engage airborne targets with a mobility denied to conventional infantry.
3. **Reconnaissance:** Using air currents allows scouts to survey large territories with a minimal energy signature, making them difficult to intercept.

---

# IV. Training Considerations

Training requires a deep understanding of the physics of flight. Students usually start with the take-off spell indoors (for safety), and then move on to atmospheric manipulation outdoors. Combustion propulsion is only taught to users who demonstrate superior thermal control, to prevent catastrophic accidents.
