# Seed of Nymaera

The **Seed of Nymaera** is a magical artifact of inestimable value, considered one of the greatest expressions of **vegetable organic magic**. It presents itself as a pulsating nucleus of natural energy capable of accelerating the biological processes of plant growth at an exponential rate, making the owner a true "forest architect".

---

# I. Properties of the Artifact

The primary function of the Seed is the immediate projection of plant biomass.
* **Quick Genesis:** The one who masters the artifact is able to generate and control the growth of entire forests of vines, roots and shrubs in a span of time measurable in a few seconds.
* **Organic Magic:** Unlike traditional environmental manipulation magic, Seed energy interacts directly with the genetic code of plants, forcing superhuman growth and granting the wielder tactical control over the shape and direction of this growth.
* **Tactical Use:** The ability to instantly create cover, barriers or immobilize targets through a jungle of vines makes the artifact a deadly weapon in both siege and defense scenarios.

---

# II. Line of Succession

The artifact has a history linked to important figures within the Alpha Sphere, who have tested its weight and responsibilities:

* **Vaximo:** Originally owned by the late [[en/Cast/Deceased/Olympus Foundation/Vaximo|Olympus Foundation]] member, Vaximo, the Seed served as a tool of power and advanced ecological research.
* **Cimo:** Upon Vaximo's death, the artifact passed into the hands of **Cimo**, member of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Cimo|MasterBase]]. Currently, Cimo is the custodian and user of the Seed, managing its potential within his guild's operations.

---

# III. Risks and Complexities

Mastering the Seed of Nymaera is not without its dangers. Organic magic requires a deep attunement with the artifact; careless management could lead to uncontrolled plant growth which would risk suffocating not only the opponent, but also the user himself or the surrounding environment.

> ### ── Archive Note ──
> *«Seed doesn't just make plants grow; it transforms them into extensions of its master's will. Whoever holds this artifact holds in their hands the potential to make a desert fertile or strangle a fortress in a breath."* — Theoretical analysis of organic relics.

---

# IV. Tactical Status

Currently, the Seed of Nymaera is under Cimo's operational custody. Its presence within the MasterBase provides the guild with a battlefield interdiction capability that few other factions can match, making any operational zone in which Cimo is present potentially dangerous due to the threat of a sudden "forest eruption".
