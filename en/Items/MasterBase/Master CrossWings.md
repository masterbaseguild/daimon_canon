The CrossWings are a device developed by [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]], capable of providing the user with great mobility and the ability to cover medium distances in a short time.

# Structure

Standard CrossWings consist of three main components:

- 2 mechanical wings
- 4 pseudo-magnetism harpoons
- Thruster

The components are held together by a backpack equipped with numerous harnesses, to distribute the acceleration throughout the user's body and ensure safety. The backpack is also equipped with a retractable hook, useful for rewinding any cloak connected to it and allowing the use of the CrossWings without hindrance.
_"No cloak!"_

# Operation

The mechanical wings allow the user to glide and maintain altitude with minimal descent.

Harpoons are instrumental in numerous techniques, such as the slingshot, through which a user can quickly launch themselves into flight from the ground, or rotation around an axis, through which the user can perform rapid reversals with minimal loss of momentum.

Finally, the thruster provides an additional means of gaining acceleration, and is used in tandem with wings and harpoons to maximize the user's mobility.

# Physiological support

Since it is very easy to reach very high and very sudden accelerations, for the most extreme pilots the use of [[en/Glossary/Structural Supplements|Structural supplements]] is recommended, a category of medicines that strengthen the most fragile elements of the human anatomy in case of high accelerations (such as bones and blood vessels).

However, not everyone needs it: some races, such as the [[en/Races/Radahian|Aviani]], are naturally inclined to high accelerations, and it is possible to exploit most of the magical techniques relating to the [[en/Items/Magic School of Flight|Flight School]] to escape temporarily the same effect as medicines.
