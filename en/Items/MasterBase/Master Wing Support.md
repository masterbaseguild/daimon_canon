While most Masters use crosswings to travel in all three dimensions, some races (primarily avian races) do not need them, or are unable to use them.

However, the weight of the equipment and the high level of mobility provided by special equipment make the natural attributes of such races disadvantaged or even unusable.

To overcome this problem, a wing support was developed, a partial version of the crosswings apparatus, consisting only of the harpoons and the propulsion engine.

Thanks to this support, the winged rays are able to take off while carrying the additional weight of combat equipment, and can enjoy the same high maneuverability that the crosswings allow their users, thanks to the presence of the harpoons.
