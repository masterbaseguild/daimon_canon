# Absorption School

The **School of Absorption** is a dark magical discipline focused on the recovery and integration of the residual energies of an intelligent being at the moment of his death. Unlike other forms of magic that draw on environmental or internal sources, this school requires the sacrifice of the victim to fuel the user's power, extracting the essence of the soul before it dissipates into the void.

---

# I. The Absorption Process (Absorb)

The beating heart of this school is the *Absorb* spell. When a sentient being is killed, its energy flow does not dissipate instantly; it remains in a state of transition for a few critical moments.
* **Mechanics:** The user of Absorb magic acts as a catalyst, forcing the flow of the dying soul's energy towards their energy core.
* **Recoverable Resources:**
* **Mana (Magical Energy):** The integration of raw mana allows the user to regenerate their reserves in prolonged battle contexts.
* **Knowledge (Residual Memory):** The most complex part of absorption. It is possible to extract fragments of memories, tactical skills or secrets that the individual kept. However, the risk of "mnemonic corruption" or confusion of personal identity is very high.

---

# II. Ethical and Social Implications

Given its necrotic and predatory nature, the Absorption School is officially banned or strictly regulated in most civilian systems.
* **Social Stigma:** Practitioners of this magic, often called *Siphons*, are viewed with horror and suspicion. Many guilds, including MasterBase and the Aura Foundations, consider the use of this magic to be a capital crime.
* **Mental Deterioration:** The constant absorption of different souls leads to a psychic fragmentation of the user. The mind of the Siphon risks becoming a mosaic of different consciences, leading to very serious mental pathologies and, over time, to the loss of one's original personality.

---

# III. Tactical Applications and Risks

Despite the ostracism, the ability to fuel its power during a fight makes the Siphon a formidable opponent:
* **Combat Advantage:** A practitioner can sustain high-level spells much longer than a conventional wizard, provided he has a constant flow of "victims".
* **The Price of Absorption:** The soul is not a clean reservoir. Each absorption introduces the victim's energy waste and trauma into the user's body. Many unwary users end up being "overwritten" by the souls they attempted to consume.

---

# IV. Countermeasures
Guilds operating in areas where Absorption magic is widespread have developed **Soul Seal** techniques. These spells, applied preventively, make an individual's soul "inaccessible" or toxic in the event of death, preventing the Siphon from completing the extraction and, in some cases, causing a devastating energetic backlash for the attacker.
