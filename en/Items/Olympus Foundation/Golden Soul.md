# GoldenSoul

The **Golden Soul** is not a simple weapon, but a sentient conduit to the consciences of the deceased. This unique sword, forged with unknown metaphysical properties, serves as a living archive, accumulating and preserving the minds of those who fall beneath its blade.

---

# I. Characteristics and Nature of the Object

* **Type:** Weapon (Sword).
* **Unique Property:** The sword possesses the ability to "absorb" the soul and consciousness of anyone killed by its blade. Unlike a magical prison, the minds contained within remain active, creating a complex and sometimes chaotic psychic mosaic within the weapon.
* **Origin:** The weapon is intrinsically linked to the soul of **Vasil**, whose spirit resides permanently within the blade, likely acting as a fulcrum or "guardian" of other absorbed souls.

---

# II. Historical Timeline and Impact

The history of the *Golden Soul* is marked by traumatic events that changed the fate of those who came into contact with it:

1. **The Rise of Haude:** The sword ends up in Haude's hands, marking the beginning of his story arc and his rise as a major figure in the system.
2. **Quark's Fate:** The weapon's psychic reach has had fatal consequences for those who have sought to understand or defy it. Quark, overwhelmed by the torments of the minds contained in the blade, reaches the tragic gesture of suicide using the weapon.
3. **Entity's Discovery:** The weapon becomes the catalyst for a hidden truth when Entity comes into contact with it at **John Drastic's** home. The touch of the sword allows Entity to access the collective memory of the slain, revealing secrets that had been buried along with their lives.

---

# III. Dangers and Considerations

The *Golden Soul* represents a form of passive and permanent "Absorption". Unlike the [[en/Items/Magic School of Absorption|Absorption School]], where the user supplements the resources, here the souls are *retained* in the sword, creating a weapon that is not only a means of death, but a burden to the wielder.

> ### ── Archive Note ──
> *«Do not brandish the Golden Soul if you are not ready to support the weight of a thousand thoughts that do not belong to you. It is a weapon that not only kills the body, but forces its owner to live with the silent screams of all who have betrayed his blade."* — Analysis retrieved from the John Drastic archives.

---

# IV. Current Status

The weapon is considered one of the most dangerous and sought-after objects in the entire Alpha Sphere. Its ability to contain forbidden knowledge makes it a primary target both for those seeking absolute power and for those who want to permanently bury the truths it holds.
