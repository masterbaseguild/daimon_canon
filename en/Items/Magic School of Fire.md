# Firebending School

The **Fire Bending School** is a complex, multidisciplinary discipline that blends **kinetic magic** and **thermal magic** to master the most destructive and mutable element in the universe. Unlike schools that simply conjure elements, this school requires simultaneous, scientific control of two distinct magical branches.

---

# I. Fundamental Theory: The Flame Triad

Generating and controlling a magical flame follows a rigorous three-step process, requiring superior cognitive mastery:

1. **Arrangement (Kinetic Magic):** The mage uses kinetic magic to accumulate and shape fuel—typically in liquid or gaseous form—into the desired location.
2. **Ignition (Thermal Magic):** Once the fuel is placed, an ignition spell is cast. At the subatomic level, the magician applies tiny forces to the atoms in the area, setting them in rapid motion. This increase in molecular kinetic energy translates, macroscopically, into heat, triggering combustion.
3. **Governance (Synergy):** Controlling the active flame requires the constant use of both classes of magic: kinetic to shape the shape and spread of the flame, and thermal to maintain or increase its temperature.

> **Theoretical Note:** Heat manipulation is defined as a form of *micro-kinetics*. Heat is nothing more than the kinetic energy of atoms; therefore, dominating fire means dominating the frenetic movement of matter itself.

---

# II. Igneus Biblios: The Peruvian Tradition

The **Igneus Biblios** is one of the most prestigious schools of magic of Peruvian origin, dedicated specifically to the advanced manipulation of fire and thermal energy. He is celebrated for transforming the physical principles of combustion into a versatile magical martial art.

### Skill Features:
* **Fire Spheres:** Concentrated plasma projectiles capable of impacting with explosive force.
* **Thermal Enhancement:** The mage envelops his weapons or body in an aura of heat, increasing the destructive power of physical attacks or creating a radiant barrier.
* **Thermal Propulsion:** Direct application of propulsion principles (seen in Flight School) to achieve rapid sprints or lift off the ground via directed flame jets.

---

# III. Challenges for the Practitioner

The discipline is known to be one of the most challenging to learn.
* **Magical Multitasking:** The user must be able to simultaneously manage flows of kinetic and thermal energy, often under the stress of combat.
* **Risk Management:** The volatile nature of flames requires near-perfect control. A miscalculation during the "Governance" phase can cause the fire to spread uncontrollably, exposing the magician himself to serious physical harm.

---

# IV. Tactical Status

Although the Igneus Biblios began as an academic tradition, its practical applications have become central to many guilds operating in extreme environments or against heavily armored adversaries. The ability to instantly generate heat and kinetic momentum makes the masters of this school among the most feared support and attack units in the Alpha Sphere.
