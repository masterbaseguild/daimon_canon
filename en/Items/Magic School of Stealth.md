# School of Magic of Discretion (Maya)

**Maya** is an advanced magical discipline that focuses on the manipulation of the perceptive fabric. Unlike crude illusion magic, Maya does not simply create visions, but acts directly on the sense organs and cerebral cortices of intelligent beings to "erase" the user's presence from their experiential reality.

---

# I. Fundamental Principles

**Maya** energy works through the modulation of energy frequencies that interact with the target's sensory receptors. The underlying concept is that "reality" is not absolute, but a sensorial construction; by altering the input received by the brain, the magician can become, effectively, non-existent to the observer.

* **Perceptual Alteration:** The magician does not make himself "invisible" in the physical sense of the term, but prevents the target's neurons from processing the visual, auditory or olfactory data concerning him.
* **Selectivity:** An expert practitioner of Maya can choose to be imperceptible to a single person, a small group, or an entire crowd, making this school indispensable for infiltration and espionage.

---

# II. Main Applications

* **Sensory Invisibility:** The most common form, where light hitting the user is interpreted by the observer's brain as "empty space".
* **Sound Suppression:** Ability to move in total silence, absorbing or deflecting the sound waves produced by movements.
* **Aura Cloaking:** For Masters or energy users, Maya allows them to mask their energy signature, making them impossible to track via magical sensors or basic technological detectors.

---

# III. Limits and Risks

Despite its potential, Maya is not an invulnerability spell.
* **Mental Effort:** Maintaining the sensory alteration on multiple subjects requires immense concentration. If the user suffers trauma or loses concentration, the "veil" falls instantly.
* **Technological Interference:** Some artificial sensors or non-biological detection tools may be immune to Maya alteration, making the wizard visible through unfiltered optical systems (e.g. thermal cameras or radar).
* **Counter-Magic:** There are schools of magic that specialize in "Revelation" or "Truth" that can pierce the effect of Maya with relative ease.

---

# IV. Strategic Use

Maya is the school of magic favored by members of guilds dedicated to espionage and covert operations in the Talos system. It is often used in combination with traditional stealth techniques to maximize the chances of success in high-risk missions.
