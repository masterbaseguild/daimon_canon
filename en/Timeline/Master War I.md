# First Master War: The Collapse of the Paladins

The **First Master War** was the first, devastating, large-scale conflict pitting the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]] against the paramilitary guild of the **Young Fighters**. The clash, originating from profound ideological differences and the discovery of clandestine terrorist activities, has reshaped the geopolitics of the sectors, decreeing the definitive hegemonic consolidation of the MasterBase and the absorption of the last independent guilds of the pre-Master era.

---

# I. The Origins: False Justice

The **Young Fighters** guild, under the iron leadership of commander **Loris**, enjoyed widespread fame in the historical sectors, presenting itself publicly as an organization of champions of justice and protectors of the weak.

However, a series of internal investigations conducted by MasterBase intelligence brought to light a much darker reality: behind the institutional facade, the Young Fighters conducted ruthless **clandestine terrorist attacks** and sabotage operations aimed at exterminating anyone who opposed their expansion plans. Refusing to be intimidated by this emerging super-guild, Entity responded to the threats by officially declaring the commencement of war hostilities.

---

# II. The Home Front: The Sacrifice of Space Fox and NextWare

Initially, the renowned guild [[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/SpaceFox|SpaceFox]] (led by **Aledge**) took to the field as a historic ally of the Young Fighters, unaware of their crimes. When the damning evidence of Loris's terrorism began to circulate through communication channels, Aledge made a radical choice, deciding to suddenly turn his back on his allies so as not to become complicit in the massacres.

The Young Fighters' retaliation was immediate, ruthless and total:
* **The StarBase Bombardment:** Loris' heavy ships opened fire on the SpaceFox orbiting fortress, bombarding and pulverizing it within minutes.
* **The Annihilation of NextWare:** Immediately afterwards, the Young Fighters troops stormed the surface of the planet to raze the infrastructures of **NextWare**, a famous aural and technological research guild closely linked and federated with SpaceFox.

---

# III. The Final Act: The Regicide of Loris

Despite the brutal show of force against the dissidents, the main military campaign against MasterBase proved to be a dead end for the Young Fighters. Entity's war machine demonstrated a clear technological and strategic superiority, repelling every offensive and squeezing the paladins in a grip of attrition.

Faced with their backs against the wall and realizing the inevitability of defeat due to the foolish conduct of their commander, the vanguard members of the Young Fighters rebelled. In a dramatic epilogue that took place behind the lines of the front, **Loris was brutally murdered by his own comrades in arms**. This internal coup instantly broke the chain of command and morale of the faction, decreeing the unconditional surrender of the Young Fighters and the **total victory of the MasterBase**.

---

# IV. Final Status Quo and New Alliances

The end of the First Master War marked the final dismantling of the Young Fighters and the birth of a new era of cohesion for the MasterBase. Left without a home and without resources after the destruction of their respective headquarters, the top surviving exponents of SpaceFox and NextWare chose to merge permanently within the victorious guild.

> * **Aledge** (Former SpaceFox leader, strategist and fleet coordinator)
> * **Chojiro** & **Disti** (Fighting aces and veterans of the old guard)
> * **Alone** (Legendary fighter, future leader of the uprising)
> * **Hooneex** (Elite scientist from NextWare's auric labs)
