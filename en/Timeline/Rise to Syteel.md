# Rise to Syteel: Colonization and Progress

**Rise to Syteel** defines the era of forced landing, exploration, and subsequent technological boom of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]] on the planet Syteel. Starting with the catastrophic loss of its orbiting fortress to cyber sabotage, this interlude has become the golden age of scientific development and planetary diplomacy for the guild.

---

# I. Arc's Sabotage and the Big Landing

The Masters' exploratory routes lead to the discovery of Syteel, a lush and perfectly livable planet, which until then had remained on the edge of the great geopolitical routes. However, the enthusiasm for the discovery is abruptly cut short by an internal disaster of unprecedented proportions.

**Arc**, the artificial intelligence and core operating system of MasterBase, suffers a devastating hacker attack from a hostile faction. Totally compromised and out of control, the A.I. initiates a sequence of reactor surges and breaches, **destroying the MasterBase from the inside**. Deprived of their historic orbiting starbase, the Masters are forced into a dramatic mass evacuation, making an emergency landing on the surface of Syteel.
