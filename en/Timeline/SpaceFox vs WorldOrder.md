## [[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/SpaceFox|SpaceFox]] vs [[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/WorldOrder|WorldOrder]]

The **SpaceFox vs WorldOrder** conflict is one of the most impressive and strategic *[[en/Glossary/Guild Wars|wars between guilds]]* in the history of the sectors. The war ends with the total destruction of the WorldOrder and with a victory for **SpaceFox** which, despite eliminating its direct rival, suffers profound economic and military attrition which initiates its definitive decline.

---

# I. The Status Quo and the Factions in the Field

The balance of the historical sectors is contested between commercial and military blocks differing in structure, size and operational philosophies.

### 1. WorldOrder
It represents a true [[en/Glossary/Guild|guild]] colossus, a massive structure comparable to an empire in terms of extension and capacity for war mobilization. Characterized by an aggressive and unstoppable strategic conduct, the WorldOrder aims at a rigid centralization of territorial control.
* **Command:** The guild is led by the supreme leader **[[en/Cast/Deceased/WorldOrder/111|111]]**.
* **Vanguard Aces:** In the front lines, the presence of **[[en/Cast/Blaze|Blaze]]**, future leader of the *[[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/SpaceHub|SpaceHub]]*, stands out.

### 2. SpaceFox
Led by project director **[[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Aledge|Aledge]]**, SpaceFox is a guild of above-average size, but structurally inferior to its rival giant. It has an elite staff made up of key figures such as **[[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Chojiro|Chojiro]]**, **[[en/Cast/Groups/Navirad/Disti|Disti]]**, **[[en/Cast/Groups/Blue Owls/Gianco|Gianco]]**, **[[en/Cast/Wisla|Wisla]]** and **[[en/Cast/Las|Las]]**. To bridge the numerical and logistical gap with WorldOrder, SpaceFox relies on the fundamental support of a network of three allied guilds, each highly specialized:
* [[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/Zeus|Zeus]]: Led by [[en/Cast/Shalida|Shalida]], a guild specialized in espionage, intelligence and infiltration operations.
* [[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/NextWare|NextWare]]: Led by [[en/Cast/Groups/Blue Owls/Alone|Halo]], guild focused on digital technologies and equipment.
* [[en/Cast/Groups/Keyplexity/Keyplexity|Keyplexity]]: Led by [[en/Cast/Groups/Keyplexity/Hooneex|Hooneex]], a guild specialized in colonization, scouting and advanced territorial control.

---

# II. Military Chronology

The conflict develops as a ruthless war of total attrition. The WorldOrder uses its numerical and structural superiority to impose constant pressure on multiple planetary fronts.

### The Defeat of the WorldOrder and the Impact of the War
SpaceFox manages to resist and turn the tide of the fight thanks to the combination of its elite forces and the coordinated intervention of its allies. Zeus cuts WorldOrder's information lines, NextWare guarantees the technological superiority of the armaments used and Keyplexity maintains control of strategic points and supply routes. This joint effort leads to the **total destruction of the WorldOrder**, the dismantling of its bases and the escape of its leaders.

However, the cost of victory for the coalition is very high:
* SpaceFox's energy and financial reserves are almost entirely wiped out.
* The three support guilds suffer similar attrition; in particular, Zeus, exhausted by the war effort, faced a **spontaneous dissolution** in the following period due to the drying up of its funds.

---

# III. The Post-War and the Relationship with the Young Fighters

The dissolution of WorldOrder does not make its members disappear from the sectors. Some of the survivors of the defeated guild join forces with some members of SpaceFox, including **Wisla**, to found a new reality: the **[[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/Young Fighters|YoungFighters]]**.

Thanks to this mixed composition, the initial relations between the two factions are extremely positive and collaborative. This closeness gradually turns into a form of **economic dependence** for SpaceFox, whose finances are devastated by the war. The Young Fighters become the main support of the Aledge guild, gaining a strong indirect influence on it, which will play an important role in the events of the [[en/Timeline/Master War I|first war master]].
