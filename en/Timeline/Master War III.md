# Third Master War (Master War III)

The **Third Master War** was a conflict of galactic proportions involving the entire X sector and the Ki sector. Initially unleashed through a proxy war led by a syndicate of mercenaries, it proved to be the scene of the return of **SpaceHub**, culminating in the damage to MasterBase and the subsequent colonization of the planet **Syteel**.

---

# I. The Prelude and the Status Quo

After the landslide Masters victory during the [[en/Timeline/Master War II|Second War Master]], the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]] experienced a lasting period of peace and prosperity. The high command has been deeply committed to diplomacy, weaving a dense and solid network of alliances across the sector. Relations with the other dominant macro-factions — from [[en/Cast/Groups/Lignum Nobili/Lignum Nobili|Lignum Nobili]] to *Keyplexity* — had never been so relaxed and collaborative.

### The Silent Raids Ode
The balance is suddenly broken when one of the MasterBase's allied bases is stormed and partially devastated by a mysterious and highly efficient order of mercenaries. Within a few weeks, the exact same pattern repeats itself against a second baseman, and then a third baseman.

Alarmed by the escalation, the Masters call an emergency meeting with leaders from across the industry. From the analysis of the data, a clear geopolitical strategy emerges: the mysterious attackers are surgically striking MasterBase's commercial and military partners, terrorizing them to push them to break the alliance treaties and isolate the guild.

### The Birth of Legacy (or MasterAlliance)
Despite the heated discussions and initial panic, reason prevails. To unite against the asymmetric threat, MasterBase and its allies sign a centralized mutual defense pact, founding the elite coalition known as **The Legacy** (referred to in some army records as *MasterAlliance*).

---

# II. The Forces in the Field

### The Legacy (Defensive Coalition)
* **MasterBase:** Represented by Headmasters [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Entity|Entity]], *Garo* and *Shane*.
* **Lignum Nobili:** Led by the trio *Keia*, *Alez* and *Nita*.
* **Keyplexity:** Coordinated in the field by *Hooneex*, *Meeleex* and *Alja*.
* **The Arpeggio (Navirad):** Led by *Black Ace*, [[en/Cast/Groups/Navirad/Kurymi|Kurymi]] and *Gastico Nox*.
* **Athan's Kingdom:** The royal armies under the command of *King Athan*, *Vice*, and *Morifex*.
* **The Blue Owls:** Elite autonomous strike force led by [[en/Cast/Groups/Blue Owls/Alone|Halo]].

### The Offensive Block
* **Crimson Sphere:** A fearsome mercenary guild originating from the *Ki Sector* (or Nobi System). Famous for the effectiveness of their heavy armor, siege engines and the ability to single-handedly dismantle factions three times their size, the members of the Crimson Sphere fight exclusively for pay: each of their clashes is linked to a contract of engagement stipulated by a hidden instigator.
* **SpaceHub:** Believed to be completely dismantled and destroyed after the Second Master War, the guild led by the revived and vengeful **Blaze** has spent two years secretly rebuilding its fleet and ranks in the shadows, acting as the mastermind and financier behind the mercenary attacks.

# III. Chronology of the Conflict

### 1. The Investigations and the First Suspects
While numerous Legacy parties preventively deploy to bases not yet attacked, the high command activates the **MasterBase secret services** to intercept enemy communications.

In an attempt to discover the secret instigator of the Crimson Sphere, two major intelligence operations are launched:
* **Aura Mission (Asgard):** The Masters infiltrate the planet Aura to verify the involvement of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Asgard Foundation|Asgard Foundation]]. Although trade links between Asgard and the Ki Sector are found, the investigation yields no hard evidence and the Masters return empty-handed.
* **Gondraw Mission (Franky Family):** The planet Gondraw is explored on the suspicion that the *Franky Family* is hunting down their deserters, but even in this case the outcome is inconclusive.

### 2. The Arpeggio Battle (The Gate Defense)
The mercenaries focus a massive siege attack on the *Arpeggio* starbase. For the first time, Legacy responds in a compact manner, deploying a strategic lineup coordinated from the *BattleDeck* by Entity, Garo and Emily.

* **First Wave:** The Arpeggio defends the West side, the Alliance the East side. In the North, the [[en/Cast/Groups/Blue Owls/Blue Owls|Blue Owls]] clearly dominate their side. In the South, the defensive line falters: *Xyroh*'s performance disappoints expectations, while *Andreas*, despite fighting valiantly with the support of *Gps*, suffers very serious wounds. On the verge of giving in, the Southern sector is saved by the clutch of the Blue Owls who, having broken the Northern front, rush to help, repelling the first wave.
* **Second Wave:** Post-fight, Andreas is taken to the infirmary by *Chojiro*, while *Duoco* rotates positions with the *FrankyRose*. Xyroh, furious, leads a devastating frontal charge together with Garo, while the Blue Owls (led by a seemingly invincible *Mape*) outclass the mercenaries. Given the poor parade, the commanders of the Crimson Sphere order the retreat of the reinforcements, leaving the soldiers left on the ground to be slaughtered.

### 3. The Siege of Athan City
The Crimson Sphere takes the offensive to *Athan's Kingdom*, attacking multiple urban entry points at once.
* **Round I & II:** Legacy's two defensive teams barely repel assaults on the main gates. The third team loses the vanguard position, but the providential intervention of the Blue Owls allows them to recover the perimeter.
* **Round III & IV:** The Owls settle down to guard a gate, but the team made up of *Tecmov* and *Shane* launches a reckless offensive charge outside the walls, being overwhelmed and leaving the mercenaries to penetrate inside the city. With the first team forced to fall back on the emergency barricades, the Legacy unites: the combined effort pushes the invaders back outside the walls, while the Blue Owls finally put them to flight.

At the end of the celebrations at the *Masteria*, the secret services intercept a crucial transmission between the Crimson Sphere and its instigator: the mercenaries plan a final, titanic attack against the Lignum Nobili set for **5 January**, however involuntarily revealing the coordinates of their main base in the Ki Sector, located at the antipodes.

# IV. The Final Act: The Ki Sector and the Fall of the MasterBase

Aware that the war and economic potential of the Crimson Sphere is drastically waning (since in prolonged contracts the contractors tend to preserve their forces rather than risk a pyrrhic victory), the Masters make a risky strategic choice: they leave the remaining bases in sector

Having arrived at the coordinates of the enemy base, the veil of mystery falls. The forces of **SpaceHub**, resurrected under the leadership of Blaze, are lining up to defend the Crimson Sphere.

The battle in space and on the planet is of unprecedented violence. Thanks to the sacrifice and skill of the Legacy, the Crimson Sphere is definitively annihilated and dismantled. However, SpaceHub manages to concentrate its barrage on the MasterBase, inflicting **catastrophic structural damage**. Now unable to maintain orbit or travel through the cosmos, the Masters' flagship is forced to make a desperate crash landing on the nearest habitable planet: **Syteel**.

---

# V. Consequences and Final Status Quo

The MasterBase ends the war stranded on the beach of **Tirsia Island**, on the planet Syteel. The Masters erect a makeshift camp on the coast, dividing their forces:
1. A portion of the questors and engineers work tirelessly day and night to repair and rebuild the engines and destroyed sections of the starbase.
2. The exploration departments begin to map the geography and fauna of the mysterious new planet.
3. The high command evaluates the new galactic balance with extreme concern: SpaceHub has inexplicably returned to activity and reports indicate that the Asgard Foundation may have facilitated their maneuvers in the Ki Sector.

*Historical note: The temporary camp on Tirsia beach will lay the foundations for the birth of **New Archen**, the future capital city of the faction.*

#VI. Future Developments (Arches Planning)

* **The Siege of Tirsia:** While the MasterBase is down for repairs, SpaceHub (which has taken control of much of the island) lays siege to the Masters' base camp. The Legacy will have to repel coastal assaults, counterattack and liberate the entire island of Tirsia.
* **The Adverni Civilization:** Pushing beyond the ocean, the Masters will explore the rest of Syteel, coming across the civilization of the *Adverni*, a complex coalition of city-states perched in an immense desert. The Questors will join the locals to overthrow the tyranny of a cruel desert tycoon, earning their eternal alliance.
* **The Planetary War:** Taking advantage of the temporary absence of the Masters, SpaceHub launches a campaign of global conquest on Syteel. Legacy will find itself leading a large-scale resistance alongside indigenous populations to liberate the various continents of the planet.
* **The Betrayal of Olympus:** Once the repairs to the MasterBase are complete, the Legacy fleet will launch the final offensive against the SpaceHub to extinguish it once and for all. During this campaign, shocking evidence will come to light: it was the **Olympus Foundation** that secretly helped SpaceHub get back on its feet after the Second Master War, opening a new, disturbing political scenario.
