# MasterBase Internal Crisis: The Prelude to Decline

The **MasterBase Internal Crisis** defines the dramatic period of political and psychological instability that hit the upper echelons of the guild in the aftermath of the *Beat coup*. Characterized by the progressive ideological isolation of **Entity** and the head-on clash with **Alone**, this parenthesis triggered a chain reaction that led to the fragmentation of the historical core of the Masters and the beginning of the so-called "fall of the MasterBase".

---

# I. Entity's Paranoia and the Halo Rift

Following thwarted threats and political wounds left by previous conflicts, the weight of command profoundly alters the psychology of Entity. The supreme leader develops an acute and suffocating distrust towards his own subordinates, fearing internal conspiracies and betrayals in every sector of the station.

This climate of generalized suspicion finds its breaking point in the relationship with **Alone**, one of the most influential, esteemed and charismatic Masters of the guild.
* Driven by an increasingly authoritarian and isolationist attitude, Entity begins to perceive Alone's independence of thought as a direct threat to his leadership.
* At the height of a harsh verbal clash and without the unanimous consent of the council, Entity performs the act of force: **officially relieves Alone of his role as Master**, depriving him of all authority on board.

---

# II. The Internal Revolt and the Great Exile

The unjustified torpedoing of Alone unleashes an unprecedented wave of indignation among the station's Questors. Most of the historic members, veterans and young people who grew up under Alone's wing refuse to bow to Entity's dictatorial drift.

What began as a diplomatic protest quickly turns into a **real internal revolt**. Alone's supporters attempt to subvert the command's latest decisions, splitting the MasterBase into two opposing factions.

However, by tightening control over StarBase's security systems, Entity manages to quell the insurrection. The final act of the crisis takes place with the issuing of an emergency decree: **Alone and the entire circle of friends, allies and Questors who had taken part in the revolt are condemned to immediate exile** and expelled from the space station.

---

# III. Consequences and Status Quo

> ### ── The Beginning of the Fall ──
> This event is unanimously considered by Alpha Sphere historians to be the starting point of the military and political decline of the MasterBase. The forced removal of a massive percentage of its elite force has left the guild severely weakened and deprived of internal cohesion.

While the MasterBase sinks into an isolationist silence under the leadership of an increasingly distrustful Entity entrenched in its BattleDeck, the exiled dissidents will find temporary asylum on board the *Arpeggio* starbase. It is precisely among the ranks of these hunted rebels that, shortly thereafter, the independent guild of **[[en/Cast/Groups/Blue Owls/Blue Owls|Blue Owls]]** will officially come to life, transforming old brothers in arms into the fiercest competitive rivals of MasterBase.
