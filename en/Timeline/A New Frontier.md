### Historical Context
Around **2015**, the [[en/Map/Alpha Sphere/Talos System/Planet Aura/Planet Aura|planet Aura]] reaches a crucial technological breakthrough: interplanetary and interstellar travel becomes public knowledge. This progress triggers an unprecedented expansion into unexplored regions of space.

### The [[en/Glossary/Guild|guilds]] of the [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] and the [[en/Glossary/StarBase|starbase]]
In this scenario, the **Quester Guilds** emerge as protagonists. The pillar of their expansion is represented by **Starbases**: autonomous and self-propelled space stations, specifically designed to facilitate the colonization and conquest of deep space.

### Intercultural Contact
With the massive diffusion of travel technology, the Aural Questar culture comes into direct contact with Questar civilizations on other planets. These alien societies are also organized in a system of Guilds, in turn equipped with their own Starbases, giving rise to an era of interaction, exchange and competition between different space cultures.
