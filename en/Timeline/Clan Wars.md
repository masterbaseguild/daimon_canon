#ClanWars

The **Clan Wars** are a famous and impressive free team fighting tournament organized in the sectors by the **[[en/Cast/Groups/Disbanded/SpaceFox|SpaceFox]]** guild, under the direct supervision and direction of its leader **[[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Aledge|Aledge]]**. The event stands as a spiritual and technical ancestor of the future elite tournaments of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]].

---

# I. Context and Historical Significance

The Clan Wars initiative was undertaken in the period following the devastating [[en/Timeline/SpaceFox vs WorldOrder|war against WorldOrder]]. Consumed by the huge economic and military losses suffered in the conflict, SpaceFox plans this tournament with the dual strategic objective of reaffirming itself as a central hub and geopolitical point of reference in the sectors and, at the same time, carrying out a systematic scouting of new talents to replenish its decimated ranks.

The event is structured as a brutal and highly spectacular competition. Although the channeling of tensions between factions in an arena has as a side effect a partial and temporary regulation of clashes in the sectors, the primary purpose remains closely linked to the consolidation and recruitment of the organizing guild. Many of the fighters and leaders who stand out in the Clan Wars arenas become key members of SpaceFox and related entities in time to come.
