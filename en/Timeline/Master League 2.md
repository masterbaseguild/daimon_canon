# Master League 2: The Age of Free Combat

**Master League 2** made history as the **first true inter-regional league** fully completed and regulated within the MasterBase quadrants. Unlike the first experiment (interrupted by the winds of war), this edition introduced the **Free Team Combat** format, allowing guilds, city districts and weapons clubs to clash without restrictions on style or arsenal.

The enormous turnout of these players forced the organizing committee to structure the competition on three hierarchical pyramid levels: **Serie A**, **Serie B** and **Serie C**, introducing for the first time a system of historic promotions, relegations and repechages.
