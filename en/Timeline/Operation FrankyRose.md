# Operation FrankyRose: The Ransom of Gondraw

**Operation FrankyRose** defines one of the most delicate, strategic and personal cutting-edge stealth operations conducted within the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]]. The extraction, conceived and carried out personally by **Emily Rose**, saw the guild penetrate the orbit of the planet Gondoraw with the aim of exfiltrating their cousin **Garo Franky**, removing him from the clutches of the family's criminal cartel and officially integrating him as a military resource among the ranks of the Masters.

---

# I. The Prelude: Emily's Redemption

After escaping the Ring (the brutal underground fighting circuit run by the Franky clan) and finding a new purpose and identity thanks to the sacrifice of her mentor **Vaximo**, Emily Rose had firmly established herself aboard the MasterBase, becoming one of the guild's most feared and trusted warriors.

However, the bond with his home planet was never completely broken. Through the dense network of information contacts left behind by the *Olympus Foundation*, Emily discovers that her cousin, **Garo Franky**, is in a position of extreme danger. Guilty of no longer supporting the ruthless and corrupt methods of the family syndicate and suspected of treason, Garo is now a moving target with no options for survival on Gondraw. Determined not to allow another member of her circle to be sacrificed, Emily appears before Entity to get the green light and the assault vectors needed for an immediate extraction mission.

---

# II. Infiltration and Extraction

The operation is planned as a surgical stealth raid to avoid starting an open war with the planetary forces and the Franky defense fleet. Exploiting a blind window in Gondoraw's radar, Emily Rose leads a small elite commando unit to the surface, navigating the depths of the Capital she once called home.

Emily manages to intercept Garo just as the ring of clan assassins is closing in around him. Demonstrating the lethality and martial coolness accumulated during her years in the Masters, Emily neutralizes the enemy vanguards before they can raise the alarm, breaking the encirclement and rescuing her cousin.

During the frantic escape to the extraction modules, Emily addresses Garo not only as a member of the family, but as a representative of the guild: **formally invites him to leave behind the Franky name, the feuds of Gondraw and join the MasterBase**, where his tactical knowledge and his arm would be used for a greater cause.

---

# III. Final Consequences and Status Quo

The operation ends with a total and strategic success, despite the strong reaction and resentment of the Gondora crime syndicate, which sees two of its most precious elements definitively escape its control.

* **Garo's Integration:** Accepting her cousin's invitation, Garo Franky boards the Masters' flagship. Subjected to the interview with Entity, the Questor makes all his ballistic skills and logistical information on the Franky clan available to the guild, being integrated into the vanguard.
* **The Axis of Roses:** The success of the operation solidifies Emily and Garo's position as one of MasterBase's most close-knit and lethal fighting pairs, bound by a deep blood debt and determined to protect their new interstellar home from any future corporate or criminal threats.
