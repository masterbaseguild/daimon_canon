# Lignum Nobili x MasterBase: The Wanderer's Alliance

During his long period of isolation as a *rogue* among the frontier sectors, **Entity** did not remain idle. The **Lignum Nobili x MasterBase** chapter documents the chance meeting between the exiled leader and the young but promising **Lignum Nobili** guild, founded by **Albe**. This partnership represents one of the first real steps towards the reconstruction of the MasterBase diplomatic network and the birth of the future **MasterAlliance**.

---

# I. The Encounter in the Border Territories

As he traveled under an assumed name, stripped of the ranks and insignia that once defined him, Entity found himself observing, from the shadows, the rapid rise of the Lignum Nobili. The guild, led by Albe, stood out in the panorama of the sectors for an atypical code of honor, a strong sense of community cohesion and a tactical freshness that recalled, in its intentions, the first fervor of the MasterBase.

Instead of approaching them as the leader of a superpower, Entity chose to observe them "from below", testing the solidity of their team spirit and the effectiveness of their combat system.

---

# II. The Albe Tournament

To test the guild, Entity decided to participate in a tournament organized by the Lignum Nobili. The event not only served to measure the martial potential of the members of the Albe guild, but served as a real "character test" in the eyes of the former commander.

During the final stages of the tournament, Entity acted unconventionally:
* **Discrete participation:** Despite his clear technical superiority, Entity chose not to overwhelm his opponents, but to guide and stimulate them, testing Albe's ability to manage crises during matches.
* **Command Evaluation:** Entity observed how Albe handled his subordinates under pressure, noting a leadership based on meritocracy and mutual support, elements that Entity himself had tragically lost during his *Downfall*.

---

# III. The Birth of the MasterAlliance

At the end of the competition, impressed by the human and strategic potential of the Lignum Nobili, Entity decided to reveal his true identity to Albe. It was a moment of profound diplomatic clarity: Entity did not propose forced assimilation or submission, but a strategic partnership of equals.

> ### ── The Entity Proposal ──
> "Your guild has what mine has forgotten: a clear vision and a trust not yet tainted. I do not ask you to be my soldiers, but my brothers in a greater cause."

The invitation to join the **MasterAlliance** was enthusiastically accepted by Albe. This pact marked a fundamental turning point:
1. **Diplomatic rebirth:** The Lignum Nobili brought new lifeblood and unprecedented contacts to the border sectors.
2. **Integration Model:** The entry of the Lignum Nobili served as an example for the future expansion of the Alliance, demonstrating that the MasterBase had once again become an inclusive entity, capable of integrating independent guilds while respecting their autonomy.

---

# IV. Status Quo and Historical Impact

The alliance with the Lignum Nobili acted as a mirror for Entity: seeing Albe's determination helped the leader purify himself of the last residues of the paranoia that had caused the collapse of his base. The Lignum Nobili's entry into the MasterAlliance was not just a military gain, but the **first true success in the Entity's path to redemption**, proving to the veterans of the old guard that their leader had changed and that the MasterBase had finally learned to trust others again.
