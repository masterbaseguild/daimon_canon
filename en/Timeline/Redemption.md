# Redemption: Espionage and Redemption

**Redemption** defines the era of subterranean plots and counter-deceptions that saw the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]] under the silent threat of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Xadrium Corporation/Xadrium Corporation|Xadrium Corporation]]. The era is characterized by the failed infiltration attempt by biological spies, culminating in the historical redemption of **Beat** and the failed military boarding of the *CC3*.

---

# I. Infiltration and the Spy Game

The chapter opens with the entry into the MasterBase of an ambitious quester known as **Black Paladin**. Intent on quickly making a career and climbing the internal hierarchy, Black Paladin throws himself headlong into a massive recruitment campaign to prove his worth to the high command. Among the various elements he manages to bring on board, the names **Beat** and **Blu** stand out.

The two newcomers, however, hide a secret: they are actually **elite Xadrium spies**, infiltrated with the aim of collecting sensitive data and sabotaging the StarBase from within.

### Beat's Counter-Deception
Xadrium's plan begins to falter after an intense and private conversation between Beat and [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Entity|Entity]]. Deeply impressed by the vision and philosophy of the leader of the Masters, **Beat decides to betray Xadrium**.
To protect the guild without blowing his own cover, Beat carries out a subtle misdirection against his own companion Blue, firmly maintaining that there is "nothing interesting" to steal on board the station. Soon after, Beat secretly and fully joins the Masters' defense team. As for Black Paladin, his unbridled thirst for power and his ambiguous ties soon come to light, decreeing the early end of his career within the foundation.

---

# II. The CC3 Boarding and Psychological Warfare

While the espionage plot is still in full swing, the MasterBase suddenly finds itself vulnerable: due to an external mission, most of the veteran Masters are absent, leaving the station undefended.

Taking advantage of the situation of numerical superiority, the hostile faction of **CC3** launches a lightning-fast boarding attack in an attempt to conquer the StarBase. The responsibility for the defense of the station falls entirely on the shoulders of **Blu** and another key operative, **Beat** (who, in this precise time bracket, is still in the middle of his very personal arc of espionage against the Masters).

### The Clash with Stelio and Nick's Chaos
The CC3 attack is led by *Stelio*, who attempts to destabilize the defenders through a dense and exhausting psychological war made up of threats and manipulations. However, Blue and Beat respond with equal and superior strategic acumen, neutralizing every single psychological move of the enemy.

During the excited phases of the boarding, the situation becomes further complicated due to the chaos generated by Nick's mind control techniques, which risk decimating the defensive lines. Despite the extreme pressure, the Masters demonstrate their clear military superiority in the field, reorganizing the front lines and repelling the CC3 incursion without suffering significant losses.

---

# III. Beat's Redemption

> ### ── The Beat Path ──
> Originally entering MasterBase with the sole purpose of acting as a spy and saboteur for outside forces, his baptism of fire while boarding the CC3 changes Beat's mindset forever.

Finding himself fighting side by side with those he was supposed to destroy, and directly witnessing the value, trust and strength of the station's police officers, Beat goes through a profound identity crisis that leads him to definitively deny his past as an infiltrator.

Having abandoned his old instigators, Beat chooses the path of redemption, offering his unconditional loyalty to Entity and becoming in all respects one of the official members and most reliable warriors of the **Masters** vanguard.

---

# IV. Final Status Quo

The *Redemption* era ends with the total failure of Xadrium's infiltration plots. The MasterBase has not only repelled the military attack of the CC3 and overcome the internal mind control crises, but has converted two of the most dangerous spies sent against it (Beat and Beat) into faithful pillars of its elite force, emerging from the crisis more cohesive and militarily prepared.
