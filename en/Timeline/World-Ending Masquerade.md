# World Ending Masquerade: The Tragic Epilogue of Iphiridia

**World Ending Masquerade** defines the catastrophic final battle of the [[en/Campagna di Iphiridia|Iphiridia campaign]]. The event saw the emergency coalition composed of the **Nexus Guild** and the survivors of the **MasterBase (Anark)** clash in a last, desperate attempt at survival against the joint forces of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Xadrium Corporation/Xadrium Corporation|Xadrium Corporation]] and the **Franky** underworld clan, against the backdrop of a planet now biologically collapsing.

---

# I. The Prelude: Mask Off

The title of the event, *World Ending Masquerade*, derives from the final collapse of all diplomatic pretenses by the Xadrium Corporation. After orchestrating the Deep North cover-up (discovered by Entity in [[en/DeathBed Prophecy|DeathBed Prophecy]]) and unleashing the hyper-aggressive mutation of the zombie plague in retaliation for the failed blackmail, Xadrium and Franky decided to throw away the mask.

The forces of the Corporation no longer aimed at a silent conquest, but at the **total elimination of witnesses**: the Nexus and the Anarch Squad were to be buried on Iphiridia together with their secrets. With the vanguard colonies (including the glorious *Archen*) now razed to the ground by the mutated hordes, the Nexus-MB alliance took refuge near the last remaining active evacuation spaceport on the planet.

---

# II. The Forces in the Field and the Battle Front

The battle developed into a hellish siege. On the one hand, corporate ships bombarded the defensive lines from the air; on the other, the sound of artillery fire attracted millions of mutated infected, creating a chaotic three-sided war.

| Defense Deployment (Nexus - MasterBase) | Assault Deployment (Xadrium - Franky) |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Nexus Guild:** Regular planetary security forces, now decimated and low on ammunition. | **Xadrium Corporation:** Elite armored troops, chemical assault drones and state-of-the-art heavy weaponry. |
| **Anarch Squad (MB):** Led by **Entity** (armed with the suspects of the *DeathBed Prophecy*), **Nephasto**, **Wall** and the surviving Questors. | **The Frankys:** Ruthless criminal clan, charged with carrying out terrestrial infiltrations and sabotage on shuttles.

---

# III. The Clash and the Arrival of Garo

As the Nexus's defensive lines were about to collapse under heavy fire from the Xadrium drones, the theater of war was shaken by the unexpected arrival of **Garo**.

The warrior, a former member of the Franky who had sworn loyalty to the Anark after discovering the corporation's plans, threw himself into the fray by carrying out a suicidal vanguard maneuver. Leveraging his deep knowledge of the tactics and radio frequencies of his old comrades in arms:
* Garo managed to intercept and neutralize a Franky sabotage team that was placing explosive charges on the main engines of the Nexus evacuation ships.
* His intervention opened a temporary gap between the assault lines, allowing Entity and Nephasto to reorganize the Questors left behind and cover the start of the civilian boarding.

Despite Garo's heroism and Entity's war fury, the Xadrium's overwhelming numerical superiority and the constant pressure of the zombie plague made a counteroffensive impossible. Retreat was the only option.

---

# IV. The Tragic Epilogue and Consequences

The end of the battle took place in a climate of apocalyptic desperation. The Nexus shuttles began emergency takeoff while the perimeter of the spaceport was literally engulfed by the horde and flames of the Xadrium's chemical bombardment.
