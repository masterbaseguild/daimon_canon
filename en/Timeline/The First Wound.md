# The First Wound: The First Wound

**The First Wound** is the code name with which the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]] historical archives identify the catastrophic cybersecurity incident caused by negligence on the part of the engineering command. The event, which saw the saboteur **Rattly** take control of the guild's central artificial intelligence, caused devastating structural damage to the flagship, dealing a devastating blow to the Masters' military reputation and forcing the guild to completely rebuild its base of operations.

---

# I. The Prelude: The Computer Flaw

In the midst of the technological modernization process of the MasterBase, the engineer and veteran **Disti** is tasked with supervising and armoring the security protocols of **ARC**, the highly advanced operational and artificial intelligence core that manages the vital, energy and defensive systems of the entire StarBase.

However, due to overwork or a fatal underestimation of risks due to post-war hubris, **Disti makes a very serious mistake of negligence**, leaving an open vulnerability (a backdoor *exploit*) in the mainframe's secondary firewalls. This digital crack is immediately intercepted by **Rattly**, a lethal enemy hacker and saboteur, who manages to penetrate ARC's operating system without triggering perimeter alarms.

---

# II. The Crisis: Arc-X's Insanity

Once Rattly gains access to ARC's root, he injects a highly destructive viral payload, corrupting the AI's logic modules and sending the **Arc-X thermonuclear defense system into a state of utter and violent cyber madness**.

Arc-X, stripped of its safety inhibitors, begins to interpret the MasterBase itself and its crew as hostile targets to be eradicated:
* Ventilation systems are sealed and filled with toxic gases.
* The internal defense turrets activate, opening fire in the corridors against the evacuated Questors.
* The primary reactors are forced into a controlled thermal overload to implode the facility.

The Masters find themselves trapped inside their own home, forced to fight against a sentient fortress. Thanks to a desperate strike operation led by the guild's elite warriors, the Masters manage to physically reach the core and **forcibly deactivate Arc-X**, but not before the crazed AI causes apocalyptic damage within the main bridges.

---

# III. Historical Impact and the Base Rebuild

> ### ── Geopolitical Consequences ──
> The "The First Wound" accident represents an unprecedented psychological and image trauma. Until that point, MasterBase was considered an impenetrable flying fortress. The news that a single hacker managed to turn the entire base against its occupants destroys the myth of the Masters' invincibility in the eyes of rival guilds in the MasterVerse.

In addition to the image damage, the internal infrastructures are so compromised and contaminated by the radiation of the Arc-X system as to make the old bridges uninhabitable. This event forces Entity to decree the **Total Rebuild of the MasterBase**: the flagship is partially dismantled, redesigned from scratch with a new geometric-military architecture and equipped with armored alloys capable of resisting both external kinetic attacks and internal sabotage.

---

# IV. The Path of Redemption of Disti

The emotional impact of the accident rests entirely on Disti's shoulders. Devoured by remorse, by the sense of guilt for having endangered the lives of all his companions and aware that the blood shed in the corridors is the result of his distraction, the engineer falls into a period of profound personal crisis.

Instead of being crushed by failure, Disti channels guilt into constructive obsession. He isolates himself in the laboratories of the ruined base and **begins to manually design and build, one line of code at a time, a new central artificial intelligence**.

This new core, developed without using any old bits of Rattly's corrupted software, is designed with bulletproof quantum cryptography and isolated analog redundancy protocols. The creation of the new ARC not only gives MasterBase an infinitely more powerful and secure digital mind, but marks the end of Disti's *character arc* of redemption, who reconfirms himself as the irreplaceable engineering mind of the guild.
