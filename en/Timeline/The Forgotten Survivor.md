# The Forgotten Survivor: Vlad's Ordeal

**The Forgotten Survivor** is the lore chapter that tells the incredible story of resistance and isolation of **Vlad**, an elite member of the **Anark** team. Forgotten on Iphiridia during the guild's hasty retreat at the end of *The Final Masquerade*, Vlad was stranded on the infected planet for seven years, surviving in total solitude against all odds, until the triumphant return of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]].

---

# I. Involuntary Sacrifice

During the emergency evacuation from Iphiridia, chaos reigned supreme. Under the constant siege of the zombie plague and the structural collapse of the headquarters, the members of the Anark team were forced to take off immediately. In the tumult, Vlad - wounded and separated from the rest of the commando during a rearguard operation - was reported missing in action.

The research protocols, unable to operate in an area now saturated with the undead, led to the inevitable conclusion: **Vlad was dead**. His name was engraved on the MasterBase war memorial, marking a deep wound in his comrades in arms.

---

# II. Seven Years of Shadows

Vlad's life on Iphiridia for the next seven years became an exercise of pure will. Without provisions and constantly hunted by the plague hordes, Vlad adapted to the wild life:
* **Tactical Nomadism:** Vlad turned the ruins of Archen into his hunting ground, constantly moving so as not to be tracked by the mutants.
* **Psychological resistance:** To maintain his sanity, Vlad kept an encrypted digital diary in the city's old servers, a chronicle of infinite loneliness and the never-ending hope of seeing his companions again.
* **Evolution:** Vlad didn't just hide; he studied the behavioral patterns of the undead, becoming an expert in their biology and learning to move among them like a ghost.

---

# III. The Reunion

When the MasterBase fleet landed on Iphiridia seven years later to claim the planet as a colony, they expected to find nothing but ruins. While clearing the northern sector of Archen, a reconnaissance team found Vlad's makeshift bunker.

The moment of discovery is described by veterans as one of the most intense in the guild's history: Vlad emerged from the shadows, emaciated but alive, bringing with him evidence of the entire history of his survival. The news of his return shook the foundations of MasterBase, bringing with it a mixture of euphoria and a deep collective sense of guilt for having abandoned him.

---

# IV. Vlad's Legacy

Vlad's reinstatement into the ranks of the Masters was not just a bureaucratic act, but a true mission of redemption:
1. **The Truth about Iphiridia:** Thanks to the data collected by Vlad over seven years, the Masters managed to understand the true nature of the virus, facilitating the definitive eradication of the plague.
2. **Symbol of Resilience:** Vlad became the living icon of the MasterBase philosophy: *no one gets left behind*. His story was used to rewrite the guild's evacuation and search protocols, which from then on became the most rigorous in the entire sector.
3. **Colonial Advisor:** With the founding of the Master Colony on Iphiridia, Vlad was appointed supervisor of security for the Archen sector, the place that had been his prison for seven years and which he now ruled as a hero returned from beyond the grave.
