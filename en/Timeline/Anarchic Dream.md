# Anarchic Dream: The Vigilante Age of North Regium

**Anarchic Dream** defines the first, historic narrative parenthesis of the young founders of Anark. Born on the high-crime streets of *North Regium*, the guild takes its first steps back and forth between the boyish dream of "playing superheroes" and the harsh reality of urban warfare, before being drawn into the global conflict between the **Olympus Foundation** and the **Asgard Foundation**.

---

# Act I: The Status Quo and the Foundation (2016)

In the suburbs of North Regium, rampant crime and the apparent absence of government authorities push a young and idealistic **Theo** (just twelve years old at the time) to try to take justice into his own hands. Driven by the desire to become a true [[en/Glossary/Quester|questor]] and clean up his town, Theo decides to take action.

Taking advantage of his proximity to an older acquaintance, the operative **Gavil**, Theo carries out a daring theft of war material, stealing a series of hi-tech armaments and particular power belts. With this improvised arsenal, Theo arms his closest childhood friends: **Eugene** (also known as *Hexatron*), **PK**, and **Bruin**.

The group officially unites under the name **Anark** (also known locally as the *Anarch Squad*). Initially the atmosphere is almost playful: for the four teenagers, arming themselves and patrolling the streets is a way to emulate comic book heroes, convinced that the government has "something better to do" than protect them.

---

# Act II: The Escalation and the New Grafts

As the months pass, the guild begins to take shape and attract the attention of other young people in the area. The ranks of Anark are strengthened with the entry of **Nephasto**, **Wall** and, above all, **Entity** (also known in the records of the first years by the pseudonyms of *Wolf*, *Little Wolf* or *LethalChaos*). Together with them, *Little Lion* and *Damashi* also join the nucleus.

The entry of Entity drastically alters the balance and philosophy of the team:
* **From Gun to Taser:** Operations undergo a clear tactical transition. What began as a group of kids with stolen weapons evolves into a coordinated and methodical urban surveillance team, reducing errors and increasing the effectiveness of interventions.
* **Rising the Scale:** Equipped with overflowing charisma and uncommon ambition, Entity begins to heavily influence the decisions of founding leader Theo. Under his pressure, the Anark abandons neighborhood petty crime to embark on increasingly larger, complex and risky missions and operations, pushing the guild far beyond its original limits.

---

# Act III: The Border of Taboo and Patronage

The dizzying escalation created by Entity leads the Anark to bite off more than it can chew. During a patrol, the young vigilantes come across a situation much bigger than them, ending up hindering and interfering in the affairs of a powerful local guild affiliated with the **Asgard Foundation**.

Hunted and in clear numerical and technological inferiority against the soldiers of Asgard, the Anark boys actually risk their lives. The team of **Gavil** and the veteran **John Drastic** (Childhood friend of Entity himself) intervene to save them from the massacre, who promptly came to the rescue to repress the enemy incursion.

### The Diplomatic Turn
In the post-clash, the confrontation between the veterans and the young rebels changes the history of the Alpha Sphere forever:

1. **Gavil's Forgiveness:** Having seen the boys' value and good faith, Gavil decides to officially forgive Theo for the theft of weapons that occurred the previous year.
2. **Drastic's Revelation:** Entity spends a lot of time with John Drastic to get answers. The mentor reveals to him the truth about the geopolitical dynamics of the planet: the North Regium guerrilla war is only the mirror of a global cold war that pits the Olympus Foundation against the expansionist threat of Asgard.
3. **The Entrance into Olympus:** Eager to concretely affect those balances and change the world, Entity convinces Theo and the rest of the guild to accept Gavil and Drastic's proposal. The Anarchist Squad definitively abandons its façade independence and officially enters the sponsored program of the **Olympus Foundation**.

### The Disciple of the Grand Magister
The Anark's entry into Olympus does not go unnoticed. **Haude**, the Supreme Grand Magister of the foundation, requests a meeting with the guild. Deeply impressed by Entity's innate talent and strategic mind, Haude decides to take him under his direct wing, naming him his personal disciple. This event will have a profound impact on the boy, who will begin to imitate the movements and style of his teacher, laying the foundations for the future birth of MasterBase.
