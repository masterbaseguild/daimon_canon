# Return to Iphiridia: 7 Years Later

The **Return to Iphiridia** marks the definitive closing of the circle for the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]]. Seven years after their catastrophic escape during *The Final Masquerade*, the guild, now militarily superior and technologically advanced, returns to the infected planet. What was once the tomb of their hopes, today becomes the **Master Colony**, the beating heart of a new imperial expansion.

---

# I. The Reclamation Operation

The need for a safe harbor in which to repair the flagship, heavily damaged by recent conflicts, pushes the Master command to choose the closest habitable planet: Iphiridia.

Upon their arrival, the situation has radically changed compared to the horror of the past. Although the cities of **Archen** and **Navirad** lie in a state of total abandonment, the zombie plague — deprived of new hosts for years — has weakened dramatically. The Masters, now forged by years of star wars and equipped with heavy *Palladium* class armaments, wipe out the remaining hordes with disarming ease, transforming the former quarantine zone into an open-air construction site.

---

# II. The Return of Vlad

During the excavation and safety work at the ruins of the city of Archen, the Masters make an incredible discovery: **Vlad**, the member of the Anarchist team who seven years earlier had been reported missing and officially declared dead.

The recordings found in his makeshift bunkers reveal a story of extreme survival: Vlad had managed to resist alone for five long years, moving among the shadows of the haunted cities, stubbornly waiting for the return of a trace of intelligent life. His reinstatement into the ranks of MasterBase is greeted with emotion and is celebrated as a symbolic event: **MasterBase no longer abandons anyone**.

---

# III. Iphiridia: New Master Colony

With the base repaired, Archen undergoing urban reconstruction, and the team finally reunited, Iphiridia is officially declared **Master Colony**. The planet becomes the nerve center of a new era of development:

* **Political Structure (Vanilla):** A colonial government based on meritocracy is established, with an administration directed by Entity and the veterans' council.
* **MasterCraft Conquest:** The planet becomes the testing ground for territorial expansion. Master-affiliated guilds launch campaigns of conquest to reclaim the wilderness, extending the boundaries of Master civilization across the globe.
* **Campaigns and Events:** Iphiridia becomes the *hub* for new tactical campaigns, with the opening of new regions in both *Nightly* (characterised by atmospheric mutations) and *Modded* (high technological areas) modes.
* **The New Global Sport (Wars):** To cement the identity of the colony and keep the adrenaline high among the Questors, a new global combat sport is introduced, transforming the ancient art of war into ritualized competition within arenas designed in the ruins of Navirad.

---

# IV. Final Status Quo

Iphiridia's transformation from "grave of the Anark" to "Jewel of the MasterAlliance" completes the guild's growth parable. Colonization is not just a strategic victory, but a powerful declaration of power: the past is no longer feared, but dominated. Iphiridia today thrives as a promised land, ready to welcome each new lever of the MasterBase under the protection of its battleships in orbit.
