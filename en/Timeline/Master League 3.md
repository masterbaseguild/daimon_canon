The **MiddleEarth Championship** was one of the most famous and experimental competitive tournaments of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]]. Featuring the return of a unique combat format, the event saw the best formations of the Alpha Sphere clash before being abruptly interrupted due to a profound internal diplomatic crisis.

# Features and Format

* **The Return of 2v2:** The competition marked the official return of the duo combat system (**2v2**), a format originally tested during pre-war tactical experiments. The reintroduction of the pairs module proved to be a huge success with the public and critics, enhancing the synergy and combined strategies of the Questors.
* **The Domination of the LitCore:** From a purely martial point of view, the tournament was literally monopolized by the members of the **LitCore** faction, who dominated the arena, defeating much of the competition thanks to their technical superiority.
* **The Cancellation:** Despite its sporting success, the MiddleEarth Championship has never seen an official final. The sudden outbreak of the political crisis known as **Aledge's Madness** - which led to the harsh clash between the tournament director Aledge and the leader Entity - caused such a rift that the organizers were forced to cancel the competition prematurely with one step away from the end.

# Narrative Impact

> ### ── Historical Note ──
> The interruption of the MiddleEarth Championship marks the beginning of the organizational schism of the tournaments. Aledge's abrupt farewell and his temporary exit from the guild will freeze the official competitive activities of the MasterBase, temporarily shifting the focus of the competitions to the external networks created by the director during his exile.
