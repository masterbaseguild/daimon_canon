# Downfall of Olympus

The **Downfall of Olympus** defines one of the most dramatic and crucial eras in the geopolitics of the Alpha Sphere. Born from the discovery of the cynical double game of the Grand Magister **Haude**, the event marked the end of the historic [[en/Cast/Groups/Olympus Foundation/Olympus Foundation|Olympus Foundation]] and redesigned the axis of planetary power on Aura through a ruthless opportunistic operation led by the alliance between the [[en/Cast/Groups/Asgard Foundation|Asgard]] and the [[en/Cast/Groups/Xadrium Corporation/Xadrium Corporation|Xadrium Corporation]].

---

# I. The Shocking Revelation

While the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]] is engaged in reconstruction and exploration after the efforts of the [[en/Timeline/Master War III|Third War Master]], the secret services bring to light a truth that freezes the high command. The funding and resources that had allowed *SpaceHub* to reorganize and unleash the mercenaries of the *Crimson Sphere* came from the Olympus Foundation.

### Haude's Motives
Although [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Entity|Entity]] had always believed in the unwavering loyalty of the faction that had acted as godmother to the Anark, the philosophy of the Grand Magister **Haude** proves ruthless. Haude did not seek peace, but the creation of the perfect guild through natural selection. Its objective was to **secretly support and finance the guilds that intended to clash with each other**, providing the means for rivals to force MasterBase (and the entire sector) into a constant war evolution dictated by the survival of the fittest.

---

# II. The Clash and the Fratricidal Collapse

MasterBase decides not to remain silent and directly confronts Olympus. The revelation of the secret dossiers tears the foundation apart from the inside: one after the other, the various Magisters of Olympus are faced with the crazy actions of their leader, causing a real structural and ideological collapse of the guild.

While MasterBase and Olympus wear themselves out in a fierce close combat, the shadow of the **Xadrium Corporation** lengthens over the conflict. Xadrium makes a pact of steel with the **Asgard Foundation**:
* Xadrium uses its political and legal channels to **officially outlaw Olympus** on a global scale.
* Asgard, in exchange, obtains total military freedom of action to launch a large-scale invasion attack, taking advantage of the chaos to **take absolute control of the planet Aura**.

---

# III. The Siege and Sacrifice of Haude

The armies of Asgard fall like a hammer on the two factions exhausted by the internal conflict. Olympus headquarters turns into a death trap.

In a final and unexpected gesture of redemption, aware of the disaster caused by his own manipulations, **Haude decides to sacrifice himself on the battlefield**. The Grand Magister erects a total magical barrier, offering his life to contain the advance of Asgard's assault troops and **allow the MasterBase to break the siege circle and escape**.

### Winter's Key Role
During the excited and desperate phases of the retreat, the intervention of [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Winter|Winter]] proved fundamental for the survival of the Masters. Using his devastating firepower and the mobility of his *Propulsion* technique, the firebender positions himself on the front line to cover the evacuation gaps. Winter single-handedly fends off entire Asgard battalions, containing the losses and allowing the surviving Questors to escape to safety before the entire Olympus fortress finally collapses.

---

# IV. Final Status Quo: Aura's Resistance

With Olympus totally collapsed and destroyed, the political landscape of the Alpha Sphere has radically changed. Asgard now holds a military monopoly on Aura with Xadrium's approval, but the Masters refuse to give up.

Feeling morally responsible to continue the interrupted work of their historic protectors, the higher-ups at MasterBase initiate a long-term contingency plan:

1. **The Recovery of the Missing:** The Masters launch clandestine operations to track down, save and unite among their ranks all the Olympus Questors and Magisters who escaped the massacre and were left without a guide.
2. **The Anti-Asgard Secret Network:** Exploiting the old underground bunkers and shadow channels of Olympus, the MasterBase weaves a **secret network of espionage and guerrilla warfare on Aura**, preparing in the shadows to undermine the new oppressive regime and form a common front against the threat of the Xadrium.
