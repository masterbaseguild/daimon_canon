# The Phoenix Crew: Origins of the Navirad

This chapter of lore documents the founding, dramatic exodus, and subsequent reintegration of the **Navirad**. Born as an independent guild in the wilds of Iphiridia, this faction of Questors experienced the trauma of the failure of the *Nexus Campaign* before rebuilding themselves in space and officially joining their destiny with that of the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]].

---

# Act I: The City on Iphiridia (The Nexus Campaign)

It all begins on Aura, when two young and ambitious adventurers, **Kurymi** and **Revoost**, receive the official announcement of the launch of the **Nexus Campaign** — the titanic military operation aimed at colonizing and conquering the planet Iphiridia. Intent on seeking their fortune and testing their skills, the two accept the call and board the first state-of-the-art ferry available.

Once landed on the surface, Kurymi and Revoost meet another duo of Questors: **GasticNox** and **Black Ace**. Finding an immediate strategic understanding, the four decide to join forces and found an autonomous guild, called **Navirad**.

The group immediately focuses their resources on building a fortified outpost: the **City of Navirad**. GasticNox immediately and deeply became attached to the base, seeing it as the cradle of their future. Shortly afterwards, to expand the ranks, the guild begins field recruitment, welcoming the promising **Winter Phoenix** and two other members into its ranks.

---

# Act II: The Disaster and the StarBase Project

The idyll of colonization ends abruptly with the collapse of the planetary defensive line. On the tragic day of the failure of the Nexus Campaign, the company is reluctantly forced to **abandon their beloved base** to escape the massacre. During the desperate and bloody escape towards the evacuation ships, the guild suffers a heavy blow: **the two newly recruited Questors are killed** under enemy fire.

Returning to Aura as refugees and without a home, the spirit of the survivors does not break. GasticNox discovers that Kurymi plans to design and launch an **independent orbiting StarBase** to take the guild into deep space.

Driven by a single, unwavering goal — *to amass enough power to one day return to Iphiridia, liberate it, and rebuild the City of Navirad* — GasticNox immediately offers his full support to Kurymi. The project is successful: the StarBase is launched into space and the Navirad is entirely gathered on board to form the elite crew.

---

# Act III: The Ideological Turning Point and the Union with the Masters

During a patrol operation in the cosmos, **Winter Phoenix** comes across the vanguard of the MasterBase. Impressed by their goals and their power, he decides to accept Entity's offer and joins the team.

Once integrated, Winter acts as a catalyst: with his enthusiasm he infects the other members of his old company, quickly leading to the recruitment of **Kurymi** and **Revoost** within the MasterBase.

This process greatly annoys GasticNox, who sees his historic companions being absorbed by another guild and perceives his dream of returning to Navirad as having now gone up in smoke.

### The Face to Face with Entity
The decisive turning point comes when GasticNox himself's turn comes to sit at the recruiting table. Speaking directly with **Entity**, Gastic discovers a fundamental truth: **the Masters had also participated in the Nexus Campaign**, suffering equally devastating losses, and they too cultivated the exact same secret dream of one day returning to free Iphiridia.

---

# Final Status Quo

Understanding that MasterBase shares the exact same blood and end goal, GasticNox accepts entry into the faction.

The event ends with Navirad becoming in effect an **official Sub-Guild of the MasterBase**. Their StarBase ceases to wander as an isolated entity and is integrated into the **Global Master Fleet**, joining forces for the future reconquest of their lost planet.
