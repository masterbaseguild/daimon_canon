# MasterBase Rising: The Birth of the Masters

**MasterBase Rising** defines the era of transition, crisis and subsequent rebirth that led to the end of the [[en/Timeline/Anarchic Dream|Anark]] experience and the founding of **MasterBase**. Going through the disintegration of the historical core and a massive interstellar recruitment campaign, the group abandons the planet Aura to launch its own counteroffensive in deep space.

---

# I. The Post-Iphiridia Crisis and the Breakdown

The trauma and heavy losses suffered during the failure of the **Iphiridia Campaign** shatter the group's status quo. The reaction among the survivors of the Anarchist Squad is dramatic:

* **Theo and Eugene's Farewell:** Grieving the loss of his childhood companions, founding leader Theo openly blames Wolf for the disastrous outcome of the campaign and abandons the team. Shortly after, Eugene (*Hexatron*) and other historic members also follow his example, leaving the guild on the brink of dissolution.
* **The Birth of Entity:** Left alone to lead the few survivors (including *Wall* and *Nephasto*), Wolf decides to suffocate his remorse. Aware of having acted by applying the cold teachings of his master Haude to the letter, he decides to move on. To reflect this drastic internal growth and cut ties with the past, he abandons his old nickname and takes on the definitive identity of **Entity**, officially taking absolute command of the faction.

---

# II. The Recruitment Campaign

Determined to rebuild the organization on a global scale, Entity launches a dense network of strategic and diplomatic operations to replenish the ranks with elite questers:

### 1. The Olympus Tournament
During the famous competitive event ***The Olympus Grand Tournament***, Entity takes advantage of the showcase offered by the foundation to observe the best talents of the Alpha Sphere. At the end of the competition, he manages to officially sign **Jey**, inserting him as the backbone of the new lineup.

### 2. The Infiltration of the Dark Angels
To further expand the network and gather forces for future projects, Entity and **Garo** implement a military infiltration maneuver, temporarily militating within a foreign guild known as **Dark Angels**. During this interlude, the two forge strong bonds with prominent questers such as *Turfy* and *Ayako*, managing to convince several key members to defect to join their cause.

### 3. Operation FrankyRose
Garo embarks on a risky stealth mission on his home planet, Gondora. The objective of the operation is twofold: to escape the control of the fearsome *Franky Family* and recover his cousin **Emily**, whose extraordinary war skills will prove fundamental for the future vanguard lines of the guild.
