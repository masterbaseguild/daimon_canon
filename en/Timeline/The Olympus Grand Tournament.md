# The Olympus Grand Tournament: The Tournament of Champions

**The Olympus Grand Tournament** defines one of the most prestigious and exclusive fencing and sword fighting championships organized periodically by the high command of the [[en/Cast/Groups/Olympus Foundation/Olympus Foundation|Olympus Foundation]]. The event went down in history for having propitiated the decisive meeting between the supreme leader **Entity** and the sword master **Jey**, shifting the axis of elite talents towards the nascent [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]].

---

# I. The Olympus Sword Circle

The Olympus Foundation, among its many training and recruiting activities in the Alpha Sphere, sponsored a renowned and elite **Sword Circle**. This club served as an academy for the best fencers in the business, a place where high-rankingquesters, knights, and mercenaries competed to hone their gaff fencing techniques and enhance their reputations.

The *Grand Tournament* was the culmination of these activities: a single-elimination championship in which only the most gifted fighters received the honor of crossing blades. Among the most feared, graceful and technically unstoppable contenders, the name of **Jey** stood out, considered by many to be the ideal candidate to climb the top hierarchies of Olympus.

---

# II. The Encounter with Entity

During the final stages of the tournament, **Entity** attends the event as an exceptional spectator and tactical observer, intent on studying the war potential of the factions affiliated with Olympus.

Witnessing the close-quarters duels in the ring, Entity is deeply impressed by Jey's fighting style, characterized by pinpoint precision and cold determination in battle. At the end of the matches, the two leaders have the opportunity to get to know each other and discuss privately. During the dialogue, Entity illustrates to Jey his futuristic vision of the future: the transition towards a united and militarized interstellar organization (the MasterBase), free from the rigid and dusty bureaucratic shackles of the Olympus Foundation.

---

# III. Consequences and Status Quo

Entity's words and the prospect of testing his sword in real war scenarios have a strong ideological appeal to Jey. Realizing that the Sword Circle has nothing left to teach him and that Olympus's horizons have become too narrow for its potential, the warrior makes a radical choice:

* **The Breakup with Olympus:** Jey officially resigns and **permanently abandons the Sword Club** and the Foundation, renouncing the privileges linked to his champion status.
* **Entry into the Masters:** The elite swordsman officially joins the **MasterBase**, converting his formidable swordsmanship into a lethal asset for Entity's vanguard. His addition will help dramatically raise the guild's standards of melee combat, preparing Questors for future sector warfare.
