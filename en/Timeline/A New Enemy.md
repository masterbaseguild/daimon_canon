# A New Enemy: The Xilvyn Incursion

The chapter **A New Enemy** marks the opening of a new, dramatic war front for the [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/MasterBase|MasterBase]]. The discovery and subsequent dimensional connection with the planet **Xilvyn** triggered a crisis of galactic proportions, bringing to the soil of **Syteel** — one of the key worlds under Master influence — a population of desperate, ferocious, technologically driven warriors oriented towards total predation.

---

# I. Xilvyn: The World of Scarcity

**Xilvyn** is classified by MasterBase observers as a "sister world" of Syteel, but radically different in terms of climate and economic conditions. It is an extremely poor planet, almost entirely desertified and devoid of stable natural energy sources.

This chronic lack of resources has forged a dystopian society, divided into warlike tribes that have been fighting each other for generations in a total civil war for control of every drop of water, gram of metal or energy residue. The fight for survival on Xilvyn produced not scientific advancement, but brutal and highly efficient **raid and adapt technology**, making the Xilvians born soldiers, accustomed to fighting in conditions of extreme deprivation.

---

# II. The Opening of the Portal

The turning point occurs when, following an energy experiment gone out of control in an ancient auric structure on Syteel, a **space-time singularity** occurs. The open portal connects the tortured heart of Xilvyn directly to the fertile regions of Syteel.

The leaders of the Xylvian tribes, sensing the energetic wealth of the arrival world, do not hesitate. Instead of attempting a diplomatic approach, they launch a massive invasion. The first Xylvian units, equipped with rudimentary equipment but adapted for guerrilla warfare in hostile environments, pass through the portal instantly transforming Syteel into a new battlefield.

---

# III. Geopolitical Impact on Syteel

The arrival of the Xylvians creates panic. For Syteel, which had until then enjoyed relative stability under the aegis of the MasterAlliance, the invasion represents an existential threat:
* **Guerrilla Tactics:** Invaders do not fight for territorial conquest, but for the systematic draining of resources. They attack depots, power plants and convoys, then vanish into thin air.
* **Psychological Terror:** The Xilvians' desperate nature makes them unpredictable adversaries; they are ready to sacrifice themselves to steal even the smallest quantity of fuel or basic necessities.
* **Challenge for the Masters:** The MasterBase, fresh from the reorganization on Iphiridia, finds itself forced to manage an asymmetric conflict. Entity must decide whether to seal the portal (isolating Syteel but losing a potential trade route) or launch a counteroffensive directly on Xilvyn's home world.

---

# IV. Status quo

The war on Syteel has just begun. MasterBase has already mobilized its elite forces to guard the portal, but the unpredictability of the Xilvians is putting a strain on the defensive lines.

> ### ── Note from the Military Archive ──
> The threat of Xilvyn cannot be resolved with a simple pitched battle. Since the home planet is a wasteland, the Xilvians have nothing to lose. This characteristic makes them the most dangerous enemy that MasterBase has ever had to face: an adversary that does not fear destruction, because he experiences it every day.

The MasterAlliance is considering a forced pacification intervention or, alternatively, an infiltration mission on Xilvyn to close the portal at the source.
