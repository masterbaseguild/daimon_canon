# DeathBed Prophecy: The Deathbed Prophecy

**DeathBed Prophecy** defines a crucial and dramatic investigative and psychological event that took place in the desolate lands of the deep north of Iphiridia. Characterized by a ruthless cover-up maneuver orchestrated by the [[en/Cast/Groups/Xadrium Corporation/Xadrium Corporation|Xadrium Corporation]], the episode went down in history for having unleashed the deep and exhausting trail of suspicions and paranoia that redefined the psychology of [[en/Cast/Groups/MasterBase/Entity|Entity]] towards the strong powers of the Alpha Sphere.

---

# I. The Xadrium Cover-up and the False Hunt

In the course of its clandestine operations on Iphiridia, the Xadrium Corporation establishes an ultra-secret and heavily fortified base of operations in the deep northern territories of the planet. To protect their experiments and prevent prying eyes from discovering the nature of their research, the corporation's leaders issue a ruthless directive: **execute on the spot anyone who gets too close to the perimeter of the complex**.

When the number of questers, mercenaries and explorers disappearing into thin air begins to become worrying, uncontrolled rumors begin to circulate in the sector. To avoid official investigations by Olympus or the major guilds, Xadrium implements a cynical disinformation campaign:
* The blame for the massacres is placed entirely on a mysterious and controversial **ice sorceress** who resides as a hermit in that same area of ​​the Arctic.
* The corporation spreads the official explanation that the woman has gone completely mad, turning into a ruthless and territorial serial killer.
* To give credibility to the farce and definitively get rid of the hermit, Xadrium places a **very heavy international bounty** on the sorceress' head.

---

# II. The Confrontation in the Frostlands

Attracted by the bounty and eager to cleanse the sector from an unpredictable threat, Entity accepts the assignment and sets out on the trail of the wanted woman. Once the woman's refuge has been identified among the perpetual ice, the leader of the Masters launches the offensive.

The fight turns out to be harsh and brutal. The ice sorceress defends herself with pinpoint ferocity: the woman, in fact, had actually started killing in recent weeks, but **exclusively in legitimate self-defense** in a desperate attempt to repel the Xadrium hitmen who were trying to eliminate her.

Despite her powerful elemental magic, the sorceress is powerless against Entity's superior war power. Seriously injured and near death, the woman collapses to the ground, but it is precisely in this tragic moment that the event deviates from its original course.

---

# III. The Last Doubt and Prophecy

> ### ── The Hermit's Last Words ──
> Gathering the very last strength left in her body and drawing on her auric energy to delay death just enough, the ice sorceress stares into Entity's eyes. Instead of begging for mercy, the woman uses her final breaths to spit out the truth, revealing the details of the cover-up and blaming the corporation.

The intensity and desperate clarity of his testimony hit Entity like a hammer. The sorceress perfectly succeeds in her posthumous intent: **to instill the seed of doubt in the mind of the leader of the Masters**. Entity suddenly realizes that the woman has been framed by a much larger entity, and that the guild has been used as a mere instrument of execution by puppeteers in the shadows.
